Alia Appointed Assistant to Editor; No ZW Correspondents

Message from Managing Editor and Webmaster Hazem Zureikat:

As the Zureikat Web enters its fourth successful month, I have made a decision not to appoint any correspondents. The current system -- in which I send an e-mail to all family members every now and then asking for their news -- seems to be working fine. It also encourages everyone to participate in the reporting of family news.

However, since I will need some help in managing this website, I have decided to appoint Alia as Assistant to the Managing Editor.

Thank you all for your contributions. Keep it up.

NOTE: I would also like to reiterate the fact that this is a 'blog-style' website. News here are reported in chronological order, not in any order of 'importance'. News that are the most recent are reported on the top part of the website; older news are further down.

Posted Monday, April 10, 2006 (News item #0060)

Reem Passes MRCP, Officially Becomes Internist

Reem has passed the final part of the MRCP examination and is now officially an internist.

MRCP stands for Membership of the Royal College of Physicians. The College is based in London and has branches in Edinburgh and Glasgow. Reem is currently preparing to attend Cornell University's medical school starting this coming September.

The Zureikat Web would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Reem on this great achievement, wishing her all the best.

What should be mentioned is that Ziad is an advisor for the Royal College of Physicians. Just last Thursday, Ziad attended a meeting in Amman in which the President of the College presented HM King Abdullah II with a fellowship.
