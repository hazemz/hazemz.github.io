Sunday, March 11, 2001

The Eid Al-Adha holiday is over

Zureikats Return from Beirut, Aqaba, and Sharm

  

Left: Abla N., her brother Dr. Yousef Nussier, and her sister Dr. Najla Bashour all together in Beirut for the first time in more than 25 years. \ Right: Marwan, Abla N., Hazem, Dr. Yousef Nussier, his family, Dr. Najla Bashour, her family, and a guest all having lunch at a restaurant in Al-Ashrafyyeh near Beirut.

The Eid Al-Adha holiday is now over.  Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem returned from Beirut, Harran and Reema returned from Aqaba, and Kareem and Ayman returned from Sharm El-Sheikh.  In their visit to Beirut, Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem were accompanied by Dr. Yousef Nusseir, Abla N.'s brother, and his family.  They spent most of their time there with Dr. Najla Bashour, Abla N.'s sister, and her family.  During their four-day visit, Marwan, Dr. Yousef, Dr. Najla, and their families spent a day at Bikfayyah (in the mountains of Lebanon).

Amal Returns from London

Amal returned home after a weeklong visit to London.  During her visit, Amal met with Talal who had an interview with St. George's Medical School and attended Grease, a musical in which Reem was the choreographer.

Zureikats Celebrate Yacoub's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Yacoub Z.'s birthday on Thursday, March 8, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Yacoub a very happy birthday and all the best.

Dana's 10th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 10th story for Jordan Television aired on the news on Friday, March 9, 2001.  This story was about two fast-track projects launched by the Ministry of Water & Irrigation to provide Amman with fresh water during the next summer.
