Alia Graduates from Royal Holloway with Honors

 

Right: A picture of an Irish band that started off the graduation ceremony (From Royal Holloway's website)

Alia graduated from Royal Holloway with honors, earning a BA degree in European Studies.  The graduation ceremony, which was held in London on Thursday, July 12, was attended by Harran, Reema, and Kareem.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Alia on her graduation and wish her all the best in the future.

Editor in Chief Leaves Sunday,

No Zureikat Weekly Next Week

Hazem will be leaving to Los Angeles, California on Sunday, July 15, at about 2:30 am.  During his two-week visit, the Editor in Chief will be joining an apprenticeship program at the Institute of Educational Advancement.  He is expected to return home on Sunday, July 29, 2001.

Due to the Editor in Chief's trip to LA, there will be no issue for The Zureikat Weekly next week.  The 115th issue will be released on Monday, July 30, 2001.

Amer Leaves to Ireland...Monday

After spending a few weeks in Amman, Amer will be leaving to Ireland, where he will continue his part time work and prepare for the USMLE, on Monday, July 16, 2001.
