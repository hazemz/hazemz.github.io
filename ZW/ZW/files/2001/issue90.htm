Sunday, January 21, 2001

Samer Breaks his Leg,

Undergoes Surgery

Samer, who is currently studying in the US, broke his leg on Monday, January 15, 2001, and underwent surgery, according to what Amal said in a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly just before releasing this issue.  Samer, who broke his leg while playing volleyball, will undergo another surgery after 8 weeks.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Samer a speedy recovery.

Zureikat Night on JTV!

JTV airs Dana's story on bi-ventricular pacing in Jordan

 

Dana's 2nd story in JTV, which aired on Tuesday, January 16, 2001, was about an operation done for the first time in Jordan, the bi-ventricular pacing process.  In her story, Dana interviewed the patient, Mustafa Muslih, Dr. Nazih Al-Kadiri, and Harran.  In his interview, Harran said, "This is a new modality of treatment...there is a significant number of patients with severe heart failure who can benefit from this procedure."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Dana on her 2nd story and wish her all the best in the future.

We forgot the doctor's birthday!!

Zureikats Celebrate Amer's 25th Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Dr. Amer's birthday on Friday, January 12, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Amer a very happy birthday and all the best in the future.  We would also like to apologize to Amer for not mentioning his completing "a quarter of a century" of his life, as he mentioned in a phone call with Reema, in the last issue.

Ziad Returns from Egypt after Short Trip

Ziad returned from Egypt after a short business trip he made last week.  Ziad had left Amman on Sunday, January 14, and returned on Wednesday, January 17, 2001.
