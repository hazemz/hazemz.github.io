Amal in London...Tuesday

Amal to meet Talal who will be interviewed by St. George's on the 1st

Amal will be leaving to London on Tuesday, February 27, to meet Talal who will be interviewed by St. George's Medical School on March 1.  Amal and Talal will also attend Reem's musical, Grease.  Amal is expected to return home on Tuesday, March 6, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Reem's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Reem's birthday on Sunday, February 25, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Reem a happy birthday and all the best in the future.

Dana's 7th and 8th Stories Air on JTV

Dana's 7th and 8th stories for Jordan Television aired on the news last week.  The 7th was about European investment in Jordan, and the 8th was about Queen Rania's visit to France.

Spotty Undergoes Successful Operation

Veterinarians at JUST found bones in his intestines

Spotty will live! Veterinarians at the Jordan University for Science and Technology (JUST) in Irbid were able to remove bones found in his intestines.  These bones, which were obstructing parts of his intestines, were making Spotty refuse food for more than 4 days.  In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Reema said, "The president of the university was involved.  The team was a wonderful team.  Spotty is now under my care!  He is on medication and fluids.  He'll start on baby food on Friday (Feb. 23), no bones at all.  It will take some time for him to recover."  In the phone call, Reema also said, "It was quite an experience.  I have been forced into this.  The year 2001 started with two new experiences, New York and Spotty."  Reema concluded the phone call by saying, "HABIB HABIBI HABIBI!"
