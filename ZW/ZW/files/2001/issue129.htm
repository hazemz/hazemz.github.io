Mona Attends Book Exhibition in Sharjah

Mona will be conducting two workshops there

Mona is currently in Sharjah, the United Arab Emirates attending a book exhibition.  She will be conducting two workshops there and returning to Sweden in a few days.  Details of this trip in the next issue.

Marwan and Dana Return from Cairo

 

Marwan returned home on Thursday, November 1, after making a three-day business trip to Cairo.  Dana also returned from a three-day private visit to Cairo on Tuesday, October 30, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Mona and Gero's Anniversary

Zureikats celebrated Mona and Gero's wedding anniversary on Sunday, November 4, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Mona and Gero a very happy anniversary and all the best.
