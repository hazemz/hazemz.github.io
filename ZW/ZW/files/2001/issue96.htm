Sunday, March 4, 2001

Goodbye Spotty

Spotty suffered an inner bleeding and died at about 10:30 pm on Monday, February 26, 2001.  Spotty, a Dalmatian that Harran bought last year, had been refusing food for the last few weeks.  He underwent an operation in Irbid last week, but the operation wasn't successful.

Accompanied by Dr. Yousef Nusseir and his family

Marwan, Abla, and Hazem to Visit Lebanon

   

Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem will be leaving to Lebanon on Friday, March 2, accompanied by Abla N.'s brother, Dr. Yousef Nusseir, and his family.  During the visit, Marwan, Dr. Nusseir, and their families will be spending some time with Dr. Najla Bashour, Abla N.'s sister, and her family.  They are all expected to return home on Tuesday, March 6, 2001.  Dana will not be accompanying Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem because she will be working during the holiday.

Kareem to Visit Sharm El-Sheikh

Kareem will be visiting the Egyptian Red Sea resort of Sharm El-Sheikh for a few days.  He will leave on Monday, March 5, and return on Friday, March 9, 2001.

Dana's 9th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 9th story for Jordan Television aired on the news on Thursday, March 1, 2001.  The story was about King Abdullah's visit to Aqaba after making it a free-trade zone.
