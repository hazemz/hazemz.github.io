93rd Issue

Sunday, February 11, 2001

Ziad in Sudan...Returns Wednesday

Ziad left to Sudan on Saturday, February 10, to attend a meeting there.  He is expected to return to Amman on Wednesday, February 14, 2001.

Zureikats Congratulate Bashar on BA Degree

Zureikats around the world congratulated Bashar on his BA degree.  Bashar completed all the requirements for his degree in Finance & Accounting late last month.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Reem said, "Send my congratulations to Bashar on his BA, and Tarek for his nomination.  They both make us very proud."

Zureikats Celebrate Omar's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Omar's birthday on Wednesday, February 7, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Omar a very happy birthday and all the best in the future.

Dana's 5th and 6th Stories Air on JTV

Dana's 5th story for Jordan Television aired on the news on Thursday, February 8, and her 6th aired on Friday, February 9, 2001.  Both stories discussed a forum, Convergence 2001, held in Amman between the 1st anniversaries of two events, the privatization of Jordan Telecom and the Dead Sea IT forum.  The stories included interviews with professionals from the IT sector in Jordan and others.

To receive a reminder before any of Dana's stories airs on JTV, send us an e-mail.
