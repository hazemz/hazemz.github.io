91st Issue

Sunday, January 28, 2001

Dian Undergoes Surgery in Syria

Dian underwent a hernia repair surgery in Syria last week.  She, accompanied by her mother, returned to Amman later.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Dian a speedy recovery.

Reema in New York to Attend a 5-Day Meeting

Reema will be spending her birthday in New York for the first time!

Reema left to New York on Saturday, January 27, to attend a meeting for the Preparatory Committee for the Special Session of the General Assembly on Children.  Reema, part of the Jordanian delegation, is representing the Families and Friends Society of Persons with Disabilities.  She is expected to return to Amman on Saturday, February 3, 2001, one day after the meeting ends.  This year, Reema will be celebrating her birthday (January 28) for the first time in New York.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Reema a very happy birthday and all the best in her trip to New York.

Dana's 3rd Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 3rd story for Jordan Television aired on the news on Friday, January 26, 2001.  The story discussed a 21-meter drop in the Dead Sea level and a shoreline retrieval on 2 km.  In her 3rd story, Dana interviewed Dr. Elias Salameh from the Department of Geology at Jordan University and Dr. Hazem El-Naser, Secretary General of the Ministry of Water & Irrigation.

Ayman Returns after 5-Day Vacation in Beirut

Ayman was accompanied by his friends from Amman National School

Ayman returned to Amman on Friday, January 26, after visiting Beirut for a few days.  Ayman left to Beirut with his friends from Amman National School on Monday, January 22, 2001.
