Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

103rd Issue

Sunday, April 22, 2001

Abu Harran Undergoes Successful Valve Replacement

Abu Harran underwent an open-heart surgery (valve replacement) on Saturday, April 21.  The operation was very successful.  Abu Harran is currently in the intensive care unit (ICU) and is expected to leave it on Monday, April 23, 2001.

Helene Graduates from SSE

Helene graduated from the Stockholm School of Economics on Friday, April 20, 2001.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Mona said, "Helene graduated from Stockholm School of Economics, which has the rank number 19 in the world.  It is a very famous institute.  The graduation took place in the City Hall, the place where the Noble Prize winners celebrate their nomination.  The celebration was very elegant...Friends of Helene from Bocconi came to Stockholm to join her on her great day.  After the ceremony, there was a banquet for all the graduates, dinner and dance till the morning hours."  In her e-mail, Mona also thanked the Zureikats for their congratulations.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Helene and wish her all the best in the future.

Mona Arrives in Amman...Monday

Mona is expected to arrive in Amman on Monday, April 23, 2001.  She will be staying in Jordan for 2 days before she leaves to Egypt and returns again to Amman.

Tarek and Reem Return to the UK

  

Tarek and Reem returned to the UK after spending Easter with the family.  Tarek, who spent almost one month in Amman, returned to Edinburgh on Tuesday, April 17, while Reem, who spent almost one week in Amman, returned to London on Friday, April 20, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Two Birthdays

  

Zureikats celebrated Yacoub S.'s birthday on Wednesday, April 18, and Abla N.'s birthday on Friday, April 20, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish both Yacoub and Abla happy birthdays and all the best in the future.  What should be mentioned is that Yacoub S. got a flute on his birthday from his parents.
