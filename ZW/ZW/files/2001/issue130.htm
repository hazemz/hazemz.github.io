Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

130th Issue

Sunday, November 11, 2001

Abla Leaves to Cincinnati

Abla N. left to Cincinnati, Ohio on Monday, November 5, to attend a convention on gifted children.  On her way to Ohio, Abla visited a school for gifted children in Chicago, Illinois.  She will be visiting another one in Washington late this week before returning home on Thursday, November 15, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Hala's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Hala's birthday on Friday, November 9, 2001.  On this occasion, Naief, Dalia, Deena, and Salem held a small surprise party for Hala.  In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Hala said, "I got a cake and a surprise party, but I didn't get any gold or silver!  It's never going to "yidhwi" as Umm Harran used to say."

Zureikats Attend Wael Zureikat's Engagement Party

Abu Harran, Harran, Amer, Marwan, Abla N., Dana, Hazem, Hassan, Dian, Amal, Ziad, Abla Z., Basil, Bashar, Hala, and Naief attended the engagement party of Wael Zureikat at the Amman Mariott Hotel on Sunday, November 4, 2001.  Wael's fiancé is Jezel Zumot.

ZW to move from Angelfire

The Editor in Chief decided to move The Zureikat Weekly from Angelfire to another hosting service soon.  The decision comes after encountering many faults with the Angelfire server throughout the past few months.  The move will bring everything back to normal (i.e. there will be background music again, and the links on the top will reappear).  The Editor in Chief also said that the CC.ST address we currently have may not be changed unless the service is permanently stopped by the provider, 2000c.net.

  • Mona, who is currently in Sharjah, UAE participating in a book exhibition, was interviewed on Thursday, November 8, 2001, by Sharjah TV.

  • Hala (in a phone call with the Editor in Chief): "They will start working on the new kitchen tomorrow (Sunday, November 11).  Hopefully, we will move back in ten days."
