Sunday, December 16, 2001

Christmas 2001 draws nearer

Zureikats Abroad Return Home...This Week

Christmas Eve dinner to be held at Harran's...lunch to be held at Marwan's

Sources confirmed to The Zureikat Weekly that there will be no changes in the Christmas celebrations this year.  The Christmas Eve dinner will be held at Harran's, and the Christmas Day lunch will be held at Marwan's.  On the other hand, Zureikats studying and working abroad will be returning home for Christmas this week.  Tarek will be returning from Edinburgh on Sunday, December 16.  Reem and Talal will be returning from London on Monday, December 17, and Samer and Ayman will be returning from the US on Thursday, December 20, 2001.

Harran Makes a Two-Day Visit to Cyprus

Harran left to Cyprus on Saturday, December 15, to attend a conference.  He is expected to return home on Sunday, December 16, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Salem's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Salem's birthday on Tuesday, December 11, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Salem a very happy birthday and all the best.
