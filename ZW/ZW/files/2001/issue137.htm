Zureikats Celebrate Christmas

The Zureikat family in Jordan celebrated Christmas Eve at Harran's house and Christmas Day at Marwan's.  During the Christmas Eve celebration, Zureikats sang Christmas Carols and were visited by Santa Claus, as usual.  Also during Christmas Eve, Marwan made a toast to Harran, and then several toasts were made to the late Umm Harran, Mona, The Zureikat Weekly, and Leanne Kawar, "the new addition to The Zureikat Weekly", as Harran put it.

The Zureikat Weekly Wishes You a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year

In its last issue in the year 2001, The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Zureikats all around the world a very merry Christmas and a happy and prosperous 2002.

Hassan and Family in Beirut

       

Hassan, Dian, Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z. left to Beirut on Christmas morning to attend a wedding there.  They are expected to return home on Sunday, December 30, 2001.

 

Post-Christmas Mansaf for the Family's "Shabab"

Marwan and Abla N. hosted the Zureikats aged between 15 and 28, Abu Harran, and others to a Mansaf on Friday, December 28, 2001.

As other Zureikat students prepare

Hazem Starts his Mid-Year Tawjihi Exams

As other Zureikat school students prepare to start their final exams within the next two weeks, Hazem started his final exams on Saturday, December 29, 2001.  Hazem will be sitting for the official Tawjihi exams next June and will be graduating in July.
