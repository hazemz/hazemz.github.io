Alia and Tarek Return to the UK

Now...they're all gone

  

After spending her Christmas vacation in Amman, Alia returned to London on Tuesday, January 9. Tarek, who returned from Sharm Al-Sheikh on Tuesday, January 9, also returned to Edinburgh, on Thursday, January 11, 2001. Now, all Zureikats who study abroad are back in their universities.

Tarek Holds Awards Banquet for Jordanian Students

Marwan, Abla, Abla, Basil, Dana, Hazem attended the banquet...Bashar receives an award

   

Right: Bashar holding a red rose which he gave to his mother 

 

Left: The hall \ Right: Bashar thanking Tarek for his efforts during the summer

Marwan, Abla N., Abla Z., Basil, Dana, and Hazem attended an Awards Banquet held by Tarek to recognize the Jordanian students, including Bashar, who had worked with him during the summer for the Southwestern Company in the US. As the student manager and organization leader, Tarek spoke at the beginning of the banquet about the experience he and his team gained during the summer. During the banquet, each student gave a red rose to his mother as a symbol of his love and appreciation, and Tarek later gave the students their awards. Bashar, who thanked Tarek for his efforts during the summer, received the Persistence Award. The banquet was attended by about 25 people. Tarek will be starting his forth summer with the Southwestern Company next June with even a larger organization.
