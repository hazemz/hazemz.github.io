Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

120th Issue

Sunday, September 2, 2001

2001-2002 Academic Year Begins

The 2001-2002 academic year will officially begin in all schools in Jordan on Sunday, September 2, 2001.  Hazem, who is currently in 12th grade, already started school two weeks ago.  Abla N., Director of the Jubilee School, and Abla Z., English Coordinator at Amman National School, also started school before the rest of the students.  Kareem is now in 11th grade; he studies at the Amman Baccalaureate School.  Sabine is in 8th grade, Nadine is in 6th grade, and Yacoub Z. is in 4th grade; the three study at the Modern Schools.  Dalia is in 7th grade, Yacoub S. is in 6th grade, Deena is in 5th grade, and Salem is in 2nd grade; they, along with Hazem, who is in 12th grade, study at Amman National School.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish all Zureikat students, teachers, coordinators, and principals a successful academic year.

Harran and Reema in Sweden

 

Harran and Reema left to Sweden on Friday, August 31.  Harran will be attending a conference there.  He and Reema will also be attending a party which will be held celebrating Gero's 65th birthday, which was on May 11, 2001.

At Last..."Dhawa!"*

Hala wins 510 JDs from the Cairo Amman Bank

A few days after her return from Germany, Hala won two prizes from the Cairo Amman Bank, one in the amount of 500 JDs and the other in the amount of 10 JDs.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Hala on the prizes and wish her all the best.

* Dhawa: from the dictionary of the late Taita Umm Harran.

 

Also, after her return, Hala sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly...

"Naief might be returning by the 10th"

Also after her return from Germany, Hala sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly.  In her e-mail, Hala said, "We started to get ready for school after returning from our lovely vacation in Germany.  Salem knows how to swim now.  He learned by himself.  Also, he and Deena can ride bikes (two wheels) with no problem.  Naief might be returning by the 10th.  He informed (us...) in an email and says hi to all."

Omar Gets his Driver's License

Omar got his driver's license on Wednesday, August 29, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Omar on his license and wish him all the best.
