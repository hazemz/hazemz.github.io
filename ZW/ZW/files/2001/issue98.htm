98th Issue

Sunday, March 18, 2001

Tarek, Amer, and Alia Return Home This Week

Tarek on Monday...Amer and Alia on Thursday

   

Tarek, Amer, and Alia will be returning home this week.  Tarek is expected to arrive in Amman on Monday, March 19, while Amer and Alia are expected to arrive in Amman together on Thursday, March 22, 2001.

Zureikats in Sweden have finally responded!

Mona: I will be Going to Tunis to Give Some Lectures

Helene returns from Chicago...Sylvia returns from Malta...Dag to visit Hanover

Zureikats in Sweden have finally responded to our e-mails and sent us some news.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Mona said, "Helene just came back from the States.  She was in Chicago through her job as Consultant for IT technology in ACCENTURE.  Sylvia was on a Doctors congress for one week in Malta.  Dag soon will be in Hanover visiting CEBIBT, the biggest technology fair in the world.  He might go to China with his company to start an office there."  In her e-mail, Mona also said, "I myself will be going to Tunis to give some lectures.  I will be there for one week where I will be receiving a medal from the Ministry of Culture."

Talal Returns to Italy after Visiting Greece

"We visited some castles and museums, and I enjoyed it a lot"

Talal returned to Italy after visiting Greece.  In a short message to The Zureikat Weekly, Talal said, "Well, I was supposed to go there on a project to discover the Menoic society in Crete, and we visited some castles and museums, and I enjoyed it a lot."

2 More Stories for Dana Air on JTV

Dana's 11th and 12th stories for Jordan Television aired on the news last week.  The 11th story was about a group of Jordanian children chatting over the internet with an official at UNICEF.  The 12th story was about an activity organized in Amman in solidarity with Palestine and Iraq in which 400 Jordanian children flew kites of different colors which were chosen from the flags of Arab countries.
