Ayman Graduates from the Amman Baccalaureate School

 

Ayman graduated from the Amman Baccalaureate School on Thursday, May 31, 2001.  The graduation ceremony, which was attended by a number of Zureikats, was held at the school under the patronage of Her Royal Highness Princess Sarvath Al-Hassan.  Later in the day, the family gathered at Amal's celebrating this occasion.

Marwan and Abla Attend Wedding in Beirut

 

Marwan and Abla N. left to Beirut on Thursday, May 31, to attend the wedding of Dara Msallam Al-Ayed, which was held in Harisa, Lebanon on Saturday, June 2.  They are both expected to return home on Sunday, June 3, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Three Occasions

Zureikats celebrated Hassan and Dian's wedding anniversary and Helene's birthday on Wednesday, May 30.  The family also celebrated Sabine's birthday on Thursday, May 31, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would to take this opportunity to wish Hassan and Dian a happy anniversary and both Helene and Sabine happy birthdays and all the best.

  • Yacoub S.'s end-of-the-year party was held in the Amman National School on Tuesday, May 29, 2001.
