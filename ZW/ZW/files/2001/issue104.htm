104th Issue

Sunday, April 29, 2001

Abu Harran Returns Home after a Very Successful Operation

Abu Harran returned home on Thursday, April 26, after undergoing a very successful open-heart surgery (valve replacement) at the Jordan Hospital in Amman on Saturday, April 21, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Abu Harran a speedy recovery and the very best in the future.

It's the 2nd Anniversary of the ZW!

Editor in Chief sends a questionnaire to the Zureikats

Zureikats celebrated the 2nd anniversary of The Zureikat Weekly on Monday, April 23, 2001.  On this occasion, Editor in Chief, Hazem Zureikat, sent a questionnaire to all Zureikats about The Zureikat Weekly.  The results will be released as soon as everyone fills the questionnaire.

Important Dates Related to The Zureikat Weekly

  • April 23, 1999: The 1st issue of The Zureikat Weekly is released.
  • May 28, 1999: The "Weekly Cartoon" section is added to The Zureikat Weekly with the 6th issue.  
  • June 18, 1999: The first issue with pictures (9th issue) is released.
  • June 25, 1999: Editor in Chief receives congratulations on the 10th issue.  The "Quickies" section is added to The Zureikat Weekly.
  • July 2 and July 9, 1999: The 11th and 12th issues are released from Beirut, Lebanon.
  • October 8, 1999: The 25th (Silver) issue is released.
  • October 22, 1999: Editor in Chief voices his objection "against the lack of cooperation from all the Zureikats who failed to provide The Zureikat Weekly with any news".
  • November 26, 1999: The 30th issue is released after a two-week break in which the Editor in Chief traveled to Lebanon in the first week and got sick in the second.
  • December 31, 1999: The 1999 Christmas Issue (35th issue) is released
  • January 7, 2000: The first issue of The Zureikat Weekly in the year 2000 is released (36th issue) with a new look and new services.
  • January 14, 2000: The Editor in Chief announces that The Zureikat Weekly will switch to Sundays instead of Fridays.
  • February 6, 2000: The 40th issue, the first to be released on a Sunday, is released.
  • April 16, 2000: The 50th (Golden) issue is released.
  • April 23, 2000: The 1st Anniversary Special (51st issue) is released.  Zureikats celebrate.
  • April 30, 2000: The 2000 Easter Issue (52nd issue) is released.
  • June 18, 2000: A special issue (59th issue) about the Zureikats' trip to Egypt is released with many pictures.
  • August 6, 2000: The "Pic of the Week" and the "Zureikat Chat" sections are added to The Zureikat Weekly.  The "Zureikat Chat" section was later deleted.
  • October 8, 2000: The 75th (Platinum) issue is released.
  • December 31, 2000: The 2000 Christmas Issue (87th issue) is released.
  • January 7, 2001: The first issue of The Zureikat Weekly in the year 2001 is released (88th issue) with a new look and the long-awaited "Zureikat Calendar".
  • April 1, 2001: The 100th issue of The Zureikat Weekly is released.  Zureikats celebrate.
  • April 15, 2001: The 2001 Easter Issue (102nd issue) is released.
  • April 27, 2001: The Editor in Chief sends a questionnaire to the Zureikats.
  • April 29, 2001: The 2nd Anniversary Special (104th issue) is released.  You're reading it right now!

Mona Arrives in Amman

Mona leaves to Sharm...returns back to Amman on Sunday

 

 

Abla Z. blowing the candles and cutting her cake

Mona arrived in Amman on Monday, April 23.  The family gathered at Harran's on the same day celebrating Mona's arrival and Abla Z.'s birthday, which also coincided on that day.  Mona later left to the Egyptian Red Sea resort of Sharm El-Sheikh on Wednesday, April 25, and is expected to return back to Amman on Sunday, April 29, 2001.

Talal Makes a Brief Visit to Amman

Talal arrived in Amman on a weeklong visit on Sunday, April 22.  He is expected to return to Italy on Sunday, April 29, 2001.

Hala Heads a Conference on Archives

Hala was the president of the Preservation and Restoration of Mediterranean Archives Conference, which was held at the Amra Forum Hotel in Amman this year between Thursday, April 26, and Saturday, April 28, 2001.  At the beginning of the conference, Hala delivered a speech and welcomed the guests who came from 15 countries around the world.

Zureikats Celebrate 2 Birthdays

 

Zureikats celebrated Abla Z.'s birthday on Monday, April 23, and Mona's birthday on Wednesday, April 25, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Abla and Mona happy birthdays and all the best in the future.

  • Abla N. and Abla Z. attended the 10th conference for the Private Schools Council (PSC) in Amman on Thursday and Friday, April 26 and 27, 2001.
