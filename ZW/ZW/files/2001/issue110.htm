110th Issue

Sunday, June 17, 2001

Alia and Tarek Complete Their College Educations

Alia earns BA with honors in European Studies

Tarek earns BA in Business & Accounting

 

Alia and Tarek completed all the requirements for their BA degrees.  Alia earned a BA degree with honors in European Studies from Royal Holloway in London, and Tarek earned a BA degree in Business & Accounting from the University of Edinburgh in Scotland.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate both Alia and Tarek and wish them all the best in the future.

Samer Returns Home after Undergoing Minor Operation

Samer returned home after undergoing a minor operation at the Amman Surgical Hospital last week.  Just days after his return home, Samer had Mansaf for lunch at Marwan's house on Friday, June 15, 2001.  He has almost fully recovered.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Samer a speedy recovery.

Marwan to Visit Toronto...Tuesday

Marwan is expected to leave to Toronto, Canada on a weeklong business trip on Tuesday, June 19, 2001.

Tarek Leaves to the US to Start Summer Work

Tarek will be starting his 4th year at the Southwestern Company with a huge organization

After completing his studies at the University of Edinburgh, Tarek left to the US on Saturday, June 16, 2001, to start his fourth year working for the Southwestern Company.  This year, Tarek has a huge organization of about 60 students.  Members of this organization, which is called the "Al-Nashama" organization, will first go to Nashville, Tennessee, where they will be receiving training.  Then, they will go to California to start their summer work there.

Abla and Hazem to Attend Lama Bashour's Wedding

Both, along with Dr. Yousef Nusseir and his family, will leave Amman on Wednesday

Marwan, Abla, and Hazem's last trip to Lebanon - From The Zureikat Weekly's archive - (Lama is the second from the left)

Abla N. and Hazem, along with Dr. Yousef Nusseir, Abla N.'s brother, and his family, will be leaving to Lebanon on Wednesday, June 20, to attend the wedding party of Lama Bashour, Abla N.'s niece.  The party will be held on Friday, June 22, in Bikfayya, an area in the mountains of Lebanon.  Abla N., Hazem, Dr. Yousef, and his family are all expected to return back home on Saturday or Sunday, June 23 or 24, 2001.

NOTE: This trip won't affect The Zureikat Weekly.  The 111th issue will be released on its due date, that is Sunday, June 24, 2001.
