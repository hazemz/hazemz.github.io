Sunday, September 16, 2001

Tarek Returns Home Soon for Safety Reasons

A few days after the recent attacks on New York and Washington, Tarek and the Jordanian students in his organization were asked by the company they work for (the Southwestern Company) to leave the US for their own safety.  Tarek is expected to return home soon.

Naief Returns Home on Wednesday 

Salem told the Editor in Chief that Naief will be returning home on Wednesday, September 19, 2001.  Naief is currently on a Dad Scholarship at Gottingen University in Germany.
