Reem: I Finished my Degree and got a Bachelor's of Science

Reem returns home...Sunday

Reem, who is studying at St. George's in England, now has a BSc degree.  In an e-mail she sent to The Zureikat Weekly, Reem said, "I finished my degree and got a Bachelor's of Science".  In her e-mail, Reem also said that she will be returning home on Sunday, July 1, 2001.  "I'm coming back tomorrow", she said.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Reem on her degree and wish her all the best in the future.

Editor in Chief to Visit Los Angeles Between July 15 and 28

After being accepted and given a scholarship to an apprenticeship program in the US, the Editor in Chief will be leaving to Los Angeles, California on Sunday, July 15.  He is expected to arrive back home on Sunday, July 29, 2001.

 

Please note the following:

  • The 113th and 114th issues of The Zureikat Weekly will be released on their due dates (July 8 and 15, 2001 respectively).

  • No issue will be released on Sunday, July 22, 2001.

  • The 115th issue of The Zureikat Weekly (the Sunday, July 29, 2001, issue) will be released on Monday, July 30, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Harran's Birthday

Zureikats will celebrate Harran's birthday on Sunday, July 1, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Harran a very happy birthday and all the best.

Thanking all who answered the questionnaire

Editor in Chief Announces Next Friday as Final Date for Submitting Answers to Questionnaire

Hazem: Any changes in The Zureikat Weekly will take effect in August

The Editor in Chief announced Friday, July 6, as the final date for submitting the answers to the questionnaire sent on April 27 celebrating The Zureikat Weekly's 2nd anniversary.  In a statement he made late Saturday, Hazem thanked all who answered the questionnaire.  He also said that any changes in The Zureikat Weekly will take effect in August.  On Friday, July 6, 2001, 70 days will have passed since the Editor in Chief sent the questionnaire to the Zureikats.  Up till now, he has received only 9 responses.
