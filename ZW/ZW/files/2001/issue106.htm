Sunday, May 20, 2001

Ziad in Atlanta to attend a conference...goes to Italy on Thursday

Talal Graduates from the UWC of the Adriatic Next Saturday

Amal, Samer, and Ayman leave to Italy on Sunday

Talal will be graduating from the United World Colleges (UWC) of the Adriatic in Italy on Saturday, May 26, after completing a two-year IB program there.  Amal, Ziad, Samer, and Ayman are expected to attend the graduation ceremony.  Ziad, who left to Atlanta, Georgia on Thursday, May 17, to attend a conference there, will arrive in Italy on Thursday, May 24.  Amal, Samer, and Ayman, on the other hand, will be leaving to Italy on Sunday, May 20.  The Sharaihas are expected to return back to Amman on Sunday, May 27, 2001, a day after Talal's graduation.

Zureikat Students Get the First Glimpse of the Summer Holiday

Ayman finished his exams on May 18...Kareem started his on May 14

  

Completing twelve years of education, Ayman finished his IB exams on Friday, May 18, and started his summer holiday.  He will be attending Tufts University next fall.  Kareem started his O-Level exams on Monday, May 14, and will finish in the beginning of June.  Other Zureikats in Amman National School and the Modern Schools still haven't started their finals yet.

Dana in Kuwait after Visiting Beirut

Dana returned home after a short business trip to Beirut.  Later, she left to Kuwait for a similar business trip and is expected to return back home in a few days.

  • The Student Council of Amman National School, with Hazem as its President, and the school's Parents and Teachers Association (PTA) held the 8th Dahnoun Festival in the school on Friday, May 18, 2001.
