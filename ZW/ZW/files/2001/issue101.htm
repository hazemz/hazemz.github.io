Breaking News:

Dana Officially Appointed as Press Officer for Queen Rania

Dana has been officially appointed by the Royal Hashemite Court as Press Officer for Her Majesty Queen Rania starting Sunday, April 8, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Dana on her new job and wish her all the best in the future.

After a series of interviews

Bashar Appointed as Management Bank Associate at Citibank

Bashar has been appointed as a Management Bank Associate in the Corporate Bank Department at Citibank.  In an exclusive phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Bashar said, "It all started two months ago when they called me at home.  They had taken my name from the university because I was one of the top of my class, and they called me for a test.  150 applicants came for the test.  After seeing the results of the test, we were short listed to 50 people, and, after that, we went to a series interviews (6 in Bashar's case), the last which was with Suheir Al-Ali, President of the bank, on Thursday, April 5.  And after that last interview in 2 hours, they called me and offered me a position."  Bashar, who completed all the requirements for his BA degree last January, signed his contract with Citibank on Thursday, April 5, and will start his new job on Sunday, April 8, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Bashar on his job and wish him all the best in the future.

Hassan to Host Easter Lunch in Umm Al-Amad

Sources confirmed to The Zureikat Weekly that Hassan will be hosting next week's Easter lunch on Sunday, April 15, 2001, in Marwan's farm in Umm Al-Amad.

Abla and Basil Make a Two-Day Visit to Aqaba

They attended a dinner celebrating the completion of a project which Basil participated in

 

Abla Z. and Basil returned to Amman on Friday, April 6, after making a two-day visit to Aqaba, Jordan.  During their visit, Abla and Basil attended a dinner celebrating the completion of a postpartum project which Basil participated in.

Amal and Ziad Visit Egypt

 

Amal and Ziad returned home after visiting Cairo, Egypt.  Both had left Amman on Monday, April 2, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Harran and Reema's Anniversary

 

Zureikats celebrated Harran and Reema's wedding anniversary on Thursday, April 5, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Harran and Reema a happy anniversary and all the best.
