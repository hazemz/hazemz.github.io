Amer, Alia, Tarek Return Home

The three, along with other Zureikats, had lunch at Hala's on Friday

 

Hala hosted Amer, Alia, and Tarek, along with other Zureikats, on a Friday lunch on March 23.  Amer, Alia, and Tarek had all returned home last week, Tarek on Tuesday, March 20, and Amer and Alia on Thursday, March 22, 2001.

Kareem Grabs 1st Place in ABS Talent Show

Kareem, the drummer, and his band won 1st place in the Amman Baccalaureate School Talent Show which was held in the school on Thursday, March 22, 2001, and was attended by Harran, his family, and Hazem.  The band won 1st place for performing a piece which included a number of songs for Elvis Presley.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Kareem on winning the 1st prize, wishing him all the best in the future.

Hassan and Ziad Return from Syria

 

Hassan and Ziad returned home after making two separate visits to Syria in which Hassan visited Damascus, and Ziad visited Aleppo.

Zureikats Celebrate Kareem's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Kareem's birthday on Sunday, March 25, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Kareem a very happy birthday and all the best.

Dana's 13th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 13th story for Jordan Television aired on the news last week.  This story was about Queen Rania launching the new "Friends of Jordanian Children" group.
