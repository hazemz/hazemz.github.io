124th Issue

Sunday, September 30, 2001

Mona Arrives in Amman

Family gathers at Marwan's

Mona arrived in Amman on Thursday, September 27, to attend a conference.  Celebrating her arrival, the family gathered at Marwan's for lunch on Friday, September 28, 2001.

Talal Starts 1st Year at St. George's Hospital Medical School

Amal and Talal left Amman on Thursday

Talal has started his first year at St. George's Hospital Medical School in London.  He and Amal left to London on Thursday, September 27.  Amal is expected to return home on Monday, October 1, 2001.

Omar Starts 2nd Year at Sumayya

Omar started his second year at the Princess Sumayya University College for Technology on Sunday, September 30.  What should be mentioned is that Bana, who studies at the University of Jordan, will start on Sunday, October 7, 2001.
