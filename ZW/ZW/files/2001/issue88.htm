88th Issue

Sunday, January 7, 2001

Zureikats Welcome a New Year, a New Millennium, and a New ZW

Happy new year. As you can see, our internet weekly now has new logos, a new layout, new pictures for Zureikat family members, and a new section, the Zureikat Calendar. As Editor in Chief, I welcome you all to the all-new Zureikat Weekly 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Abu Harran's 90th Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Abu Harran's 90th birthday on Monday, January 1, 2001. On this occasion, The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Abu Harran a happy birthday and a long and prosperous life.

Sharaihas All Leave in Less than 12 Hours...

Amer Back in Dublin

Alia to leave Tuesday accompanied by Edella

Reema: Edella has been a fantastic member of the family and a loving and dedicated one

After spending their Christmas holiday in Amman, Amer returned to Dublin on Tuesday, January 2, Talal returned to Italy on Saturday, January 6, Samer returned to the US also on Saturday, January 6, and Reem returned to London also later on Saturday, January 6. The next to leave Amman is Alia on Tuesday, January 9, 2001, accompanied by Edella, who will be leaving Amman for good. Concerning Edella's departure, Reema told The Zureikat Weekly in a phone call last week that Edella will be leaving after 20 years of housekeeping at their house. She said that Edella has been a "fantastic member of the family and a loving and dedicated one". "I dread the life without Edella housekeeping," Reema added, "I have been warning that they (the family) better eat well before her departure, because I believe that the whole family will turn to vegetarians, and they will live on fast food eventually." Harran made a toast to Edella in front of the whole family on Christmas Eve two weeks ago.

Tarek in Sharm..Returns Tuesday..Leaves Thursday

Mukherjee to return with Tarek to attend Awards Banquet on January 9

In a vacation with some of the managers in the Southwestern Company, Tarek left to the Egyptian resort of Sharm Al-Sheikh on Friday, January 5. Tarek will be returning to Amman on Tuesday, January 9. On the same day, Tarek will hold an awards banquet at the Holiday Inn Hotel in Amman to recognize the Jordanian students who worked with him in the summer program, including Bashar. Tarek's manager, Robin Mukherjee, will be arriving to Amman with Tarek on January 9 to attend the banquet. Tarek is expected to return to Edinburgh on Thursday, January 11, 2001.

Zureikat School Students Prepare for Finals

Ayman, Hazem, Kareem, Sabine, Nadine, Dalia, and Yacoub S. are currently preparing for their final exams for the first term. They will all start their exams next week.
