145th Issue - February 24, 2002

Basil Undergoes Hernia Operation...Returns Home

Basil underwent a hernia operation at the Jordan Hospital on Wednesday, February 20, 2002.  He returned home on Thursday after spending one night at the hospital.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Basil a speedy recovery and all the best.

Zureikats travel in the Eid holiday:

Harran and Reema Return from Lebanon,

Amal and Ziad Still in the Far East,

Hazem Returns from Egypt

Sharm El-Sheikh, Egypt

After attending a play in Lebanon, Harran and Reema returned home on Saturday, February 23.  They had left Amman on Thursday, February 21.  On the other hand, Amal and Ziad left to the far east on Friday, February 22, on a two-week trip which includes a visit to Bangkok, Tokyo, and Kuala Lumpur.  Another Zureikat who traveled during the Eid Al-Adha holiday, which ended on Monday, February 25, was Hazem.  Hazem returned from Egypt on Monday, February 25, 2002, after a visit to the Egyptian Red Sea resort of Sharm El-Sheikh.  Hazem accompanied Dr. Yousef Nusseir and his family on this four-day trip.

Abla Recovers from Bacterial Infection

Abla N. recovered from a bacterial infection after staying home during the Eid Al-Adha holiday.  Marwan and Abla were supposed to travel to Sharm El-Sheikh during the holiday but canceled their trip due to Abla's sickness.
