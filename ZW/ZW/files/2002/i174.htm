Marwan: The Zureikat Weekly has Become Part of a "Proud Family Culture"

Marwan sent the following e-mail to the Editor in Chief last week:

Dear Editor,

Thanks for the latest issue.  It felt like a medical report on health status of many of the Zureikats.  I am glad all are well.

I never felt about the Weekly as much as I did when the last three issues came from Minnesota.  No longer is The Zureikat Weekly the result of a dedicated work of a high school family member, it has now become part of a proud family culture.  This would not have happened without the perseverance of the Editor in Chief who continued to publish the magazine one issue after the other not deterred by occasional shortage of news or lack of support from members and "correspondents".

Keep up the good work.  What a difference you have made Mr. Editor.

Marwan Zureikat
