Zureikats Gather at Hala's Celebrating Her Birthday

Zureikats had lunch at Hala's on Friday, a day before her birthday

 

 

 

Zureikats celebrated Hala's birthday on Saturday, November 9.  On this occasion, the family gathered at Hala's house for lunch on Friday, November 8, 2002.  Lunch included Mansaf and Freekeh with chicken.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Hala a very happy birthday and all the best.

 

Zureikats Celebrate Two Other Occasions

 

Besides Hala's birthday, Zureikats celebrate two other occasions last week, Mona and Gero's wedding anniversary on Monday, November 4, and Leanne's birthday on Friday, November 8, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Mona and Gero a very happy anniversary and Leanne a very happy birthday and all the best.
