144th Issue - February 17, 2002

Reem Graduates with Honors from St. George's Medical School

Amal and Talal attended the ceremony

Reem graduated with honors from the St. George's Hospital Medical School with a BSc degree in Neuroanatomy.  The graduation ceremony, which was held in London on Thursday, February 14, 2002, was attended by Amal and Talal.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Reem on her graduation and wish her all the best.  What should be mentioned is that Reem completed all the requirements for her BSc degree late last June.

 

Amal Returns from London after Attending Graduation

Amal, who left to London on Wednesday, February 13, to attend Reem's graduation, returned to Amman on Saturday, February 16, 2002.

Mona Returns to Sweden

After a nine-day working visit to Amman, Mona returned to Sweden on Friday, February 15, 2002.

Bana's Grades: 5 A's and a GPA of 3.86

In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Abla Z. announced Bana's grades: 5 A's and a GPA of 3.86.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Bana on her excellent results and wish her all the best.  Bana completed all the requirements for her BA degree two weeks ago.

Dana's 20th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 20th story aired on JTV on Friday, February 15, 2002.  This week's story was about the youth solicitors who are always seen in the streets of Amman and other major cities in Jordan.

Zureikats Celebrate 2 Birthdays

 

Zureikats celebrated two birthdays last week, Basil's birthday on Tuesday, February 12, and Alia's birthday on Thursday, February 14, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Basil and Alia very happy birthdays and all the best.
