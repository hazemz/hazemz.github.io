Family, Friends Attend Pre-Engagement Dinner at Harran's

From The Zureikat Weekly's archive

Harran and Reema hosted the Zureikat and Kawar families and other friends on a dinner party on Wednesday, June 19, 2002.  The dinner party comes ahead of Amer and Leanne's engagement party to be held next month.

Harran and Reema Leave to Prague

 

Harran and Reema left to Prague, the Czech Republic on Friday, June 21, 2002.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Reema said that she and Harran will be returning to Amman "in one week".

Zureikats Celebrate Editor in Chief's Birthday
