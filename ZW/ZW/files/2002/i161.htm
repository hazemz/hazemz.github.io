From Al-Ra'i Newspaper (Sunday, June 16, 2002)

As other Zureikat students start their summer holiday

Hazem Starts Tawjihi Finals

Hazem sat for the Arabic exam on Sunday, June 16

Hazem started his Tawjihi finals on Sunday, June 16.  He sat for his first exam, the Arabic exam, on Sunday, and he will be doing his second exam, the Social Studies exam, on Tuesday, June 18.  Hazem's last exam will be on July 6, after which he will graduate from ANS on July 11.  All this comes as Zureikat school students, except Kareem, have already started their 2002 summer holiday.  Kareem will be starting his holiday on Tuesday, June 18, 2002.

Hassan Returns from Beirut

Hassan returned from a business trip to Beirut on Friday, June 14, 2002.

Dana Returns from Cairo after 2nd Visit This Month

Dana returned home on Sunday, June 16, after making the second visit to Egypt in less than two weeks.  During her five-day visit, Dana attended a wedding in Cairo.  Dana had left Amman on Wednesday, June 12, 2002.

As a member of the Jordanian Higher Council for Health

Ziad Meets with the Prime Minister

From the front page of Al-Ra'i Newspaper (Friday, June 14, 2002)  Ziad is the second from the left

As a member of the Higher Council for Health in Jordan, Ziad, along with the other council members, met with the Jordanian Prime Minister Ali Abu-Ragheb on Thursday, June 13, 2002.  During the meeting, which was held at the Prime Ministry in Amman, the council discussed many issues related to health in the Kingdom.

A New Chandra Joins the Family

The second Chandra in the family arrived at the Shomalis' residence on Saturday, June 15, 2002.
