Bana Participates in "Voices Sing for Palestine" Choir

Bana participated in a charity choral concert entitled "In the Name of Music, Voices Sing for Palestine".  The choir, which was conducted by Shereen Abu Khader, included 90 female and 20 male vocalists.  The concert was held at the St. John Baptist Church - De La Salle College in Amman on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday, April 16, 17, and 18, 2002.

It Happened on the Same Day!

Abu Harran announces that his "Jaha" was on the same day as Amer's...April 11

 

Last week, Abu Harran made a surprising announcement about a surprising coincidence.  He said that he had asked for the hand of the late Umm Harran on April 11, the same day as Amer asked for the hand of Leanne Kawar.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Reema said, "This is a sign of blessing and a good omen."

Harran and Reema Leave to Cairo

 

Harran and Reema left to Cairo on Saturday, April 20, and are expected to return home on Tuesday, April 23, 2002.  During the three-day visit, Harran will be attending a conference.

Tarek Returns to Edinburgh from Bristol

Tarek, who is currently working with the Southwestern Company in the UK, returned to Edinburgh last week after spending about three weeks in Bristol.

Dana Conducts Two Exclusive Interviews

Dana conducted two exclusive interviews with Paul Larudee, a member of an international solidarity movement, hours after arriving from Palestine and Thomas McDermott, UNICEF's Regional Director.  The interviews were aired on JTV on Sunday and Friday, April 14 and 19, 2002, respectively.

Amal and Talal Return from Aqaba after 2-Day Visit

 

Two days after Amal's return from Turkey, she and Talal left to Aqaba on Wednesday, April 17, on a two-day vacation.  They returned home on Friday, April 19, 2002.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief before she left to Aqaba, Amal said, "For the first time, Amal is not traveling on first class.  She is traveling on an air-conditioned "Trust" bus!"

Zureikats Celebrate 2 Birthdays

 

Zureikats celebrated Yacoub S.'s birthday on Thursday, April 18, and Abla N.'s birthday on Saturday, April 20, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Yacoub and Abla very happy birthdays and all the best.

  • Talal got his driver's license on Saturday, April 13, 2002.  Congratulations Talal!

  • Maan Bashour, Abla N.'s sister's husband from Lebanon, delivered a lecture in Amman on Wednesday, April 17, 2002.
