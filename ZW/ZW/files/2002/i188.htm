Zureikats Celebrate Christmas

 

Right: Zureikat children unwrapping their Christmas presents on Christmas Day

 

Zureikats around the world celebrated Christmas 2002.  On this occasion, family members in Jordan gathered at Harran's house on Christmas Eve and at Marwan's house on Christmas Day.  The Christmas Eve gathering included, as usual, Christmas carols followed by Santa's visit.  This year's Christmas toasts were made by Marwan and Amer.  In his toast, Marwan sent Dana's regards to the family.  He said that since Dana is now working in the US, we won't be seeing her as much as we would like to.  Amer, on the other hand, made a toast to his father.  He said that all of us in the family want to have some of Harran's qualities.  Amer also thanked the Editor in Chief for what he called an 'exceptional' year for The Zureikat Weekly.  On Christmas Day, the family gathered at Marwan's house for lunch.  The lunch was followed by dancing on Christmas carols.  Several family members attended half of their Christmas Day celebrations at Marwan's and the other half at their in-laws'.  What should be mentioned is that Amal, who is still recovering from her back injury, did not leave her house on Christmas Day.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Amal a very speedy recovery and all the best.

Season's Greetings from the ZW

The Editor in Chief, Correspondents, and Photographers of The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Zureikats all around the world a very Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.  May the new year be a prosperous and peaceful year for all.

Hala Promoted to Grade 1

Hala will now receive the privileges of a senior government official

Hala, who is currently the Director of International and Public Relations at Jordan Television, has been promoted to Grade 1.  Hala will now receive the privileges of a senior government official, such as first class medical insurance.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Hala on her promotion and wish her all the best.

 

Marwan and Abla Host the Young Zureikats on a Mansaf

 

Marwan and Abla hosted the Zureikat young generation on a Mansaf on Saturday, December 28, 2002.  What should be mentioned is that a similar 'post-Christmas Mansaf' was held at Marwan's on December 28, 2001, exactly one year before this year's Mansaf.

Mona and Gero Leave to Dubai

Mona and Gero left to Dubai on Saturday, December 28, 2002.  They will be spending the New Year there and returning back to Sweden afterwards.

Dana Makes her First Appearance on Radio Sawa

Dana, who is currently working with the Voice of America in Washington DC, edited and read the news headlines on Radio Sawa several times last week.  Radio Sawa, based in DC, broadcasts on FM and medium waves in several countries in the Middle East, as well as Arabsat, Nilesat, and Eutelsat.
