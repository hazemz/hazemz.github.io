156th Issue - May 12, 2002

Samer and Ayman Return Home

 

Samer, who is studying in Tulane University in New Orleans, and Ayman, who is studying in Tufts University in Boston, returned home for the summer on Sunday, May 12, 2002.

Family Attends Samer Zureikat's Wedding in Rabba, Karak

Members of the extended Zureikat family attended the wedding of Samer Ghaleb Zureikat and Nicole Schnkel which was held in Rabba, Karak on Thursday, May 9, 2002.  Among those who attended were Harran, Reema, Marwan, Abla N., Hazem, Amal, and Ziad.

Naief Leaves to Istanbul for Conference

Naief left to Istanbul, Turkey on Wednesday, May 8, to attend a conference on biochemistry there.  He is expected to return home on Wednesday, May 15, 2002.

Dana's 34th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 34th story aired on JTV on Friday, May 10, 2002.  This week's story was about the global conference on children held at the United Nations last week.

Zureikats Celebrate Gero's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Gero's birthday on Saturday, May 11, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Gero a very happy birthday and all the best.
