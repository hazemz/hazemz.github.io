149th Issue - March 24, 2002

Tarek Returns to the UK

After making a brief visit to Amman, Tarek returned to the UK on Wednesday, March 20, 2002.

Amer in Dublin after Sitting for Final Part of USMLE in Pennsylvania

Amer returned to Dublin on Sunday, March 10, 2002, after sitting for the final part of the USMLE in Pennsylvania.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Reema said, "Amer left to Pennsylvania on the 6th and had the test on the 8th.  He returned to Dublin on the 10th after some interviews."

Talal in Italy after Finishing Exams

Ayman in Canada for Spring Break

 

Talal, who is currently studying at St. George's in London, left to Italy on Friday, March 22, for a UWC reunion.  In an e-mail he sent to The Zureikat Weekly before leaving to Italy, Talal said that he will be returning home in April.  "I am going to Italy...for a small UWC reunion," he said, "then probably going back to Jordan in April."  In his e-mail, Talal also said, "Life here is the same, yet I spent the last four weeks simply studying for my exams.  Reem is fine, enjoying her time, as usual, and still helping me out."  On the other hand, Ayman, who is studying at Tufts University in Boston, is now in Montreal, Canada.  In an e-mail he sent to the The Zureikat Weekly, Ayman said, "I went to Toronto and Montreal for spring break.  I'm in Montreal now.  I left Boston on the 16th and I'm returning on the 24th.  I'm staying at my friends house."

Hassan Returns from 4-Day Trip to Syria

Hassan returned home last week after making a four-day business trip to Syria.

Nadine Participates in "Jerusalem Day" at her School

Nadine participated in the "Jerusalem Day" activity held in her school on Saturday, March 23, 2002.  During the activity, Nadine spoke to a number of parents about Palestine and its history.  While speaking to the parents, Nadine used a 1.5-meter high map of Palestine that she and her friend drew.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Nadine said that she and her friend drew three such maps which were all displayed at Saturday's activity.

Dana's 25th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 25th story aired on JTV on Friday, March 22, 2002.  This week's story was about the UN Conference on Poverty held in Monterrey, Mexico.
