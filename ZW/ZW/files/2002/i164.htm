At last, the day has come!

Amer and Leanne are Engaged!

Family and friends gather to celebrate the first engagement among the Zureikat grandchildren

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

"Dr. Amer, do you take Leanne to be your fiancée?...Leanne, do you take Dr. Amer to be your fiancé?"

With these words, uttered by the priest, Adeeb Ammari, Amer and Leanne finally became engaged.  The engagement took place on Thursday, July 4, 2002, at George Kawar, Leanne's father's, residence in Abdoun.  The ceremony was then followed by a party attended by many family members and friends.  The engagement, which is the first among Zureikat grandchildren, comes almost three months after Amer's 'Jaha' which was held last April.  What's also worth mentioning is that Amer celebrated his dependence day on the American independence day...what a coincidence!  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Amer, Leanne, and the Zureikat and Kawar families on this joyous occasion and to wish Amer and Leanne all the best in the years to come.

The Editor in Chief would like to welcome Ms. Leanne Kawar who has now become an official member of The Zureikat Weekly.

Zureikats Celebrate Harran's 60th Birthday

 

 

 

 

 

 

Zureikats celebrated Harran's 60th birthday on Monday, July 1, 2002.  Family and friends gathered at Harran's on Monday night in a dinner party held on this occasion.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Harran a very happy birthday and all the best.

Reema injured her nose during Harran's 60th birthday party.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Reema a speedy recovery.
