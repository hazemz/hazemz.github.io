150th Issue - March 31, 2002

Hazem Offered a "Kofi Annan" Scholarship from Macalester

Hazem, who is now in 12th grade, was offered a "Kofi Annan International Scholarship" from Macalester College in St. Paul, Minnesota.  In an e-mail sent to Hazem last week, Jamieson L. Crowder, Director of International Admissions at Macalester, said, "I wish to congratulate you on your acceptance to Macalester.  You have been chosen from the most academically competitive and personally dynamic applicant pool in the
College's history."  In her e-mail, Crowder also said, "We are pleased to offer you a Kofi Annan International Scholarship...and a work/study award.  These awards are renewable each year that you attend Macalester, provided that you make satisfactory academic progress and comply with the regulations of the College."

Visit Macalester's website at http://www.macalester.edu (Opens in a new window)

Bashar Promoted 2 Grades

Bashar, who is currently working at Citibank in Amman, was promoted from level 11 to level 13.  This promotion, which was granted to Bashar because of his "amazing" performance at work, as his manager put it, also includes a raise.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Bashar on his promotion and wish him all the best in the future.

Kareem Performs in "West Side Story"

Kareem performed in the musical "West Side Story", which was organized by the Amman Baccalaureate School last week.  The performance was held at the school's theater on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday, March 25, 26, 27, and 28, 2002.  It was attended by Harran, Reema, Alia, Bana, Hazem, and other relatives.  After Monday's performance, on Kareem's birthday, Kareem's family and friends surprised him with a birthday cake, and they all celebrated his birthday at the school's cafeteria.  The Editor in Chief would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Kareem on his excellent performance in the musical and wish him all the best in the years to come.  What should be mentioned is that, almost three weeks ago, Kareem had won the first and second prizes at his school's talent show for his "Stomp" performance and for another performance in which he played the drums as part of a band.

Zureikats in Sweden Celebrate Easter

Zureikats in Sweden will be celebrating Easter on Sunday, March 31.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish the family in Sweden a very happy Easter and all the best.  What should be mentioned is that Zureikats in Jordan will be celebrating Easter on Sunday, May 5, 2002.
