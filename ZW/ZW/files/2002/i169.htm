169th Issue - August 11, 2002

Amer in Amman on a 2-Day Visit

Amer arrived in Amman on Saturday, August 10, for an interview with the US Ambassador in Jordan regarding his visa.  Amer is expected to return to Dublin on Monday, August 12, 2002.

Marwan and Abla Spend a Week in Marmaris

 

Marwan and Abla N. left to Marmaris, Turkey on Monday, August 5, on a weeklong vacation.  They are expected to return home on Monday, August 12, 2002.

Abla and Basil Leave to Beirut...Sunday

 

As part of the 25th wedding anniversary gift they got from their children, Abla Z. and Basil will be leaving to Beirut on Sunday, August 11.  They are expected to return home on Wednesday, August 14, 2002.

Hassan Returns from Cairo

"Nothing comes on time in Egypt, except one thing, when a woman gets pregnant, in 9 months she'll deliver!"

Hassan returned from Cairo on Saturday, August 10.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief after his return, Hassan said, "I had a meeting in Street No. 7 in Maadi, so I took a driver at 9 am, and we reached the street at 6 pm.  They were closing.  Apparently, the real name of the street is Street No. 107, but many call it Street No. 7.  The conclusion of all this is: If you want to run a business in Egypt, you have to be sure that nothing comes on time in Egypt, except one thing, when a woman gets pregnant, in 9 months she'll deliver!"  Hassan had left to Cairo on Tuesday, August 6, 2002, on a four-day business trip.

Dana's 47th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 47th story aired on JTV on Friday, August 9, 2002.  This week's story was about the 12th Session of the General Conference of the Aal Al-Bayt Foundation for Islamic Thought which was held in Amman last week.

Zureikats Celebrate 3 Occasions

     

Zureikats celebrated Samer's birthday on Tuesday, August 6, Hassan's birthday on Friday, August 9, and Amal and Ziad's wedding anniversary on Sunday, August 11, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Samer and Hassan very happy birthdays and Amal and Ziad a very happy anniversary and all the best.
