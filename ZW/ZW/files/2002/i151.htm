151st Issue - April 7, 2002

A day after his return to Amman

Talal Undergoes Successful Operation, Returns Home

Talal returned home on Friday, April 5, after undergoing a successful Polynidal Sinuses operation (PNS) at the Amman Surgical Hospital on Thursday, April 4.  Talal, who is currently studying at the St. George's Medical School in London, had returned to Amman on Wednesday, April 3, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Talal a speedy recovery and all the best.

Hazem Offered Another Scholarship from McGill

A week after being offered the "Kofi Annan International Scholarship" from Macalester College, Hazem was offered a scholarship from McGill University in Montreal, Canada.  In her letter to Hazem, June Morrier, the Manager of Scholarships at McGill, said, "McGill scholarships are merit-based and highly competitive...The offer of this scholarship indicates our belief that you have a great deal to offer any university and reflects our confidence in your abilities."  In her letter, Morrier also said that this scholarship is renewable each year provided that Hazem maintains a "very high academic standing".

Ayman on Dean's Honor List with a GPA of 3.8

In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Amal said that Ayman, who is currently studying at Tufts University in Massachusetts, has been added to the Dean's Honor List with a GPA of 3.8.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Ayman on his accomplishment and wish him all the best.

Dana's 28th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 28th story aired on JTV on Friday, April 5, 2002.  This week's story was about a daylong telethon organized by Jordan Television to support the Palestinian people.  The telethon ended at 11pm Jordan summer time with a total of 10 million Jordanian Dinars.

Zureikats Celebrate 2 Occasions

   

Zureikats celebrated two occasions last week, Ziad's birthday on Monday, April 1, and Harran and Reema's wedding anniversary on Friday, April 5, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Ziad a very happy birthday and Harran and Reema a very happy anniversary.

  • During the past week, Zureikats have been participating in protests and rallies in support of the Palestinian people.

  • Abla N. participated in a conference discussing gifted education.  The conference was held at the Holiday Inn Hotel in Amman.
