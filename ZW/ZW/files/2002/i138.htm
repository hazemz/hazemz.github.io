138th Issue - January 6, 2002

Welcome to the new

Zureikat Weekly

As Editor in Chief of The Zureikat Weekly, I would like to welcome you all to our new 2002 page.  The new page, as you may have already noticed, has the following new features:

1. The front page of our website is always the latest issue, so you don't have to click on anything to read the latest issue.

2. The links to the (List of Issues), (Zureikat Calendar), and (JordanLIVE) pages can be found right under the number and date of the latest issue (i.e. in the front page).

3. Now, you can read ALL Zureikat Weekly issues through the (List of Issues) page.

4. The new (Rewind) section has been added to The Zureikat Weekly.  It can be found in every issue between the (Quickies) and (Pic of the Week) sections.  This new section contains headlines from issues released exactly one and two years before the issue itself.  In April, it will also include headlines in issues released three years before the issue.

I hope you enjoy the new page and the new features we have added, wishing you all a happy and prosperous new year.

Zureikats Celebrate Abu Harran's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Abu Harran's birthday on Tuesday, January 1, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Abu Harran a very happy birthday and all the best.

Tarek Leaves to the US, and Reem to the UK

 

Tarek left to Nashville, Tennessee on Wednesday, January 2, to attend several meetings there.  Tarek, who is currently working in the Nashville-based Southwestern Company, said he will probably return home in the next few months before his summer work starts.  On the other hand, Reem left to London on Friday, January 4, 2002, after spending Christmas in Jordan.  Reem is currently studying at the St. George's Medical School in London.

  • Abu Harran got a new, bigger television last week.

  • Hala hosted the Karadsheh's for dinner on Friday, January 4, 2002.  A source told The Zureikat Weekly that the Zureikats' turn is next.
