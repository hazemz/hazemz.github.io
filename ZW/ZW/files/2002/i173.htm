173rd Issue - September 15, 2002

Harran and Reema Make a Short Visit to Cyprus

 

Harran and Reema left to Cyprus on Friday, September 13, on a two-day business trip in which Harran will be attending a conference.  Harran and Reema are expected to return home on Sunday, September 15, 2002.

Marwan and Abla Host Family and Friends in Umm Al-Amad

 

Marwan and Abla N. hosted around 70 people for dinner in their farm in Umm Al-Amad on Thursday, September 12, 2002.  Zureikats were among those who attended, but Amal and Ziad were unable to attend because they had a dinner for the British ambassador in Jordan at their house.

News from the Karadshehs...

Naief and Deena undergo medical tests; everything is fine   

Salem joins Little League

Hala: Jeena is NOT returning to the Karadshehs

       

In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Hala updated us on the latest news in her family.  In her e-mail, Hala said, "The Karadsheh kids are doing fine, enjoying school.  Last week, Salem enrolled in the Little League.  His team is called "Aramex".  He is a defender.  So far, the team have had two games (on Fridays), the first of which they won 3-1 and the second was a draw.  Deena, on the other hand, has recently had a number of tests because she keeps feeling dizzy at school.  The results were fine, nothing to worry about.  All the three kids are invited to a birthday party today [Saturday, September 14], which they are very excited about!  Naief has also undergone some tests at Herran's.  He is fine.  [We] had lunch yesterday [Friday, September 13] at Umm Al-Amad with Jiddo!  Please also note that Jeena is NOT returning to the Karadshehs.  [We] have already gotten a new 20-year old Indonesian girl named Ayouni (my eyes!)."

  • Basil's brother Majdi stopped over in Amman on his way to Syria, and so as not to waste Majdi's excellent culinary skills, the Shomalis held a little barbeque on Monday, September 9.  Hala and Naief were invited and so was Jiddo Abu Harran.  What should be mentioned is that Majdi Shomali had earlier visited Amman on his way to the US between Saturday, August 31, and Monday, September 2, 2002.

  • Dian's sister, Mai, is also in town.  Her husband had a checkup at Harran's last week.  Dian also had some checkups herself.  And as we mentioned above, Naief and Dalia also had some checkups.  According to Hala, "It seems most of the Zureikat clan has been visiting Jordan Hospital this past week or so!!!"
