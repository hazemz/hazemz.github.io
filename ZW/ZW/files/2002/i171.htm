171st Issue - September 1, 2002

Samer, Ayman, Hazem Leave to the US

Hazem will be starting his first year at Macalester College

     

Samer, Ayman, and Hazem are now in the US getting ready to start the 2002-2003 academic year.  Hazem, who will be starting his first year at Macalester College in Saint Paul, Minnesota, left Amman on Saturday, August 24.  Samer, who will be starting his third year at Tulane University in New Orleans, Louisiana, also left on Saturday, August 24, while Ayman, who will be starting his second year at Tufts University in Boston, Massachusetts, left Amman on Sunday, September 1, 2002.

(Some pictures that Hazem took in Minnesota can be found right before the (Quickies) section)

Abla Returns Home after Accompanying Hazem

Abla N. returned home on Sunday, September 1, after accompanying Hazem to Minnesota.  Abla had earlier left Amman with Hazem on Saturday, August 24, 2002.

Zureikats Back to School

Kareem is this year's "Zureikat graduate"

Schools in Jordan have officially opened their doors for the 2002-2003 academic year on Sunday, September 1, 2002.  Kareem, who is studying at the Amman Baccalaureate School, is in 12th grade now.  This is his last year.  Sabine (Modern Schools) is in the 9th grade; Dalia (Amman National School) is in 8th; Nadine (Modern Schools) and Yacoub S. (Amman National School) are both in 7th; Deena (Amman National School) is in 6th; Yacoub Z. (Modern Schools) is in 5th, and Salem (Amman National School) is in the 3rd grade.  Abla N., Director of the Jubilee School, and Abla Z., Coordinator of the English Department at Amman National School, both started school late last month (August).

More about this subject from our correspondents:

Abla Z.'s summer vacation has finally come to an end.  She has started going to Amman National School every day to prepare for the arrival of the students this semester.  Let us all send Abla "good luck" vibes to help her control and tolerate the 'sometimes' nasty teenagers at ANS "illi birfa'u il daght" (quote: Abla Z.).

Yacoub S. is preparing for the new semester at school.  He was reportedly quoted as saying: "Hadoal kutub j-hannam" (These are books from hell) when he received the thick books he will be studying this year.  Once again, we wish Yacoub good luck to face these "books from hell" and the academic year as a whole.

Salem went and had a nice hair cut...He is a little bit afraid from his new teacher Mrs. Budeiri because Youbi Shomali told him that she carries a gun when she is upset...poor Salem!

Dag Appointed in Swedish Company as Head of Marketing in Europe

Dag, who was looking for a job and helping out in Dar Al-Mona last week, has been appointed as the Head of Marketing in Europe at a Swedish company.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Mona said, "Dag has got a top job in a Swedish company and will be the head of Marketing in Europe."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Dag on his new job and wish him all the best.

Sylvia Back in Vienna to Start Internship

Sylvia will be working at the Vienna University Hospital

Sylvia returned to Vienna last week to start her internship at the Vienna University Hospital.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Mona said, "Sylvia is back in Vienna where she will start an internship and cardiology on Monday, September 2, 2002, in Vienna University Hospital."

Harran and Reema Make a 2-Day Trip to Lebanon

 

After spending two days in Lebanon, where they visited the "magnificent" Kefrayya vineyards, Harran and Reema returned home on Friday, August 23.  Harran and Reema had earlier left to Lebanon on Thursday, August 22, 2002.

Ziad Chairs Gastroenterology Conference

From Al-Ra'i Daily Newspaper (Thursday, August 29, 2002)

As President of Zone 4 of the World Congress of Gastroenterology, Ziad chaired a gastroenterology conference in Amman last week.  The conference, which was patronized by HRH Prince Faisal bin Al-Hussein, was held between August 27 and 30, 2002.

Bana Returns from Aqaba

Bana returned from Aqaba on Friday, August 23, 2002.  She was invited by her friend to spend one night there last weekend.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Bana said that she "enjoyed the relaxing trip immensely and was lucky enough to spot friendly dolphins in the Red Sea."  At the moment, Bana is back in Amman soothing her burnt skin!!

Zureikats Celebrate Ayman and Talal's Birthday

 

Zureikats celebrated Ayman and Talal's birthday on Friday, August 23, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Ayman and Talal a very happy birthday and all the best.
