140th Issue - January 20, 2002

Hours after taking his driver's license

Ayman Returns to the US

Just a few hours after taking his driver's license, Ayman returned to the US on Tuesday, January 15, 2002.  Ayman is currently studying at Tufts University in Massachusetts.

Marwan Returns from 3-Day Visit to Yemen

Marwan returned from a three-day working visit to Yemen on Thursday, January 17.  During his trip, Marwan visited Sanaa, Ta'izz, and Aden.

Bana: The Countdown Begins

The countdown has begun for Bana.  In just two weeks, Bana will have her BA degree.  We'll bring you more on this story in our coming issues.

Amer Leaves to Rhode Island on the 30th

Alia returns home next Saturday

 

In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Reema said that Alia is expected to return home on Saturday, January 26.  She also said that Amer, who has just resumed his work at Beaumont Hospital in Dublin, will be visiting the US on January 30, 2002, for an interview with Brown University in Providence, Rhode Island.

2 More Stories for Dana Air on TV

Two more stories for Dana aired on TV last week.  The first was about a Royal Jordanian conference held in Amman under the patronage of HM King Abdullah II, and the second was about HM Queen Rain's visit to Dubai.

  • Hazem finished his "tajreebi" exams on Saturday, January 19, while Kareem started his finals on Sunday, January 20, 2002.  Other Zureikat school students finished their finals earlier last week.

 

  • Ten days after the storm, 50cm of snow still cover Anjara.  It is "out of this world", as Reema described it.

  • Maan Bashour made a brief visit to Amman on Sunday, January 20, 2002.
