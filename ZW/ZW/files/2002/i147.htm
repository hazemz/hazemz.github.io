147th Issue - March 10, 2002

JTV Interviews Dana on the Friday Morning Show "Yis'ed Sabahak"

Jordan Television interviewed Dana live on the Friday Morning Show "Yis'ed Sabahak".  In the interview, Dana, the supervisor for the Jordan Movie Channel (Channel 3), spoke about the future plans for the channel, stressing the need to stay in touch with the audience.  She also commended the work of the people at the Graphics & Animations Department at the channel, saying that their work is what makes the channel successful.

After a 15-day trip to the Far East

Amal and Ziad Return Home

 

After a fifteen-day trip to the Far East which included Thailand, Japan, and Malaysia, Amal and Ziad returned home on Saturday, March 9, 2002.

Marwan in Boston...Returns Sunday

During his trip, Marwan met with Ayman

Marwan left to Boston, Massachusetts on Monday, March 4, on a business trip.  He is expected to return home on Sunday, March 10, 2002.  During his trip, Marwan spent some time with Ayman, who is currently studying at Tufts University near Boston.

Hassan Returns from London

After making a three-day business trip to London, Hassan returned home on Wednesday, March 6, 2002.

Reema on a Three-Day Visit to Oman

Reema left to Muscat, Oman on a three-day business trip on Friday, March 8.  She is expected to return home on Monday, March 11, 2002.

Dana's 22nd Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 22nd story aired on JTV on Friday, March 8, 2002.  This week's story was about an Arab media conference held in Amman last week.

Zureikats Celebrate Yacoub's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Yacoub Z.'s birthday on Friday, March 8, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Yacoub a very happy birthday and all the best.
