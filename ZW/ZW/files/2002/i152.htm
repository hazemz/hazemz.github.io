152nd Issue - April 14, 2002

Amer and Leanne Make it Official

The Zureikats ask the Kawars for their daughter's hand in marriage

The ceremony was attended by family and friends from both sides

 

"I ask for your daughter, Leanne George Kawar's, hand in marriage for my grandson, Dr. Amer, the son of Dr. Harran Zureikat."  With these words, Abu Harran made it official: Amer and Leanne are to be engaged soon.  The Zureikat-Kawar "Jaha" ceremony was held on Thursday, April 11, 2002.  It was attended by many family and friends from both sides.  The program started at about 5:30pm when Zureikat family and friends gathered at Harran's house.  The ceremony then moved to Geroge Kawar's residence in Abdoun at about 6:30pm.  Zureikats and their friends received a warm welcome from the Kawars and then entered the living room, where the main event took place.  All this as Zureikat and Kawar women were listening in from the second floor.

Amal Leaves to Turkey to Attend Conference

Amal left to Turkey on Friday, April 12, to attend a conference there.  She is expected to return home on Monday, April 15, 2002.

Hazem to Begin Mocks on April 27, Finals on June 16

Hazem, who is currently in 12th grade, will be finishing school on Thursday, April 25.  His mock (tajreebi) exams will start on Saturday, April 27, and will finish on Tuesday, May 14.  His official final exams, the Tawjihi, will start on June 16 and end on July 6, 2002.

Dana's 29th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 29th story aired on JTV on Tuesday, April 9, 2002.  This week's story was about a demonstration held under the patronage of HM Queen Rania in support of the Palestinian people.
