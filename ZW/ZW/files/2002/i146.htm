146th Issue - March 3, 2002

Hassan Returns from Syria...Leaves to London

Hassan returned home on Friday, March 1, after a one-day visit to Syria.  On the other hand, Hassan is expected to leave to London on a business trip on Sunday, March 3, 2002.

Amal and Ziad in Tokyo after Visiting Bangkok

 

Amal and Ziad are now in Tokyo, according to what Amal told the Editor in Chief in a phone call before she left Amman last week.  "From Bangkok, we will go to Tokyo on March 1," she said, "Then on March 5, we will go to Kuala Lumpur, spend two days, and return back to Amman."

Dana's 21st Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 21st story aired on JTV on Friday, March 1, 2002.  This week's story was about the new "Culture Street", a pedestrian street in Shmeisani to be open to the public next May.

Zureikats Celebrate Reem's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Reem's birthday on Monday, February 25, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Reem a very happy birthday and all the best.

  • Zureikats in Amman visited Wael Zureikat and Jezel Zumot Zureikat in their new home in Amman.

  • Hazem, Dalia, Yacoub S., and Deena all got certificates for academic excellence in the first semester of this school year.  Congratulations!
