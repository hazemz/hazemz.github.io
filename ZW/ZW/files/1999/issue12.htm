Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

12th Issue

Friday, July 9, 1999

This issue was released from Beirut, Lebanon

Reema to Return from Hospital Soon

Due to Pneumonia, Reema had to go to the hospital a few days ago.  Abla N. told the Editor in Chief that Reema will be returning home in a couple of days.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Reema a speedy recovery.

Zureikats Celebrate Three Occasions Last Week

Zureikats all around the world celebrated three occasions last week.  The family celebrated Amal's birthday on July 3, Abla Z. and Basil's anniversary on the same day and Marwan and Abla N.'s anniversary on July 4.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to send its best wishes to Amal, Abla Z., Basil, Marwan and Abla N..

 

Family Members Gather at Harran's House Celebrating His Birthday

Many family members currently in Amman gathered at Harran's house to celebrate his birthday on July 1.  They all had dinner and cake there.  

Harran Now in Yemen...Hassan in Egypt on Sunday

Harran, accompanied by a team of doctors, left Amman on Wednesday, July 7, for Yemen.  Harran will be spending almost four days there.  On the other hand, Hassan will be leaving for Egypt on Sunday, July 11.

Editor in Chief to return to Amman next Saturday

Hazem Attends IC Commencement...Tours Lebanon

Hazem visited Junieh, Harisa, J'eeta and Brummana

Hazem, who is currently in Lebanon, attended his cousin, Khaled's, graduation from the International College in Beirut.  Khaled graduated from the Mathematics section with honorable mention.  Khaled's parents, some of his family members and their friends attended the graduation.  A few days later, Najla, Abla N.'s sister, Khaled and Mr. Sami Farah, a relative, took Hazem to some touristic attractions in Lebanon.  Hazem visited Junieh, a city near Beirut, where he was carried by a sky ride from the shore to Harisa, on the mountains.  Hazem also visited the J'eeta Grotto and then Brummana, where he had dinner.  Khaled also took Hazem to a play at the Beirut International Art Festival for the Younger Generation, where Hazem had his portrait drawn.  The Editor in Chief is expected to return to Amman on Saturday, July 10, 1999.
