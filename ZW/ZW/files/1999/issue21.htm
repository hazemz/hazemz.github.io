Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

21st Issue

Friday, September 10, 1999

Ziad Undergoes Surgery...Returns Home

Ziad underwent an operation to remove abscess from his leg last week.  After staying in the hospital for a few days, Ziad returned home.  A source told The Zureikat Weekly that Ziad's condition has improved "dramatically" and that he will be leaving for Cairo to attend a conference there next Sunday.

Lunch for Umm Basil at Marwan's

    

A small family lunch was held for Umm Basil, who is currently staying at Basil's house in Amman, on Thursday, September 9, at Marwan's house.  Umm Basil, Abu Harran, Harran, Reema, Alia, Kareem, Basil, Abla Z., Bana, Omar and Yacoub attended the lunch.

Amer Returns to Dublin after Severe Sinusitis

Amer, who was supposed to return to Dublin two weeks ago, postponed his trip after suffering from a severe sinusitis.  Amer returned to Dublin on September 5 after the CT Scan tests were negative.

After returning from a Far Eastern tour

Reem Starts her First Clinical Year

"They have unleashed us on to the patients"

After returning to London from a Far Eastern tour, Reem sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly.  In her e-mail, Reem said, "I have returned from my trip.  It was amazing.  At the moment, I have just started my first clinical year, they have unleashed us on to the patients."  Reem also sent her best wishes to her father, who underwent surgery, and to all Zureikats.

After her guest from Egypt returned home

Dana to Return to Cairo Monday

Hiba Andeel, Dana's guest from Egypt, returned to Cairo on Tuesday, September 7, after spending almost ten days in Amman.  Dana, who spent more than one month in Amman, is expected to return to Cairo on Monday, September 13, to start her second year at AUC.

Zureikats at ABS Start School  

Samer, Ayman, Talal and Kareem, who study at the Amman Baccalaureate School, started school on Saturday, September 4.  Salem, who is in KG2 at Amman  National School, also started school on the same day.  The rest of The Zureikats started school on September 1.
