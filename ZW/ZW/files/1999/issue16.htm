On July 30, 1999, two years after the death of Umm Harran, Zureikats visited the Umm Harran Gazebo which was built in Ajloun last summer.  The family later went to have lunch at the Ya Hala restaurant in Jerash.

Dana Returns Home and Celebrates Her Birthday

Dana will be staying in Amman till the beginning of September

Dana cutting the cake

After attending a summer course at the American University in Cairo, Dana returned home on Friday, July 30, 1999 to spend one month in Amman.  On August 1, Zureikats celebrated Dana's birthday.  Family members called Dana to wish her a happy birthday.  A small lunch was also held at Marwan's house on Dana's birthday.  Abla Z. and some friends attended.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Dana a happy birthday.

The latest on Reem's Far Eastern tour

Reem Starts a Three-Day Sailing Cruise

Reem, who is currently touring the far east, sent a brief e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly from Australia on August 3.  Reem said, "I am in Airlie Beach at the moment waiting to go on a sailing cruise for three days.  It is beautiful here."

Amal and Samer Attend an Opera in Lebanon

Samer will be celebrating his birthday in Beirut

    

Amal and Samer left for Beirut a few days ago.  They attended an opera there.  Samer will be celebrating his birthday in Beirut on August 6.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Samer a happy birthday.

After returning from Syria

Hassan Prepares for Family Vacation

Hassan returned from Syria on July 31, 1999 after going there on July 29.  A source told The Zureikat Weekly that Hassan might be taking his family to Turkey soon.

A week after Ayman's return from Washington

Talal Returns from Five-Week Summer Camp

Talal returned home on July 30 after spending five weeks in a summer camp in Canada.  Ayman had returned to Amman from Washington on July 21 after attending the Seeds of Peace Conference there.

Naief and the Girls Go to a Church Camp

         

Naief, Dalia, Deena and some relatives of Naief  went to Ajloun on a church camp.  They will all return to Amman on Friday, August 6.
