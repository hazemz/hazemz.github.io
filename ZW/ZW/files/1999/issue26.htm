Talal Starts 1st Year at UWC

Amal: Life is tough there, but he's getting used to it every day

Talal left for Trieste, Italy on Monday, October 11, 1999, to start his first year in the United World Colleges of the Adriatic.  Talal was accepted last week to attend UWC and was asked by the college to arrive in Italy "as soon as possible".  A small farewell party for Talal was held at Amal's house.  The party was attended by Bana, Omar, Hazem, Kareem and Talal's classmates from Amman Baccalaureate School and Amman National School.  In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Amal said, "Talal is fine.  He's living with Michael, an American who is originally Italian.  Life is tough there, but he's getting used to it every day.  Ayman here is also fine.  He still didn't feel Talal's absence.  His friends from ABS and ANS are calling him regularly to check on him."  Talal is expected to return back home in mid December.

After a short stay in Amman

Tarek Back in Edinburgh

After a short stay in Amman, Tarek returned to Edinburgh on Monday, October 11, 1999, to start his third year in the University of Edinburgh.  Tarek is currently studying Business and Accounting.

Abla Hosts the Family in a Friday Lunch

 

On Friday, October 15, 1999, Abla Z. hosted the Zureikats in a lunch celebrating Mona's visit.  The lunch included Mlookhyyeh and the usual Koosa and Warak.  Abu Harran, Harran, Reema, Kareem, Marwan, Abla N., Hazem, Dian, Sabine, Nadine, Yacoub Z., Mona, Hala, Dalia, Deena and Salem attended the lunch.

Mona Returns from Ramallah

After spending a few days in Ramallah, Mona returned to Amman on Sunday, October 10.  Mona is expected to make a brief visit to Beirut next week.

Hassan Starts a Short Trip to Damascus

Hassan and Dian are expected to leave for London on November 1

In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Dian said that Hassan left for Damascus on Friday, October 15, for a short, one-day business trip.  Dian also said that Hassan is expected to leave for London on November 1 for another business trip.  She added that she may accompany Hassan in his trip to London.

Abla: Basil will not Return from SA before Wednesday

Basil is currently on a business trip in Saudi Arabia

Abla Z. told The Zureikat Weekly that Basil, who left for Saudi Arabia on a business trip on October 6, will not be returning home before Wednesday, October 20.
