Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

7th Issue

Friday, June 4, 1999

Amer Earns 1st Diploma

Amer, who studies in Dublin, Ireland got his first diploma in obstetrics and gynecology with honors.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Amer on earning his diploma and wish him all the best.  On the other hand, six students, including Amer, got nominated for the Golden Medal Award last week.  Amer is currently doing his final exams and is expected to return home on June 19, 1999.

 

Busy Week for Zureikat Students

Coronation delays summer holiday in Jordan

Tarek, who studies at the University of Edinburgh, started his final exams on Wednesday, June 2.  He will finish on June 4.  Tarek will later leave for the States to work there in the summer and is expected to come to Jordan in October.  Meanwhile, Bashar and Bana, who study at the Jordan University, started their exams on May 31, 1999 and are expected to finish in the second week of June.   Omar and Hazem, who study at Amman National School, started their final exams on June 3.  Omar had a math exam on Thursday, June 3, and Hazem had a history exam.  Omar and Hazem will finish on June 14.  Samer, who studies at the Amman Baccalaureate School, will start his exams on June 7 and finish on June 16.  Ayman and Talal, who started their IGCSE's on May 17, are currently having a ten-day holiday and will finish their exams around June 15 while Kareem, who also studies at the Amman Baccalaureate School, will start his exams on June 12.  Omar, Samer and Hazem were supposed to finish their exams a day earlier, but now June 9 will be a national holiday in Jordan.  Sources say that there will be a coronation on that day for the new King Abdullah and Queen Rania.

 

Dana Returns Home

Dana, who studies at the AUC in Egypt, returned to Amman on Wednesday, June 2, 1999.  Dana was received at the airport by her parents who attended a reception at the Marriott Hotel earlier in the evening.

 

Reema and Alia Return From London

After spending almost two weeks in London, Alia returned to Amman on Friday, May 28 after finishing her finals.  Reema later returned from London on Monday, May 31.  Family members visited Harran's house to welcome back Reema and Alia. 

 

The Zureikat Weekly Receives the last e-mail from Birmingham

Hala and Family Attend Farewell Dinners

"Saturday we are making a garage sale for the last things we have"

"Our telephone will be off as of Tuesday June 8"

Hala sent her last e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly from the US on Wednesday, June 2.  Hala said that anyone can send her an e-mail until Sunday, June 6.  Hala also said that her telephone will be off as of Tuesday, June 8, 1999.  Hala, Naief and the kids have been invited to several farewell parties.  Regarding this, Hala said, "tomorrow, we are invited for a farewell dinner in a Lebanese friend's house.  Saturday the principal of the school is inviting us to eat dinner outside with her daughter who used to be Dalia and Deena's teacher for English as special language."  Hala will be making a garage sale on Saturday, June  5, after packing most of her things.   "Saturday, we are making a garage sale for the last things we have.  We have kitchen appliances and desks, and of course our van still unsold," she said.

 

Hassan's Family Celebrates Anniversary and Birthday

Hassan and Dian celebrated their anniversary on May 30.   They, along with the children, also celebrated Sabine's birthday on May 31.   Sabine got a cake and had a small party with her family.

 

Hassan to Travel to Egypt Soon

Source: He may spend 2 - 3 days there

A source told The Zureikat Weekly that Hassan might be going to Egypt soon.  The source also said that Hassan will spend almost two to three days there.  Meanwhile, Dian and the kids are expected to go to Syria in mid June.   The source said Hassan may meet his family in Syria after his trip to Egypt.

 

Harran, Abla and Alia Eat Dinner at Marwan's

A few days after returning from London, Alia, along with Harran, ate dinner at Marwan's house on Sunday, May 30.  Abla Z. was also there.

 

Marwan and Family Spend an Afternoon in Umm Al-Amad

Marwan, Abla N. and Hazem spent an afternoon in Umm Al-Amad on Friday, May 28.  They all returned to Amman later in the day.

 

Lama Bashour Arrives in Amman

Lama Bashour, Abla N.'s niece from Lebanon, arrived in Amman on Tuesday, June 1.  Lama is expected to stay in Amman until Sunday, June 6, 1999.

 

End of 7th Issue
