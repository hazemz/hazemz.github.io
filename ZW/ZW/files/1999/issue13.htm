Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

13th Issue

Friday, July 16, 1999

Reema Returns Home

Reema is currently recovering from Pneumonia

Reema after returning home

After staying at the hospital for a few days due to Pneumonia, Reema returned home and is currently recovering.  Family members visited Reema to wish her well.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Reema a speedy recovery.

Family Celebrates Two More Birthdays

Zureikats celebrated Nadine's birthday on July 11, 1999.  Family members wished Nadine a happy birthday.  Nadine also invited some friends and family to a play place in Amman.  The family will be also celebrating Bashar's birthday on July 18.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Nadine and Bashar happy birthdays.

Harran Returns Home...Hassan to Return Friday

Harran returned from Yemen on Sunday, July 11, 1999 after spending four days there with a group of doctors.  On the other hand, Hassan, who is currently in Egypt, is expected to return home on Friday, July 16.

Amal and Reem Return From London

         

Amal returned home on Friday, July 9, after visiting Casablanca and London.  Reem later returned to Amman on Sunday, July 11.  Amal and Reem had attended a memorial service for the late King Hussein in London.  On the other hand, Reem is expected to leave Amman on Sunday, July 18, for London.  Reem will be later going to Australia, Singapore, Thailand and Malaysia.

After visiting Alai

Editor in Chief Returns Home

Hazem continued his tour in Lebanon by visiting Alai, in the mountains, on Friday, July 9 accompanied by Najla, Khaled and Mr. Sami Farah.  The Editor in Chief returned home on Saturday, July 10, 1999.

Bana to Sing at a Choir Next Monday

Many family members will be going to the Amman Castle to watch Bana

Bana will be singing with a choir next Monday, July 19, 1999 at the Amman Castle.  Many family members will attend.  Bana had sung a duet with Ala' Zureikat at an S.O.S party in Amman last month.

  Celebrating the return of Hala and the grandchildren

Family Lunch to Be Held at Abla's

A family lunch will be held at Abla Z.'s house on Friday, July 16.  Abla Z. told The Zureikat Weekly that this lunch will be made celebrating Hala's return and the return of the Zureikat students that were abroad.
