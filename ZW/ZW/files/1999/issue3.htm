Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

3rd Issue

Friday, May 7, 1999

Mona Describes Her Trip to Egypt and Morocco

"Please give my greetings to the whole family"

On an assignment for the Swedish government, Mona left for Egypt and Morocco two weeks ago to attend seminars there.  After returning to Stockholm on May 5, Mona wrote an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly telling us about her trip.   In her e-mail, Mona said, "I came back from a long trip to Egypt and Morocco.  It has been great to be in Rabat, Fez, Miknas and Walily, beautiful cities of Andalus.  I felt the great history of the Arabs and listened to Andalusian songs and music.  Besides it was great to feel the Soofism and philosophy everywhere.  Morocco is the place I recommend to all of you."  In her e-mail, Mona also expressed her happiness after hearing that Amal was recovering quickly.

 

Hala to Return Between 3/6 and 10/6

Reema: Everything is fine in Hala's house here in Amman

In her weekly e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Hala said that she, Naief and the kids will be returning to Amman sometime between June 3 and June 10, 1999.  In her e-mail, Hala also included her news there.  "The summer swimming pools are now open in our apartment complex.  The children enjoyed the water yesterday (Monday the 3rd) afternoon," she said.  Regarding the visit of Naief's sister Muntaha and her husband Zuheir to Birmingham, Hala said, "Naief's sister and her husband left to Georgia early morning to visit family members there."  Hala also asked about Amal, who underwent hysterectomy two weeks ago.  In another e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Hala said, "Dalia's class celebrated Mother's Day.  I attended a very nice party in her classroom and they offered us some hand-made presents.   Dalia said that my mother deserves to be the mother of the years because she helps me in everything and she cares then she said an acrostic (M for music, O for original, T for thinks, H for honest, E for eager and R for responsible.  That makes a nice poem for Mother).  Also Naief was invited two weeks ago to attend breakfast with Dalia in her classroom celebrating Father's Day.  We might go this weekend to Six Flags in Atlanta."  On the other hand, Reema and her driver Mohammad checked on Hala's house in Amman last week.  Reema told The Zureikat Weekly that everything was fine there.

 

Hassan and Dian Leave for Beirut

Children to stay at Marwan's house

On Wednesday, May 5, 1999, Hassan left for Beirut, Lebanon on a business trip.  Dian also accompanied Hassan in his two-day visit there.  Sabine, Nadine and Yacoub are expected to stay at Marwan's house and sleep there Thursday (May 6) night.  Abla N. said that she will take the children to Umm Al-Amad.

 

Tarek to Work in Southern California This Summer

Tarek, who studies at the University of Edinburgh, Scotland, sent an e-mail last week to The Zureikat Weekly.  Tarek said that Reem, who studies in London, will be visiting him on May 8, 1999.  He also said that he will be leaving to the United States on June 20 to work there after being chosen student manager in the Southwestern Company which recruits students from around the world for summer work.   Tarek said that he will be working somewhere near San Diego in Southern California this summer.

 

Ziad Attends a Conference in Hims

On Friday, April 30, 1999, Ziad returned from Hims, Syria after attending a conference on the digestive system there.  Ziad had left for Syria on Tuesday, April 27, 1999 accompanied by delegations from Iraq, Egypt and of course Jordan.  Ziad also made a lecture there regarding the digestive system.

 

Accompanied By Mahmoud, Nabila, Rana and Rasha Lababidi

Marwan and Harran Spend A Day at the Dead Sea

On Saturday, May 1, 1999, Marwan, Abla, Hazem, Harran, Reema, Kareem and their friends Mahmoud, Nabila, Rana and Rasha Lababidi went to the Movenpick Resort and Hotel in the Dead Sea - Jordan.  They all ate lunch at  the "Saraya" restaurant, one of the many restaurants there, then made a quick tour of the new hotel.  Saturday, May 1, was a national holiday in Jordan celebrating Labor Day.

 

Abu Harran, Abla and Harran Go To Umm Al-Amad

On Friday, April 30, 1999, Abu Harran ate lunch at Abla Z.'s house and later went with her to Amal's house to check on her after undergoing her surgery.  Then, Abu Harran and Abla Z. went to Marwan's farm in Umm Al-Amad.  Later in the evening, Harran, Reema and Kareem came and ate dinner there.  Marwan, Abla and their friends, Mahmoud and Nabila Lababidi, left for Umm Al-Amad Friday morning.

 

Harran to Attend a Conference in France

A source told The Zureikat Weekly that Harran and Reema will be going to France on May 17, 1999.  Harran will be attending a conference on cardiology there.  The source also said that Harran and Reema will later go to London to visit Alia who will leave for London on May 15.

 

Omar goes to Petra with ANS

Omar, along with his friends from the 11th grade in Amman National School, went to Petra on Wednesday, May 5, 1999.  The students enjoyed the city of Petra then returned to Amman in the evening.

 

Harran and Marwan Eat Dinner at Yousef's House

Marwan, Harran, Abla N., Reema, Hazem and Kareem ate dinner at Dr. Yousef Nusseir's (Abla N.'s brother) house on Thursday, April 29, 1999.

 

Sabine Dances in the Modern School

On Thursday, May 6, 1999, Sabine danced in a sports festival in her school, The Modern School, in Amman with other students.  Sabine later returned to Marwan's house with Nadine and Yacoub to stay there after Hassan and Dian's departure to Beirut.

 

Alex Leaves for Damascus

Bana's friend from the United World Colleges in Wales, Alex, who arrived in Amman last week, left for Damascus, Syria Wednesday (May 5) afternoon after spending a few days in Amman.  Alex visited downtown Amman, Jerash, Petra and other places.
