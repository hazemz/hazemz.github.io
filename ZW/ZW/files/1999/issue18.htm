Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

18th Issue

Friday, August 20, 1999

Abu Harran Recovers from Minor Accident

Abu Harran is currently recovering from bruises after slipping in his bathroom.  Amal told The Zureikat Weekly that Abu Harran is now fine and that he had lunch at Harran's on Thursday, August 19.

Abla Now Up and Around after Bacterial Infection

Zureikats visited Abla to wish her the best

After staying in bed for a few days due to a severe bacterial infection, Abla N. told The Zureikat Weekly that she is fine now.  Abla also said that she will be resting for a few days and will later return to work.  Zureikats visited Abla at home and wished her the best a speedy recovery.

 After mistakenly getting hit by his car

Ziad ALSO Recovering from Bruises and Wounds

Ziad is currently recovering from some bruises and wounds after mistakenly getting hit by his car.  Amal told The Zureikat Weekly the reasons behind this accident.  "We were returning from Marwan's house after visiting Abla," she said, "when we arrived at our house, Ziad saw Polo, the dog, biting a cat, so he ran out and kept the car on "Drive" mode.  I couldn't do anything so the car slipped towards my car.  Ziad stood between the cars and got hit by his car."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Ziad a speedy recovery after this painful yet strange accident.

Dana's Friend Arrives in Amman

Sara will be visiting the Dead Sea and other touristic sites in Jordan

Sara after having lunch at Marwan's

Sara Sullivan, Dana's American friend from Egypt, arrived in Amman on Wednesday, August 18 to spend a few days there.  Sara and Dana will be visiting the Dead Sea and other touristic sites in Jordan.

Shomali Dinner to be Held at Abla's

Majdi and family left Amman last week

    

After spending almost ten days in Amman, Majdi, Basil's brother, his wife and their children left Amman.  On the other hand, Rose, Basil's sister, and her daughter arrived in Amman last week.  Basil's mother will also be joining the Shomalies on Friday, August 20.  A source told The Zureikat Weekly that a dinner for the Shomalies will be held at Abla Z.'s on Friday, August 20.

Reem to Leave Australia Friday

Reem will be going to Thailand, the last country in her tour

Reem, who is currently in Sydney, Australia on a Far Eastern tour, will be going to Thailand on Friday, August 20.  Reem will stay in Thailand till September 2 then will leave for London.
