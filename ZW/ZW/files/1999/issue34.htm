Friday, December 24, 1999

Everyone Back in Amman to Celebrate Christmas

Zureikats to gather at Harran's on Christmas Eve

Family celebrates Amer and Reem's return at Amal's

       

 During the last two weeks, Talal, Dana, Tarek, Amer, Reem, and Alia returned to Amman for their mid-year holiday.  The last to return home were Amer, Reem, and Alia.  Amer and Reem returned on Saturday, December 18, while Alia returned on Monday, December 20, 1999.  Zureikats celebrated Amer and Reem's return at Amal's house on Saturday, December 18.  Family members also visited Harran's house on Monday to see Alia.

A source confirmed that the family will be gathering at Harran's house on Christmas Eve on Friday, December 24, 1999.  The family will also have lunch at Marwan's on Christmas Day.  Last week, Helene also confirmed her visit to Amman.  Helene will be arriving on Tuesday, December 28, 1999, and will leave on January 4, 2000.
