Zureikat Students Prepare for School

Omar and Samer will be starting their last year

Hala's children will go back to ANS

     

     

     

Zureikat students in Jordan are currently preparing for the 1999\2000 scholastic year.  This year, Omar and Samer will be starting their last year in school, Ayman and Talal will be in the 11th grade, Hazem will be in 10th, Kareem in 9th, Sabine in 6th, Dalia in 5th, Nadine and Yacoub S. in 4th, Deena in 3rd, Yacoub Z. in 2nd and Salem in KG 2.  Dalia, Deena and Salem will be going back to Amman National School this year after spending a year in the Green Valley Elementary School in Birmingham, Alabama.

Mona, Gero and Sylvia to Spend a Week in Greece

Mona, Gero and Sylvia will be going to Greece on Sunday, August 29, to spend one week there.  In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Mona said that Dag and Helene will be staying in Stockholm.  Mona also said that Helene will be going back to her university on Monday, August 30.

Harran to Leave for Barcelona Thursday

Harran will be leaving for Barcelona, Spain on Thursday, August 26.  Details to come  in the next issue.

Amer Back in Dublin August 31

Alia to spend two more weeks in Jordan

    

Amer, who will be graduating next year from Dublin, will be returning to Ireland on August 30 or 31, 1999, while Alia, who studies in England, will be staying in Amman till September 15.

Reem Continues Her Far Eastern Tour in Ko Samui 

A photo of the Ko Samui Island

After arriving in Thailand, the last country in her Far Eastern tour, last week, Reem sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly.  In her e-mail, Reem said, "At the moment, I am in Thailand.  It's beautiful here, but extremely hot and stuffy.  I am on Ko Samui Island which is on the south east.  It's beautiful and the people here are friendly as well."  Reem also sent her love to the family.

Ayman and Talal Celebrate Their 16th Birthday

    

Ayman                                                  Talal

Ayman and Talal celebrated their 16th birthday on August 23, 1999.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish them a happy birthday.

Umm Basil in Amman...Rose and Daughter Leave

After spending a year in the US, Basil's mother arrived at her son's house in Amman last Saturday, August 21.  On the other hand, Rose, Basil's sister, and her daughter left Amman after spending a few days there.

More Guests from Egypt at Marwan's

Sara Sullivan, Dana's friend from Egypt, left Amman on Tuesday, August 24, after spending six days in Jordan.  Sara visited the Dead Sea and other touristic attractions in Jordan.  Hiba, another friend of Dana's, will be arriving in Amman on Saturday, August 28.
