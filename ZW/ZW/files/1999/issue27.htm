Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

27th Issue

Friday, October 22, 1999

As you can see, this issue of The Zureikat Weekly is almost blank.  The Editor in Chief wishes to voice an objection against the lack of cooperation from all the Zureikats who failed to provide The Zureikat Weekly with any news.  The Zureikat Weekly is your magazine and can only continue with your support and cooperation. 

After a three-week visit to the Middle East

Mona Returns Home

Mona returned home after visiting Ramallah, Beirut

After spending almost three weeks in Amman, Ramallah and Beirut, Mona returned to Sweden on Thursday, October 21.  Family members gathered at Abu Harran's to see Mona before her departure.  During her stay in Amman, Mona visited schools and delivered a lecture.  Mona is expected to visit Sharjah, UAE soon.

Zureikats Celebrate Dalia's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Dalia's birthday on Tuesday, October 19, 1999.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Dalia a happy birthday and all the best.

Majdi Shomali in Amman

Basil's brother, Majdi Shomali, arrived in Amman on Thursday, October 22, 1999, to spend a few days there.

  • Marwan pays his condolences in Karak after the death of Dr. Jiries Zureikat's brother last week.

  • Amal, Abla Z., Bana and Hazem participate in Al-Hussein \ Al-Amal March in Amman on Friday, October 22.

End of 27th Issue
