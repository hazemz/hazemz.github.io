Friday, July 23, 1999

Celebrating Hala's return

Zureikats Gather at Abla's

The hosts

Reem, Bana and Alia

A family lunch was held at Abla Z.'s house on Friday, July 16, 1999, celebrating Hala's return home.  Abu Harran, Harran, Amer, Alia, Kareem, Marwan, Abla N., Hazem, Hassan, Dian, Sabine, Nadine, Yacoub, Reem, Samer, Hala, Naief, Dalia, Deena and Salem were there.  The lunch included Freekeh, the usual Koosa and Warak and more dishes.

Bana Sings at Amman Citadel with Choir

Part of the choir

Zaid Dirani

A concert for the young Jordanian musician, Zaid Dirani, was held in the Amman Citadel on Monday, July 19.  The concert included almost ten pieces composed by Dirani.  The pieces were played by Zaid Dirani himself along with the orchestra of the National Music Conservatory.  Bana, who sang with a choir at Monday's concert, told The Zureikat Weekly that she was honored to sing at a concert for Zaid Dirani, who is a prodigy in Amman now.  Abla Z., Basil, Yacoub S., Amer, Alia, Marwan, Abla N. and Hazem attended the  concert.

Hala Appointed Director of Channel 2

An official statement is expected to be issued soon

A source told The Zureikat Weekly that an official statement saying that Hala Zureikat will be appointed Director of Channel 2 - Jordan Television is expected to be issued soon.  Hala is currently the Acting Director in Channel 2.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Hala and wish her all the best.

Family and Friends Eulogize Nabil Ammari

The late Nabil Ammari

A eulogy was held for Nabil Ammari, who died almost forty days ago.  Harran, who was one of the speakers, said that Nabil always kept his morale high and was loving and warm at all times.  "He avoided hate and detestation," he said, "even with his sickness."  Harran also spoke about his special relationship with the late Nabil Ammari. 

Three Birthdays Celebrated in the Last Two Weeks

   

Zureikats celebrated three birthdays in the last two weeks, Nadine's birthday on July 11, Bashar's birthday on July 18, and Dian's birthday on July 20.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish them all happy birthdays.

 Reem Leaves Amman to Start Far Eastern Tour

Reem left Amman on Sunday, July 18, 1999.  Reem will be starting a tour that will include Australia, Singapore, Thailand and Malaysia.

Ayman Returns from Washington

Ayman was attending the Seeds of Peace Conference

After attending the Seeds of Peace Conference in Washington, Ayman returned home on Wednesday, July 21.  Talal, who is currently in Canada, will be returning home in a few weeks.

After attending a summer course at AUC

Dana to Return Home Next Week

Dana, who studies at the American University of Cairo, will be returning home next week.  Dana is currently attending a summer course.

Helene Sends an E-mail from Frankfort

Helene will be returning to Stockholm on August 5

Helene sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly.  In her e-mail, Helene said, "I am still in Frankfort working for a bank.  I have enjoyed my stay here so far...This coming weekend I am going to Saarland by train.  Saarland is situated by the French border.  In Saarland, I will visit my "family", that is my host family by whom I have been living as an exchange student six years ago."  Helene also said that she will be returning home to Stockholm on August 5, 1999.
