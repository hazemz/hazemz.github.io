Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

2nd Issue

Friday, April 30, 1999

Amal Returns Home After Surgery

Amal returned home on Tuesday, April 27, 1999 after undergoing hysterectomy, a major surgery, in Farah Hospital - Amman on Thursday, April 22, 1999.  Family and friends visited Amal in her house after returning from the hospital and sent some flowers.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Amal a speedy recovery.

 

The Zureikat Weekly receives e-mails from Alabama and Sweden

Helene: Sylvia to Return in June

Hala: Our Trip to Mobile was Very Nice

In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Helene said that she still studies at the Stockholm School of Economics and will graduate in one year.   Helene also said that Sylvia will be returning to Sweden from Vienna (where she is studying medicine) on her birthday (June 4).  She also stated that Dag returned from a one-week business trip to Japan.  Dag is working with a company specializing in information technology.  The e-mail also said that Gero and Mona were doing fine.   The Zureikat Weekly also received an e-mail from Hala in Birmingham, Alabama.  Hala said that she is accompanying her husband Naief, who is a Fulbright fellow and a visiting professor at the medical school in the University of Alabama.  Dalia, Deena and Salem are attending school at Hoover.  Dalia (4th grade) and Deena (2nd grade) are attending the Green Valley Elementary School while Salem, who is four years old, is attending the Oak Mont Day Care.  The children are having fun in the U.S.  Hala also said that she isn't working but she's doing a lot of sports and enjoying her time doing activities with her kids.  Naief, Hala and the kids spent a weekend in the Gulf Coast (Saturday, April 24 - Sunday April 25, 1999).  Naief had a conference to attend there.  Regarding this trip, Hala said in her e-mail, "we returned Sunday evening from Mobile.  It was a very nice trip with a lot of sea food and drinks, invitations here and there.  The kids enjoyed Dauphin Island very much and swam in the sea.  It was a fantastic experience especially for Salem".  In her e-mail, Hala also said that she is invited for a special party in Dalia's class on May 5th celebrating Mother's Day.  Meanwhile, Hala has started preparations for their return to Jordan which is due in the first week of June.  Naief and Hala are currently enjoying the company of Montaha (Naief's sister) and her husband Zuheir.

 

On an assignment for the Swedish government

Mona Travels to Egypt and Morocco

On an assignment for the Swedish government, Mona left Sweden on April 21, 1999 for Cairo, Egypt to attend a seminar there.  She met Dana (who is studying at the American University of Cairo) and had dinner with her.  Mona later traveled to Rabat, Morocco to attend another seminar.  She  talked about children's books in the two seminars.  Mona is expected to return to Stockholm on May 5, 1999.  In an e-mail to Hazem, Dana said that she accidentally left her purse in one of Cairo's cabs.  Mona, on the other hand, couldn't give up and made every cab driver she rode with swear on the Koran that the purse wasn't in his car!

 

Dana: I Wish I Can Share Cairo With Every Zureikat

Dana sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly on the 29th telling us what exactly she's doing in Cairo, Egypt.  Dana is studying for her MA degree in TV Journalism in the American University of Cairo (AUC).  She also works with the Adham Center on an electronic journal (TBS), you can read her three articles by clicking on the previous link.  Dana also does the PR (public relations) for the center and helps out in a print publication called "In Focus".  Dana is staying in a two-bedroom apartment in Cairo.  She is really enjoying it there.  Dana also sent her greetings to the Zureikat family and her best wishes to Amal after undergoing her surgery.   Dana said that she welcomes anyone from the family going to Cairo to stay with her and that she wishes she could share Cairo with every Zureikat member.

 

Abla Celebrates Her Birthday in Umm Al-Amad

Abla Z. celebrated her birthday on Friday, April 23, 1999 in Umm Al-Amad.  Abla spent the day there with Marwan, Abla N., Basil, Omar, Hazem and Yacoub.  They all returned to Amman Friday evening after eating cake and drinking tea.  Due to weather conditions, Abla couldn't blow a proper candle on a cake, so Marwan used  matches!

 

Hassan to Visit Beirut on the 5th

Hassan will be leaving for Beirut, Lebanon on Wednesday, May 5, 1999 for a two-day business trip there.  Hassan had returned from Saudi Arabia last week after meeting with government officials and the private sector there.  In a phone call to The Zureikat Weekly, Dian said that she may accompany Hassan in his trip to Beirut.

 

Abla and Abla Attend the PSC

Under the patronage of HRH Princess Basma, the 8th conference of the Private Schools' Council (Al-Moltaka Al-Thakafi Al-Tarbawi) was held at the Ma'arif School - Amman.  Abla N. and Abla Z. attended the conference in its two days. The conference's  theme this year was "protecting the identity".   To see a picture of Abla N. in the PSC, click HERE.

 

Amer Rotates Around Different Hospitals

Amer, who is currently studying at the Royal College of Surgeons in Dublin, is doing a 2-4 week training through different departments in different hospitals in Ireland.  Amer has already finished his training in the psychiatric, pediatric and obstetrics departments.  Amer had taken the responsibility of delivering three girls and two boys in the obstetrics department!  He is currently training in the surgical department in one of Ireland's hospitals.  Amer is expected to return to Amman on June 15, 1999 to work for a month and head back to Dublin.

 

Bana's Friend From UWC Arrives in Amman

Alex to visit Petra and Aqaba

Bana's friend from the United World Colleges in Wales, Alex, arrived in Amman on Monday, April 26, 1999 starting a series of visits to Jordan, Syria and Lebanon.  Alex is staying at Basil and Abla's house.  Bana took her guest to downtown Amman, Al-Kalha Restaurant and other places.  Alex will be going on Friday, April 30 to Petra and will also go to Aqaba on Saturday, May 1. 

 

Alia to Return to London May 17th

Alia, who studies in the Royal Holloway in London, is expected to return to her university there on May 17, 1999 to sit for her final exams.   Alia will then return to Amman on May 29 or May 30 to spend her summer vacation there.

 

CIH Virus Hits Harran's Computer

A very strange virus hit thousands of computers around the world on April 26, 1999.  This virus, the CIH (or Chernobyl) virus, is kept in the memory of some computers until the above date, then it removes all the files from the computer.   Alia's friend used the computer mistakenly on that day causing their computer to get infected.  The computer was later taken to the company and fixed.

 

Kareem to Perform at ABS Talent Show

Reema told The Zureikat Weekly that her son Kareem will be playing the drums in a small band on Wednesday, May 12, 1999 in the Amman Baccalaureate School's talent show.
