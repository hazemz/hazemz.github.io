The first issue of The Zureikat Weekly was released on Friday, April 23, 1999.  Since then, our website have received almost 300 hits (visits).  Now, twenty five weeks after the release of the first issue, Zureikats around the world sent their congratulations to the Editor in Chief of The Zureikat Weekly on the release of the 25th issue, The Silver Issue.

E-mail from Alia:

Congrats to you.  You're superb, as well as to the whole family for their contribution!!!

Some congratulations sent to The Zureikat Weekly on the release of the 10th issue on June 25, 1999:

Harran and family:

                   

Harran and family extend their thanks and appreciation to all the marvelous efforts of Hazem, Editor in Chief, for the idea and execution of The Zureikat Weekly.  This is a wonderful contribution to keeping in touch with all members in the family locally and abroad.  Great ideas start with a dream.  Thank you Hazem again, and again, and again.

Abla Z. and family:

              

This warm and fascinating news bulletin has done a great job.  It has brought those who are afar so close to us. It has also added warmth, love and humor to our daily routine.  We would like to extend our deepest wishes to the master mind behind all this.  Hazem, a special grandson of our family, whose great talents and relentless efforts made this bulletin so candid and true.  Thank you Hazem and all our best wishes.

Mona and family:

We send our congratulations to The Zureikat Weekly, which has shown great appreciation among its readers.  The quality of the issues discussed has won the heart and mind of all those who subscribe in it.  The Swedish correspondent and its team wish you a bright and successful future.

Talal Accepted...Leaves Monday

Last week, Talal got accepted to attend the United World Colleges in Italy.  Talal is expected to leave Amman on Monday, October 11, 1999.  Details on Talal's departure in the 26th issue.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Talal on his acceptance.  Talal is the second Zureikat to attend UWC after Bana.

Mona Starts a Visit to Jordan

After spending five days in Amman, Mona left for Ramallah on Thursday, October 7.  Mona is expected to return to Amman from Ramallah on Monday, October 11.  She will then spend a few days in Amman and leave for Beirut.  After that, Mona is expected to return again to Amman and then return back to Sweden on October 21.

Mona's Arrival

Mona and Abu Harran at the night of her arrival

Mona arrived in Amman on Saturday, October 2.  She was received at the airport by Abla Z. and Bana.  A small dinner was then held at Abu Harran's.  The dinner was attended by Abla Z., Basil, Bana, Reema, Kareem and Hazem.

Mona meets Queen Rania

Jordan's Queen Rania Al-Abdullah

During her five-day stay in Amman, Mona met with Queen Rania Al-Abdullah of Jordan.  The meeting was described as being successful.

Tarek Returns Home

Tarek returned home on Saturday, October 2, 1999.  He was received at the airport by Bashar and Omar Tillawi, Tarek's friend.  Tarek, who spent the last three months working in the United States, is expected to return to Edinburgh on Monday, October 11, 1999.

Zureikats Celebrate Bana's Birthday

Zureikats around the world celebrated Bana's 19th birthday on October 3, 1999.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take his opportunity to wish Bana all the best.

In her first e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly

Alia: Send Everyone There my Love

"Wish Talal warm congrats for getting the scholarship"

After getting connected to the internet, Alia, who is currently in London, sent her first e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly.  In her e-mail, Alia congratulated The Zureikat Weekly on the Silver Issue.  In her e-mail, Alia also said, "wish Talal warm congrats for getting the scholarship!  Tell him Alia says, "a fat lot of studying you sure are going to do with all those Italians!""  At the end of her e-mail, Alia said, "Send everyone there my love and take care."
