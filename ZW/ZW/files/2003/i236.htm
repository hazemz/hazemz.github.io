November 30, 2003

Harran Resumes Work after Undergoing Cataract Operation

Harran resumed his work on Saturday, November 29, after undergoing a cataract operation on his right eye on Monday, November 24, 2003, and spending the rest of the week at home.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Alia said, "It was fun having Dad home.  We watched loads of movies!!  Family came round throughout the week."

Bana Returns from London

After a weeklong trip sponsored by Fastlink in which she attended a four-day training course on marketing, Bana returned home on Sunday, November 30, 2003.

Amal and Ziad Return from Germany

 

Amal and Ziad left to Germany on Wednesday, November 26, where Ziad attended a conference.  They returned to Amman on Saturday, November 29, 2003.

 

Ziad Attends Breakfast Hosted by the King

Ziad, along with members of the new Senate, attended a breakfast hosted by His Majesty King Abdullah II on Tuesday, November 25, 2003.

Zureikats return from their vacations after Eid and Thanksgiving

Marwan and Abla spent the Eid El-Fitr holiday in Sharm El-Sheikh, Egypt.  They left Amman on Monday, November 24, and returned on Friday, November 28, 2003.

Left: Dana, Ammar, Hazem, and Ammar's sister-in-law and her two daughters.

Samer spent his Thanksgiving holiday with a friend in Atlanta, Georgia.  He left New Orleans on Tuesday, November 25, and returned on Sunday, November 30, 2003.

Tarek Hosts Another Dinner for Hassan, Shomalis
