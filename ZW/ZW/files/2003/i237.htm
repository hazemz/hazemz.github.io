Mona Arrives in Amman

 

 

 

 

Mona arrived in Amman on Saturday, December 6, 2003, for a weeklong visit in which she will attend two events related to Dar Al-Mona's new book Fatat Al-Burtuqal, one at Darat Al-Funun and the other at the Jordanian Writers' Union.  Celebrating Mona's visit, the family gathered at Abu Harran's on Sunday night (December 7).  In an e-mail she sent to the Editor in Chief, Abla N. said that during the gathering, Mona "talked about how she met Gero, then about the civil war in September 1970, then about her marriage, then about her books, and she signed the books she gave her brothers.  She also talked about the Jordanian royal visit to Sweden."

JCE Changes its Name to 'engicon'

Jordanian Consulting Engineer Co. has officially changed its name to engicon starting Sunday, December 7, 2003.  Marwan, Chairman of the company's board, said the change aims at reflecting "the new status of the company".  He said the new name will also help the company to better position itself in the outside market and "respond to new opportunities".  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Marwan, Tarek, and engicon on the new name, wishing the company all the best.

Zureikats Celebrate Marwan's Birthday

 

 

 

Zureikats celebrated Marwan's birthday on Tuesday, December 2, 2003.  On this occasion, the family gathered at Marwan and Abla's on Tuesday night.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Marwan a very happy birthday and all the best.

Hala Continues Physiotherapy for Bursitis

Hala has been suffering from leg pain for a few months
Doctors: Everything should be okay by February

Hala, who has been suffering from leg pain for a few months, underwent some tests and said that the results where "okay".  She said that the tests showed that the leg pain was caused by bursitis -- which results from excessive work on the hips.  Hala said that she will continue her physiotherapy exercises and that doctors told her everything should be okay by February.  Hala also said that she got a permit from the University of Maryland to allow her to park her car near the building she goes to daily to minimize walking.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Hala a speedy recovery and all the best.

Jordanian Senate Holds Its First Session

Ziad sitting among members of the Senate and the lower house of parliament

The new Jordanian Senate, with Ziad as one of its members, held its first session on Monday, December 1, 2003.  Amal also attended the session.

Dian Gets a New Jaguar

Dian bought a new Jaguar about two weeks ago.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Dian on her new car, wishing her all the best.
