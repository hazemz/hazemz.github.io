Family and Friends Celebrate Dana's Graduation from AUC

The June 27 dinner party was attended by over 70 people

 

 

 

 

Over 70 family members and friends attended the dinner party which was held in Marwan's farm in Umm Al-Amad on Tuesday, June 27, celebrating Dana's graduation from the American University in Cairo with a Masters degree in TV journalism.  During the dinner, the family also celebrated Lara Ghaleb Zureikat's birthday which was on June 26.  Dana's graduation was held in Cairo on June 14, 2000 and was attended by Marwan, Abla N., Hazem, Reema, and Alia.

In an unexpected turn of events

Abla, Dana, and Hazem Visit Beirut

 

Left: Abla and the Editor in Chief with the two graduates \ Right: Some of the graduates during the commencement ceremony

 

Left: Abla with her sister Najla \ Right: A street in Beirut

Abla N., Dana, and Hazem made a surprising two-day trip to Beirut, Lebanon on Wednesday and Thursday, June 28-29, 2000, to attend Ahmad and Bana Bashour's graduation from the American University of Beirut.  Ahmad and Bana are Abla N.'s sister, Najla's, children.  Ahmad graduated from the Department of Business Administration, while Bana graduated from the Department of Arts & Sciences with distinction and the Penrose Award. 

Zureikats Celebrate Harran's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Harran's birthday on July 1, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Harran a very happy birthday and all the best.

To participate in a summer camp 

Ayman Leaves for England...Sunday

Ayman will be leaving for England to participate in a summer camp there on Sunday, July 2.  In a short statement, Ayman told The Zureikat Weekly that he will be returning home on August 6, 2000.

Slight Change of Plans on Dana's Internship

On July 20, Dana will be leaving to Beirut, then Cairo, then Amman, then London

As an outstanding graduate at the Adham Center for TV Journalism in the American University in Cairo, Dana won the Showtime Internship Award early last month.  The award includes three months internship in Showtime's offices in London, airfare, and accommodation.  In a phone call with Showtime, Dana was informed of a slight change of plans in her internship.  First of all, Dana will be leaving to Beirut to shoot something there on July 20.  On July 27, 2000, Dana will be leaving to Cairo to do the same there.  Dana will then return home to spend a few days with the family and then leave for London to start her three-month internship.

Almost two weeks after the Zureikat trip to Egypt

Editor in Chief Receives Letter from Alia

Almost two weeks after the Marwan, Abla N., the Editor in Chief, Reema, and Alia visited Cairo to attend Dana's graduation, Alia sent the following letter to the Editor in Chief:

"Dearest Hazem,
...
I'm writing this email as a gesture of heart-warming thanks for all that you, Ammo Marwan, and Auntie Abla, as well as Dana, have done for me and my mother in Egypt.  As the lovely pictures reveal, we have managed to see all the majestic places and sites that characterize Egypt as one of the most culturally and historically attractive cities in the world.  This, together with attending Dana's graduation (which means a lot to us!!), made it the trip of a lifetime!  It was definitely worth all the long hours of walking we endured.

Last but not least, hope you had a wonderful BDAY, and may you have a wonderful year ahead with many more to come.

All my love,

Alia".
