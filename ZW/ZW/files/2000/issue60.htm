Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

60th Issue

June 25, 2000

Amer Makes a Surprising Brief Visit Home

Amer attended his uncle's wedding, returns to Dublin Sunday

Amer made a surprising return home last week on a brief visit to Amman in which he attended his uncle, Walid Ayoub's, wedding on June 23.  Amer is expected to return to Dublin on Sunday, June 25, 2000.

Almost one week after her return home

Zureikats to Celebrate Dana's Graduation...Tuesday

A picture from Dana's June 14 graduation (The Zureikat Weekly's archive)

Dana returned home on Wednesday, June 21.  Celebrating her graduation which was held in Cairo on June 14, Marwan and Abla N. will be holding a dinner party on Tuesday, June 27, in their farmhouse in Umm Al-Amad.  Zureikat family members, including some "extended" Zureikat members, Abla N.'s family, and other friends will be attending the dinner party.  What should be mentioned is that Marwan, Abla N., Hazem, Reema, and Alia had earlier attended Dana's graduation ceremony in Cairo on June 14, 2000.

Editor in Chief Celebrates his Birthday

On Friday, June 23, 2000, Zureikats celebrated Hazem's 16th birthday.  Family members around the world called Hazem to wish him all the best on this occasion.  Marwan, Abla N., Dana, and Hazem also went to have lunch at a restaurant on that day celebrating this occasion.

On a short business trip

Hassan to Visit Beirut Monday

Hassan will be leaving for Beirut on Monday, June 26, 2000, on a business trip.  He is expected to return home a few days later. 

After a week of intensive training in Nashville, TN

Tarek and Bashar to Arrive in Virginia

  

After leaving Edinburgh and Amman early last week and taking intensive training courses in Nashville, Tennessee, Tarek and Bashar, along with the other members of Tarek's group, are expected to arrive in Virginia, where they will be working in the next three months, within the next 24 hours (Saturday-Sunday, June 24, 25, 2000).  More details in the next issue.

Zureikats Attend Walid Ayoub's Wedding

Harran held a pre-wedding dinner on Tuesday, June 20

Zureikats is Jordan attended Reema's brother, Walid Ayoub's, wedding.  Walid got married to Dr. Valentina Hamshawi on Friday, June 23.  Family members, including Marwan, Abla N., Hazem, Amal, Abla Z., Bana, Hala, Naief, and of course Harran, Reema, Amer, Alia, and Kareem, also attended the dinner party which was held after the wedding at the Grand Hyatt Hotel.  On the other hand, Harran earlier held a pre-wedding dinner on Tuesday, June 20, 2000.  Zureikats also attended that dinner.
