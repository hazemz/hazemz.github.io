Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

71st Issue

September 10, 2000

 

 

 

A few days before Alia's departure

Zureikats Gather at Marwan's

In the second largest family gathering for the year 2000 after Easter, Zureikats in Jordan had lunch at Marwan's on Friday, September 8, 2000.  The lunch was held a few days before Alia's departure this week.

Hassan and Family Return Home

Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub started school on Thursday

Hassan, Dian, Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z. returned from Syria on Wednesday, September 6.  Hassan had left for Syria on August 31, while Dian and the children left almost one month ago.  Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z., who study at the Modern School, started school on Thursday, September 7, 2000.

Departure Date Unknown...Alia Awaits Confirmation

Alia and Reema were supposed to leave to Hemel Hempstead on Sunday

Sunday's flight to London is full, and Alia is still awaiting a confirmation for a flight on either Monday or Tuesday, September 11 or 12.  Alia and Reema, who were supposed to leave on Sunday, September 10, 2000, will first go to Hemel Hempstead where Alia will be doing some exercises.  After spending almost two weeks there, they will leave to London, and Alia will start her last year at the Royal Holloway.  Details of Alia's departure in the 72nd issue.

One day after the training course in Denmark ended

Bana: I'm Here in Sweden with Mona, Gero, and Dag

  

Bana is currently on a social visit to Sweden after attending a three-week training course in Denmark entitled "Youth Empowerment and Democracy".  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly from Sweden, Bana said, "I'm here in Sweden with Mona, Gero, and Dag.  I'm relaxing and having a good time."  Details of Bana's visit to Denmark and Sweden in the coming issues.

After Hassan rejoined his family, Ayman alone at home eating tuna fish and eggs!

Ziad in Egypt after Visiting France

 

From Al-Ra'i Daily Newspaper - Tuesday, September 5, 2000

Ziad is currently in Egypt after making a business trip to France.  Ziad left Amman on Monday, September 4, 2000.  Details on Ziad's trip in the next issue.

Talal Makes a One-Day Visit to Venice

In an e-mail he sent to The Zureikat Weekly on Friday, September 8, Talal said that he will be making a one-day visit to Venice, Italy with the college on Saturday, September 9, 2000.  Talal is currently in his second and last year at the United World Colleges of the Adriatic in Trieste, Italy.

Hala to Visit Rabat This Week

Hala will be visiting Rabat, Morocco to attend a Euro-Mediterranean meeting there between September 12 and 19, 2000.
