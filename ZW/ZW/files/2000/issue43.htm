Helene Starts Studying at Bocconi University

Helene is currently taking courses on fashion, Corporate Finance, and Logistics

After arriving in Milan, Italy last January, Helene started studying at Bocconi University.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Helene said, "A week ago, I completed my Italian course here in Italy with a good grade.  Now, I have finally started studying at Bocconi University.  I have chosen to take three strategy courses, one about the fashion industry, another Corporate Finance course, and a course in Logistics.  I especially enjoy studying for the fashion course, because Milan is the center of fashion in the world, and we will have guest speakers from Versace, Armani and Gucci at our lectures."  In her e-mail, Helene also said, "A week ago, I also visited Florence with some friends from my university.  In Florence, we visited the famous Uffizi museum with artworks from above all Rembrandt, Raphael and da Vinci.  The Duomo (a huge cathedral) was also a
very impressive sight.  I will, in the near future, also travel down to Rome to visit an Italian friend from my university, who is working in Rome at the moment.  As you know, my family and I lived in Rome when I was a child, so it will be really interesting to see our old "homes" and my old school again."

Zureikats celebrated Reem's birthday on Friday, February 25, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Reem a very happy birthday.

Zureikats Congratulate Marwan on JCE's Upgrading

Harran and family: We are sure that the input of this office, with such an outstanding leadership, will bring proper development to Jordan

After Jordanian Consulting Engineers Co. was upgraded last week to Class A, Zureikats around the world congratulated Marwan on this achievement.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Harran and his family said, "Harran and family extend their congratulations to Marwan and the Jordanian Consulting Engineers family on the upgrading.  We are proud of the degree of excellence the office runs and are sure that the input of this office with such an outstanding leadership will bring proper development to Jordan.  All our love."  Mona also sent her congratulations by e-mail.
