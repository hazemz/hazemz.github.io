The Zureikat Weekly Switches to its Christmas Layout...Next Week

Celebrating Christmas 2000, The Zureikat Weekly will be switching to its Christmas layout starting next week.  The layout includes our Christmas logo, Christmas trees background, and Christmas background music.  The Christmas issues will include extensive coverage on who returns when (to Amman) to celebrate Christmas and all the latest news on the Christmas Eve and Christmas Day celebrations.

Zureikats Celebrate Deena's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Deena's birthday on Tuesday, November 21, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Deena a happy birthday and all the best.

Tarek Sends his First Video E-mail

A still picture of Tarek's first video e-mail

Using his roommate's Logitech video camera, Tarek sent his first video e-mail from Edinburgh on Thursday, November 23.  In his video e-mail, Tarek briefed the family on his latest news and sent his regards to everyone.  Another video e-mail was sent from Tarek on Saturday, November 25, 2000.

  • Hazem, along with the Student Council and PTA of Amman National School, distributed foods and other things for the poor at Al-Sadaqa village near Ma'an, Jordan on Saturday, November 25, 2000.  The distribution is part of the "Winter Campaign" ANS does every year before Ramadan.

  • Amal and Ziad returned from Rabat.

  

  • For those of you who want to know, Ramadan starts on Monday, November 27, 2000.
