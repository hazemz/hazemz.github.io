Zureikats Congratulate

Dr. Amer Zureikat

on Graduating

The graduation was attended by Harran, Reema, Alia, and Marwan

Dr. Amer Harran Zureikat graduated from the Royal College of Surgeons in Dublin on Friday, May 26, 2000.  The graduation was attended by Harran, Reema, Alia, Marwan, and Reema's brother, Samir Ayoub.  Zureikat all around the world called Amer to congratulate him.  The Zureikat Weekly would also like to take this opportunity to congratulate Amer on his graduation and wish him all the best in the future.  In a short e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Marwan said that the graduation was very grand and that 153 students from 28 countries graduated.

Next Zureikat graduate: Samer, on May 31, 2000.

Marwan Leaves for Ireland, Germany, and the US

Marwan attended Amer's graduation in Dublin and left for Germany

Marwan left Amman on May 24, 2000, to visit Dublin, Ireland, Hanover, Germany, and Denver, Colorado.  After attending Amer's graduation on May 26, Marwan left for Germany on May 27 on a business trip.  He will also visit Denver, Colorado on June 1 on another business trip.  Marwan is expected to return home on June 4, 2000.  What should be mentioned is that Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem will be leaving for Cairo on June 11 to attend Dana's graduation there.

Zureikats in ANS Start Their Finals on Wednesday

Hazem and Dalia, who study at Amman National School, are expected to start their final exams on Wednesday, May 31, 2000.  Dalia will finish her exams on June 6, while Hazem will finish on June 8, 2000.  

Ziad and Talal Return

  

Talal returned home to spend his summer holiday in Amman on May 22, 2000.  Ziad also returned from a business trip in the US on May 27, 2000.
