A Historic Lunch for the Zureikat Grandchildren at Abu Harran's

 

 

Zureikat grandchildren currently in Jordan (Dana, Bashar, Bana, Omar, Ayman, Hazem, Kareem, Sabine, Dalia, Yacoub S., Nadine, Deena, Yacoub Z., and Salem) all attended a historic lunch at Abu Harran's on Saturday, November 18, 2000.  The lunch was only for the grandchildren.  Hala and Dian were also allowed to come to accompany their young children.  The lunch included Koosa and Waraq and Chicken and Rice in addition to the starters, Hommos, Fatteh, and Tabbouleh.  At the end, the grandchildren took a picture with Abu Harran (the picture is shown above).  The lunch came after an announcement that Abu Harran made at a Zureikat lunch at Abla Z.'s house last week.
