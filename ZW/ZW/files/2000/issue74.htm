Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

74th Issue

October 1 , 2000

Abu Harran Undergoes Operation,

Returns Home Soon

The Zureikat Weekly didn't announce anything on Saturday upon the request of some Zureikats

Abu Harran underwent an operation on Saturday, September 30, 2000, in the Jordan Hospital to remove a blockage from his colon.  The blockage was localized and was removed easily.  Abu Harran is currently in the Intensive Care Unit (ICU) and is expected to return home very soon.  The Zureikat Weekly didn't announce anything at first upon the request of some Zureikats.  More details about the operation from Harran in the next issue.

Tarek and Bashar Return Home

Both are expected to arrive in Amman early Sunday

  

Tarek and Bashar are expected to return home on Sunday, October 1, 2000, by 1 AM.  Tarek will be coming from London after he spent two days with Dana, and Bashar will be coming from Detroit, Michigan, where he spent a few days with his uncle.  Tarek and Bashar have been working for the Southwestern Company in the US since June.  Both have entered the Presidency Club; Tarek, after making $2700 in one week, and Bashar after making $650 in one day.

Reema and Amal Return

Alia and Reem back to their universities in London

  

Reema returned home on Thursday, September 28, after accompanying Alia, who has started her last year in London, back to her university.  Amal also returned home on Friday, September 29, 2000, after accompanying Samer, who has started his first year in Tulane University,  to his university.  Amal also attended conferences in the US and spent a few days with Reem in Hawaii and the Maui Islands.  Reem is now back to her university in London.

Amer Back in Dublin after Brief Visit

Amer returned to Dublin after spending a few days in Amman on Sunday, September 24, 2000.

With Hazem as its President

Students at ANS Elect Their Student Council

The new council held its first meeting on Saturday

After Hazem was named President last week, students at Amman National School elected the other members of the Student Council.  Following are the results: Vice Presidents: Amer Abu Al-Ragheb and Zeena Jaja, Secretary: Masarra Toukan, Treasurer: Zein Malhas, and Committee Coordinator: Fares Sweiss.  The council held its first meeting on Saturday, September 30, 2000.
