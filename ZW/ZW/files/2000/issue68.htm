Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

68th Issue

August 20, 2000

Accompanied by Amal

Samer Leaves to the US

Amal hosts the Zureikats on a Thursday dinner

 

 

Number 12 of the Zureikat grandchildren to study abroad left Amman Saturday to start his first year at Tulane University in New Orleans, Louisiana.  Amal held a dinner on Thursday, August 17, for Samer before he left.  Zureikats in Amman attended the dinner.  Samer, accompanied by Amal, will first visit Chicago and Houston before he arrives in New Orleans on August 25, 2000.  Amal, who will be spending a few days with Samer in New Orleans, will then visit Arizona to attend a course and Honolulu to attend a meeting.  Reem is expected to meet with Amal in New Orleans and Honolulu, and both will be taking a vacation in the Maui Islands for three days before Reem returns to London in the end of September.  Amal will return to Amman also around the end of September.

Dana Starts 3-Month Internship at Showtime

Dana left for London on Wednesday, August 16

After her visits to Beirut and Cairo with Showtime, Dana visited Amman for two days and then left for London to start a 3-4-month internship with Showtime Television Network on Wednesday, August 16, 2000.  Dana and her Egyptian roommate Effat Kamel will be sharing an apartment in Oxford Street during the internship.

 Bana Starts 3-Week Training Course in Denmark

Mona to visit Denmark later this week

Bana left Amman on Monday, August 14, to attend a 3-week training course for Arab youth leaders entitled "Youth Empowerment and Democracy" in Denmark.  After the training course, Bana is expected to visit Mona and the family in Sweden on September 4, 2000.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Mona also said that she may be visiting Denmark this week for one day.

Hala and Family Return from Aqaba

Hala, Naief, Dalia, Deena, and Salem returned from a four-day vacation in Aqaba, Jordan on Saturday, August 19.  Hala and family had earlier left for Aqaba on Wednesday, August 16, 2000.

Harran to Visit Amsterdam...August 25

Harran will be leaving to Amsterdam, Holland on Friday, August 25, 2000 on a one-week visit to attend a conference there.
