80th Issue

November 12 , 2000

Abla and Basil Host the Zureikats

No pictures available...the camera was at Marwan's office

  

Abla and Basil hosted the Zureikats on a Friday lunch on November 10.  Most Zureikats in Amman attended.  After lunch, Zureikats sang Happy Birthday for Hala, who celebrated her birthday on Thursday, November 9, 2000.  We apologize for not having any pictures of this lunch because the digital camera was at Marwan's office at that time.

Abu Harran Announces Lunch for Grandchildren...Next Saturday

During the lunch at Abla Z.'s on Friday, November 10, Abu Harran announced that he will be holding a lunch for his grandchildren on Saturday, November 18, 2000.  All Zureikat grandchildren in Amman are expected to attend.  Details AND PICTURES in the 81st issue.

Zureikats Celebrate Hala's Birthday

The family also celebrated Mona and Gero's anniversary last week

  

Zureikats celebrated Hala's birthday on Thursday, November 9.  As mentioned above, the family made a small celebration on this occasion in Abla Z.'s lunch on Friday, November 10.  Zureikats also celebrated Mona and Gero's anniversary on Saturday, November 4, 2000.

 

We welcome any suggestions for a new logo and a new look for The Zureikat Weekly in the year 2001.
