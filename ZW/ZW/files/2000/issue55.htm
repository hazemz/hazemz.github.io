Busy week for the Sharaiha family

Talal Returns Home

Preparations underway for Samer's graduation next week

  

The Sharaihas are having a very busy week.  On Monday, May 22, 2000, Talal is expected to return home to spend his summer holiday.  Samer, who finished his IB exams on May 17, will also be graduating from school next week on Wednesday, May 31, 2000.  Samer's prom will be held on May 25.  Amal is expected to have a pre-prom party at her house for Samer and his friends on the same day.  On the other hand, Ayman is preparing for his final exams which will start on June 7.

Two other graduations also coming up

Harran and Reema Leave for Paris before Dublin

Marwan Leaves on May 24

Marwan, Abla, and Hazem to attend Dana's graduation on June 14

  

Harran and Reema left for Paris on Monday, May 22, before going to Dublin to attend Amer's graduation will be held on May 26.  Marwan, who will also attend the graduation, is expected to leave for Dublin on May 24.  Alia and Reema's brother, Samir Ayoub will also attend.  On the other hand, Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem are expected to attend Dana's graduation on June 14.  An awards dinner, in which Dana is expected to receive her Showtime Internship Award, will be held two days before the graduation.  Marwan, Abla, and Hazem will be leaving for Cairo on June 10 or 11, 2000.  A source also told The Zureikat Weekly that Amal may be attending Dana's graduation.

The fourth Zureikat graduation is on July 11

Omar Prepares for "Tawjihi" Finals

A dinner party for ANS's Class of 2000 will be held on May 25

Omar finished the school's final exams on May 17 and is currently preparing for the Tawjihi finals (held by the Ministry of Education in Jordan) which will start on June 15 and end in the beginning of July.  Omar's graduation will be held in Amman on July 11, 2000.  On the other hand, the 11th grade in Amman National School and the Student Council there, with Hazem as its Vice President, will be holding a dinner party for the Class of 2000 on Thursday, May 25, 2000.

Dana's Final Project Rated #1 Amongst her Class

In her second and last year in the Masters Degree program at AUC, Dana made 11 projects (stories or TV reports).  Dana's final project was rated number 1 amongst her class by visiting critics from the US and the UK.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Dana on this accomplishment and wish her all the best.  As we mentioned above, Dana's graduation will be held in Cairo on June 14, 2000.

Ziad in San Diego to Attend Two Conferences

Ziad left for San Diego on Thursday, May 18, to attend two conferences from the American Academy of Gastrointerology and the World Congress of Gastrointerology.  Ziad is expected to return home on Friday, May 26, 2000.
