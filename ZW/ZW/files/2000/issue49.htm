Dana Leaves on Monday...Tarek on Wednesday

  

Dana, who arrived in Amman on Monday, April 3, is expected to return to Cairo on Monday, April 10, 2000, after spending six days with Tarek in Amman.  Dana will be staying in Cairo till June; her graduation will be held on June 14 right after an awards dinner on June 12 in which her name will be announced for winning the Showtime Internship Award.  Dana will then return to Amman for two weeks and leave for London to start her three-month internship.  On the other hand, Tarek, who returned to Amman last month, is expected to return to Edinburgh on Wednesday, April 12, 2000.  Tarek will immediately leave for the US in June to start his third summer working with the Southwestern Company.  In this summer, Tarek will be an organization leader.  He will be responsible for almost 30 people, including some Jordanians who he is currently recruiting in Amman.

After celebrating their anniversary in Amman

Harran and Reema Visit Iraq

  

Harran and Reema left for Iraq on Friday, April 7, on a business trip for Harran; they are expected back in Amman on Monday, April 10.  Harran and Reema had early celebrated their anniversary on Wednesday, April 5, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Harran and Reema a happy anniversary and all the best.

Mona Visits Dubai on her Way to Amman

After returning from Milan last week, Mona left for Dubai on Friday, April 7, 2000, on her way to Amman.  Mona is expected to arrive in Amman sometime next week.

Amal and Ziad Return Home

  

After spending five days in Cairo on a business trip for Ziad, Amal and Ziad returned to Amman on April 7, 2000.  During his trip, Ziad attended a conference in Egypt in his new post as president of Zone 4 in the World Congress of Gastrointerology.

50th (GOLDEN) Issue to be Celebrated with 51st Issue

The 50th issue of The Zureikat Weekly, to be released next week, will be a normal issue.  The 51st issue, to be released on April 23, will be a special issue celebrating the 50th (GOLDEN) issue and the 1st anniversary of The Zureikat Weekly, which is April 23, 2000.
