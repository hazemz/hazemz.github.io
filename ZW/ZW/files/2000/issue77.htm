77th Issue

October 22 , 2000

Harran and Reema Return from D.C...Monday

Harran left to the US last Sunday to attend a conference and a training course

  

Harran and Reema are expected to return home on Monday, October 23, after a weeklong visit to Washington D.C.  Harran and Reem had left Amman on Sunday, October 15, 2000.  Harran is currently attending a conference and a training course in the US.

Dana Arrives in Edinburgh to Visit Tarek

  

Dana arrived in Edinburgh on Saturday, October 21, to visit Tarek, who is studying there.  Dana, who is currently on a three-month internship with Showtime, is expected to return to London on Monday, October 23, 2000.

Zureikats Celebrate Dalia's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Dalia's birthday on Thursday, October 19, 2000.  On her birthday, Dalia invited her classmates to a small party at her house.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Dalia a happy birthday and all the best.

After they left the hospital and everything was back to normal

Alia Sends her Regards to Abu Harran and Marwan

In an e-mail she sent two weeks ago, Alia sent her regards to Abu Harran and Marwan, who were both hospitalized in the last month:

"I'm...very glad to see that Ummo Marwan is up and running!  Please send him my love.  I would also like to wish Jiddo Abu Harran a speedy recovery.   I'll be thinking of him."

The Zureikat Weekly would like to apologize to Alia for not publishing what she said in the previous issue.

  • Hazem, President of the Student Council at Amman National School, along with the rest of the Student Council and the administration of the school, is starting a campaign in Jordan to boycott all Israeli and American products.  The Student Council of ANS is expected to meet with student councils of other school in Amman on Monday, October 23, 2000, to spread the campaign.
