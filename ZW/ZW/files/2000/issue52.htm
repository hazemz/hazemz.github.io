Zureikats in Jordan

Celebrate Easter

The family gathered at Harran's on Easter Sunday

 

 

 

Zureikat children egg hunting

Zureikats in Jordan celebrated Easter on Sunday, April 30, 2000.  On this occasion, the family gathered at Harran's house for its annual Easter Sunday Lunch.  Besides the usual Arabic food, the Easter lunch also included an egg hunt for Zureikat children.  Two guests attended the Easter Lunch this year, Dian's sister Jumana and Amal's guest from Egypt.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish all Zureikats a very happy Easter and all the best.

Under the patronage of HRH Prince Faisal bin Al-Hussein

Cyber Kids Holds a Successful Electronic Art Fair

From Al-Ra'i Newspaper.  Please note that Abla appeared in a picture on Addustour Newspaper.  We'll try to provide you with this picture in the next issue.

After weeks of preparations, Cyber Kids - Amman, with Abla N. as its Educational Director, held its First Electronic Art Fair at the Royal Cultural Center in Amman under the patronage of HRH Prince Faisal bin Al-Hussein on Thursday, April 27, 2000.  The fair included projects which were created on the computer by Cyber Kids students.  What should be mentioned is that Cyber Kids is a franchise, first established in Cyprus.  Its main purpose is using the computer as a tool in learning, just like a paper and a pen.  The fair, which was only five hours long, was described as being "very successful".  The fair was mentioned in the 6 o'clock news on Friday, April 28, and in major Jordanian daily newspapers.

Mona, Reem, and Sang Leave

Mona celebrated her birthday in Amman this year 

  

Mona, who arrived in Amman on Tuesday, April 11, on a 15-day visit to the region, returned to Sweden on April 26, 2000.  On April 25, one day before her departure, Zureikats celebrated Mona's birthday at Abu Harran's house.  On the other hand, Reem, who arrived in Amman a couple of weeks ago, left for London on April 26.  Her Korean friend Sang also left with her.

It's That Time of the Year Again...The Final Exams

This year's graduations:

Amer: May 26, Samer: May 31, Dana: June 14, Omar: July 11

With the start of May, the summer holiday gets closer and closer every day.  Zureikat students all around the world have started preparing for their final exams.  Omar, who is currently doing his last year in school, has already started the school's final exams and is expected to sit for the government's exams on June 15.  His graduation will be held on July 11.  Samer, on the other hand, who is also in his last year, will start his IB (International Baccalaureate) exams on May 2 and finish on May 17.  His graduation will be held on May 31, 2000.  Kareem, Hazem, Sabine, Nadine, Yacoub Z., Ayman, Yacoub S., and Dalia will start their finals exams by the beginning of June.  As for Zureikats abroad,  Amer is currently sitting for his finals.  His graduation will be held on May 26 in Dublin.  Harran, Reema, Alia, and Marwan will be attending the graduation there.  Alia, who is currently in Amman, will return to London on May 6 or May 9 and join her parents in Dublin on May 22, 2000.  Talal will return to Amman probably by the end of May.  Tarek will finish his finals in June, and Dana is expected to finish her final project within the next two weeks.  She may be returning home soon, and she will later leave for her graduation which will be held on June 14, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Zureikat students around the world all the best in this hectic time.  More news on other Zureikat students in the coming issues.
