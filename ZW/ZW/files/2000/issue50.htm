Mona Arrives in Amman

After spending a few days in Amman, Mona left for the West Bank

Mona arrived in Amman on Tuesday, April 11.  After spending two days in Amman, Mona later left for the West Bank and is expected to return to Amman on Sunday, April 16, 2000.  Mona's made her last visit to the region between October 2 and October 21, 1999.

Dana and Tarek Leave...Reem Returns Sunday

     

After spending almost one week in Amman, Dana left for Cairo on Tuesday, April 11.  Tarek also returned to Edinburgh on Wednesday, April 12, after recruiting a number of Jordanian students to work in the United States this summer.  On the other hand, Reem, who is currently in London, is expected to return to Amman on Sunday, April 16, 2000.

Kareem Wins 1st Place in Another Tournament

In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Kareem said that he won first place in a 400 M marathon which was held in Amman on Thursday, April 13, 2000.  Kareem also one a 100 M and a 400 M relay race on the same day.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Kareem on his latest winnings and wish him all the best.  What should be mentioned is that Kareem had won 1st place in another tournament which was held last month in Amman.

Zureikats to Celebrate 5 Special Occasions in the Next Two Weeks

Zureikats will be celebrating 5 special occasions in the next two weeks.  First, the family will be celebrating Yacoub S.'s birthday on April 18, then Abla N.'s birthday on April 20, then Abla Z.'s 50th birthday on April 23, and of course the 1st anniversary of The Zureikat Weekly on the same day, and finally, Mona's birthday on April 25, 2000.
