Zureikats Celebrate Basil's 50th Birthday

Basil and his family went out for dinner celebrating this occasion

Zureikats celebrated Basil's 50th birthday on Sunday, February 12, 2000.  Celebrating this occasion, Basil, Abla Z., Bashar, Bana, Omar, and Yacoub S. went out for dinner on Sunday night.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Basil a very happy birthday and all the best.

 

Zureikats Also Celebrated Two Other Birthdays

 

Besides Basil's birthday, Zureikats also celebrated Omar's birthday on February 7 and will also be celebrating Alia's birthday on February 14, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Omar and Alia a happy birthday and all the best.

Amal Leaves for London Next Thursday

Amal will be attending a fashion show directed by Reem

 

In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Amal confirmed that she will leave for London on Thursday, February 17, to attend a fashion show directed by Reem.  The fashion show will be held on Friday, February 18, 2000.  Amal also said that her trip to London will be 4-5 days long.

Hala Promoted to Grade 2

From Al-Ra'i Newspaper

Hala, Director of Channel 2 in Jordan Television, was promoted to Grade 2 (the second highest grade in the government).  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Hala on this promotion.

Reema updates us on the latest with Alia

"Alia is Shooting a Film on Accessibility on Campus"

Alia took an A* on a presentation about King Abdullah II's latest sudden visits

In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Reema updated us on the latest news with Alia, who is currently studying at Royal Holloway in London.  "Alia," Reema said, "is shooting a film on accessibility on campus for the committee of the handicapped.  The film is four hours long."  Reema also said that Alia did an oral presentation in Spanish on "the phenomena of King Abdullah II being in disguise" in several sudden visits he made to different sectors in the Kingdom.  Alia, who took an A* in her presentation, started it with The Zureikat Weekly's Weekly Cartoon about this issue.

Zureikats Around the World Score Excellent Results

   

Most of the results for the first term for Zureikat students came out in the last two weeks.  Dana, who is studying in Egypt, scored a 4.0 average, Bana got 5 A's and 1 B+, Bashar got 4 A's and 2 B+'s, and Hazem got a 97% average.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Zureikat students on these excellent results.

Reema Delivers a Lecture About "The Interactions and the Relationships of Persons with Disabilities with Animals"

On Sunday, February 6, 2000, Reema delivered a lecture run by the International Society for the Protection of Animals in cooperation with the Jordanian Society for the Protection of Animals and the Ministry of Education in Jordan.  Reema's lecture was about "The Interactions and the Relationships of Persons with Disabilities with Animals."  In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Reema said, "It was a quite successful lecture.  I'm getting a lot of support on the spot."

Talal Visits France Next Month

"I will be traveling with a group of 20"

In a short message to The Zureikat Weekly, Talal said that he will be leaving for France in March for his "Project Week".  Talal also said that he will be traveling with a group of 20 students.  Details in the coming issues.
