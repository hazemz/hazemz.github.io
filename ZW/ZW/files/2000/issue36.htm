Zureikats Enter 

the 21st Century

The Zureikat Weekly starts the millennium with a new look and new services

Every Zureikat celebrated the New Year in his own way

Zureikats all around the world celebrated the year 2000, the 21st century, and the beginning of the 3rd millennium.  Each Zureikat celebrated the new millennium in his/her own way.  Harran, Reema, Marwan, Abla, Amal, Ziad, and their friends celebrated the New Year at Marwan's restaurant, Houston's.  Other Zureikats spent the New Year at home or with their friends.  Helene and her friend, Louise, who were in Amman on New Year's Eve, celebrated the New Year with Amer at his friend's house.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Zureikats a very happy new millennium.

A picture of Helene wearing the traditional Jordanian "Hatta" on the eve of her departure

Helene and Kristiansson Leave Amman after Celebrating the New Millennium

  

After a one-week visit to Jordan, Helene and her friend, Louise Kristiansson, left Amman on Tuesday, January 4, 2000, at 3:30 AM.  During their stay, Helene and Louise visited Mt. Nebo in Madaba, the Jordan Valley, the Dead Sea, Umm Qais, Petra, and other touristic sites in Jordan.  The two guests also had Mansaf at Hala's on January 1 and at Marwan's on January 2, 2000.  On the eve of their departure, many family members visited Abu Harran's house to say goodbye.

Reem and Amer Return to the UK

Dana, Tarek, Alia, and Talal leave next week

  

After spending their mid-year holiday in Amman, Reem and Amer left for the United Kingdom.  Reem left on January 3, while Amer returned on January 4, 2000.  Dana is expected to return to Egypt on January 15, Tarek on January 9, Alia on January 9 or 12, and Talal on January 7, 2000.
