Christmas 2000 Draws Nearer as Zureikats Return from Abroad

Talal returns home...others to return this week

Source: No changes in Christmas Eve and Christmas Day celebrations this year

It's that time of year again when Zureikat students return home to celebrate Christmas and when Zureikat houses are filled with Christmas trees and decorations.  Talal was the first to return home on Saturday, December 9.  Next will be Tarek on Thursday, December 14, then Alia on Friday, December 15, then Amer on Saturday, December 16, then Reem on Sunday, December 17, then finally Samer on Thursday, December 21, 2000.  Regarding the Christmas Eve and Christmas Day celebrations, a source told The Zureikat Weekly that there will be no changes this year.  The Christmas Eve celebration will be held at Harran's house, and the Christmas Day celebration will be held at Marwan's.

 Zureikats Celebrate Two Birthdays

  

Zureikats celebrated Tarek's birthday on Friday, December 8.  The family will also be celebrating Salem's birthday on Monday, December 11, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Tarek and Salem very happy birthdays and all the best.

Reem Sends an E-mail from Italy During her Visit

While visiting Talal in Italy, Reem sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly on Sunday, December 3, 2000.  In her e-mail, Reem said, "I am with Talal in Italy.  We just had a very nice meal.  Tomorrow, we are going to Venice...I did the choreography for the med school revue and was in charge of the fashion show and Grease auditions.  It was also my end of year dinner as year representative, so I was given an award, which was really cool."
