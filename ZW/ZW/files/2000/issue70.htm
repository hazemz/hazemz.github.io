70th Issue

September 3, 2000

Zureikats Back to School...Sunday

Ayman: 12th, Hazem: 11th, Kareem: 10th, Sabine: 7th, Dalia: 6th, Nadine: 5th, Yacoub: 5th, Deena: 4th, Yacoub: 3rd, Salem: 1st

The 2000-2001 academic year is finally upon us, and Zureikat students in Jordan have complete their final preparations for the new year.  Hazem (11th grade), Dalia (6th grade), Yacoub (5th grade), Deena (4th grade), and Salem (1st grade), who study at Amman National School, Sabine (7th grade), Nadine (5th grade), and Yacoub Z. (3rd grade), who study at the Modern School, and Ayman (12th grade) and Kareem (10th grade), who study at the Amman Baccalaureate School, will all start on Sunday, September 3.  Abla Z., the Head of the English Department at Amman National School, has already started work on Sunday, August 27, 2000.  What should be mentioned is that Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z. have not yet returned from their trip to Syria.  They are expected to start school immediately after their arrival.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish all Zureikat students in Jordan, and especially Ayman who will be starting his last year, all the best.

Harran Returns from Amsterdam

After attending a conference in Amsterdam, Harran returned home on Monday, August 28.  Harran had earlier left for the Netherlands on Friday, August 25, 2000.

No more tuna fish, sardines, and eggs

Hassan Leaves to Syria...Returns with Family Soon

Hassan left for Syria on Thursday, August 31, 2000.  Sources said that Hassan is expected to return with his family soon.  Dian, Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z. are currently on a visit to Syria.

Training Course Ends Monday...Bana to Visit Sweden

Bana, who is currently attending a training course in Denmark, is expected to visit Sweden after the training course ends on Monday, September 4, 2000.  Details in the next issue.

Alia and Reema to Leave September 9 or 10

Alia and Reema will visit Hemel Hempstead before going to London

  

Alia and Reema are expected to leave town on Saturday or Sunday, September 9 or 10, 2000.  Before leaving for London, they will first go to Hemel Hempstead (near London), where Alia will be doing some exercises.  More details in the next issue.

  • Cyber Kids - Amman completed its preparations for the new academic year, opening three new computer labs in two schools in Amman.  Teachers from Cyber Kids will be working in these labs starting Sunday.
