Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

46th Issue

March 19, 2000

Breaking News:

Tarek Makes a Surprising Return Home

Tarek made a surprising return home on Sunday (March 19) night.  Tarek, who was expected to make a ten-day, mountain climbing trip to Morocco on Saturday, March 18, and return to Amman on March 29, canceled his trip for unknown reasons.  More details on this story soon.

Bana Starts a Ten-Day Visit to the United Kingdom

Bana and Alia will be returning home together on March 24

Almost two years after her return from the United World Colleges in Wales, Bana started a ten-day visit to the United Kingdom on Tuesday, March 14.  During her visit, Bana is expected to visit her friends from the UWC and some Zureikats in the UK.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly shortly after contacting Alia, Reema said, "Alia informed me that she received Bana in London.  She stayed with her one night and then went off to see her friends.  Bana will be staying again with Alia next week."  Bana and Alia are expected to return home together on Friday, March 24, 2000.  More details on Bana's trip after her return.

Marwan and Family Spend Three Days in the Jordan Valley

     

Marwan, Abla, and Hazem spent three days (Wednesday, March 15 - Friday, March 17) at their farm in the Jordan Valley as part of the five-day Muslim Eid Al-Adha holiday which started on Wednesday, March 15, 2000.  During their stay, Harran visited the farm, and Dr. Yousef Nusseir, Abla N.'s sister, and his family spent two days there.

 Amal, Ziad, Samer, and Ayman Return from Egypt on Sunday

Amal, Ziad, Samer, and Ayman spent the four days of Eid Al-Adha (Thursday, March 16 - Sunday, March 19) in the Sharm Al-Sheikh Resort in Egypt.  They are all expected back in Amman on Sunday night.

Talal Returns to Italy after Spending his Project Week in France

"The trip was extremely great, we had the chance to visit the wonderful sites in France"

After spending his project week in France, Talal returned to Italy on Sunday, March 12, 2000.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly after his return, Talal said, "The trip was extremely great, we really had the chance to visit the wonderful sites in France, such as the Louvre and Orsay museums and Versailles Castle.  We also visited lots of other places and had the whole week to relax."

In a mass held by Pope John Paul II in Amman

Yacoub Takes his First Communion on Tuesday

Yacoub S. will be taking his first communion on Tuesday, March 21, 2000, in a large mass held in Amman by Pope John Paul II on the second day of his visit to Jordan.

Harran Visits Turkey Next Friday

In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Reema said that Harran will be leaving to Turkey to attend a conference on Friday, March 24, 2000.
