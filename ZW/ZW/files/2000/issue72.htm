Breaking News:

Amer Arrives in Amman..Saturday

Edella told The Zureikat Weekly that Amer will be arriving in Amman on Saturday (Sep. 16) night to spend a few days with Harran and Kareem.  Details in the next issue.

Alia and Reema Leave to Hemel

Reema is expected to return home before the end of this month

 

After a series of flight cancellations, Alia and Reema left to Hemel Hempstead (near London) on Tuesday, September 12.  After Alia finishes her physiotherapy there, she and Reema will both go to London, and Alia will start her last year at Royal Holloway on September 22, 2000.  Reema will then return home a few days later.

Hala in Rabat to Attend Euro-Mediterranean Meeting

Hala is expected to return home on Monday, September 18

Hala left for Rabat, Morocco to attend a Euro-Mediterranean meeting there between Tuesday, September 12, and Monday, September 18, 2000.

Bana Returns Home after Visiting Denmark, Sweden

Bana returned home on Saturday, September 16, after visiting Denmark and Sweden.  Bana had left Amman on August 14, 2000 to attend a training course in Denmark for three weeks and make a social visit to Sweden.

Amal and Reem in Hawaii

Reem: We just spoke to Samer, and he is settling in and is doing fine

...Give our love to everyone!!  Especially Jiddo.

  

Reem sent the following e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly on Monday, September 11, 2000:

"I spent two weeks in New York with Sang, where we saw a very famous off Broadway show called "Blue Man Group".  It was very intelligent and witty, and we visited museums (Metropolitan and MOMA).  Sang and I then met my mom and Samer in New Orleans, where we spent a couple of days there touring the area.  It was very hot, around 53 degrees.  Now (Sep. 11), my mom and I are in Houston; we flew back on the third.  Today, in celebration of Belinda's birthday, we attended a Victorian fashion show in League City, a hundred miles out of Houston.  It was very informative.  Mom is leaving to Tucson tomorrow (Sep. 12) to spend a few days with Dr. Becker working in his clinic.  I will be here in Houston working in the VA hospital with Dr. Graham.  I will be meeting my mom on Friday (Sep. 15) in LA airport inshallah to fly to Hawaii...We just spoke to Samer, and he is settling in and is doing fine...Give our love to everyone!!  Especially Jiddo."

Bashar Hits 3-Year Record...Makes $650 in One Day

Bashar is now a member of the Presidency Club

Bashar, who is currently working in Tarek's "Al-Nashama" team with the Southwestern Company in the US, hit a three-year record for first-year members by making $650 in one day.  Therefore, Bashar is now a member of the Presidency Club, which Tarek joined a few weeks ago after making $2700 in one week.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Bashar on this accomplishment and wish him all the best.  What should be mentioned is that Tarek and Bashar, who are expected to return home by the end of the month, are most likely to qualify to attend the Presidency Club Dinner.
