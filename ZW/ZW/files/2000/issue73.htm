Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

73rd Issue

September 24, 2000

Marwan Leaves Hospital...

Everything is OK

Marwan returned home on Friday, September 22, after spending two days at the Amman Surgical Hospital.  Marwan had been hospitalized on Thursday, September 21, 2000, for a bleeding ulcer in his stomach.  Friday's tests showed that everything was ok and that Marwan could leave the hospital.  Family and friends gathered at Marwan's after his return home.

Hazem...President of the Student Council at Amman National School

Hazem was the only candidate for the presidency

Being the only candidate for the presidency, Hazem was named President of Amman National School's Student Council on Thursday, September 21.  Hazem, who is currently in 11th grade at ANS, was Vice President at the Student Council last year.  Elections for Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, and Committee Coordinator will be held on Thursday, September 28, 2000.  What should be mentioned is that Tarek was also a two-year member at the Student Council of ANS, a Committee Coordinator in the first year and a President in the second.  Bana was also a Vice President in the Student Council in her last year at ANS.

Hala Returns from Rabat...Hassan from Cairo

  

Hala returned from Rabat, Morocco on Monday, September 18, 2000 after attending a Euro-Mediterranean meeting there.  Hassan also returned last week from a business trip in Cairo, Egypt.

Amal and Reem Visit Hawaii, Maui Islands

  

Amal and Reem visited Hawaii and are currently in the Maui Islands.  Reem sent two e-mails last week, one from Hawaii and the other from Maui.

Reem's e-mail from Hawaii - September 17, 2000:

"Wakiikiki ukkiu huki mai tai, lava flow kuia kaaka kukuk ikiki maki kusumku kikil...This is what we hear all day long in Hawaii.  We took the trolley tour today and went all around Waikiki, Honolulu, Moano, and others.  Just add kiki to anything it becomes Hawaiian.  Halla tal3een nakul sushi!!"

Reem's e-mail from Maui - September 23, 2000:

"Aloha!!  Well, we are now in Maui, in the middle of the Pacific Ocean.  It is so pretty and exotic here.  I have been surfing and snorkeling, and last night, we had the most delicious meal, lobster, at Longhi's.  We toasted you all.  (maybe we should all come here for a Zureikat reunion!)"

Amal is expected to return home by the end of next week after Reem returns to Londonkiki.

Tarek and Bashar Return This Week

After spending three months in the US, Tarek and Bashar are expected to return home this week.  Details in the 74th issue.
