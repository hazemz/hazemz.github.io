Celebrating his birthday

Zureikats Gather at Marwan's

Zureikats celebrated Marwan's birthday on Saturday, December 2, 2000.  On this occasion, the family gathered at Marwan's on Saturday evening.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Marwan a very happy birthday and all the best.

Zureikat Students Abroad Prepare to Return Home

Talal will be the first to arrive...on December 9

              

Zureikat students abroad are preparing to return home for their mid-year holiday.  The first to return will be Talal.  He will be returning from Italy on December 9.  Tarek will then return on December 14, Alia on December 15, Amer on December 16, Reem, who is currently visiting Talal in Italy, on December 17, and finally Samer on December 20, 2000.

Sources: Dana to be Appointed in JTV Soon

Sources told The Zureikat Weekly that Dana may be appointed in Jordan Television within the next few days.  Dana, who has completed an MA degree in TV Journalism and a three-month internship program with Showtime, has already started working at JTV last week.

  • Abla Z. has already decorated her house for Christmas.  Other Zureikats are expected to do so in the next two weeks.

  • Amal, Ziad, and Ayman broke into their own house on Sunday, November 26, 2000, after Ziad forgot the house key inside.  After hours of staying outside, they finally broke one of the doors and went in.
