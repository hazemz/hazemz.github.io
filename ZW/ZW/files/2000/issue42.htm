Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

42nd Issue

February 20, 2000

3rd Zureikat Applies for the UWC Scholarship

Hazem submitted his application form on Thursday, February 17

On Thursday, February 17, Hazem submitted his application form to attend one of the seven United World Colleges next year to complete his IB diploma. Hazem will sit for a series of exams in the coming weeks.  Then, 20 will be chosen for the interview.  After the interview, seven from Jordan will get the scholarship.  What should be mentioned is that Bana was previously granted the same scholarship four years ago to attend the UWC of the Atlantic in Wales, and Talal is currently in his first year at the UWC of the Adriatic in Italy.

JCE Upgraded to Class A

Marwan: This puts JCE amongst the top three consulting firms in Jordan

  

In a brief interview with The Zureikat Weekly, Marwan, chairman of Jordanian Consulting Engineers Co., said, "Jordanian Consulting Engineers was upgraded to Class A Consultant in all engineering fields in Jordan.  This puts JCE amongst the top three consulting firms in Jordan."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Marwan on this achievement.

Abla Appears on TV in a Special Report on Cyber Kids

  

Abla N. appeared on JTV in its Friday morning show on Friday, February 18, 2000.  In a recorded statement, Abla explained about Cyber Kids, her center, and new methods of teaching through the computer.  JTV also made a live interview with a teacher at Cyber Kids and two students.

Amal Leaves for London to Attend Reem's Fashion Show

Amal left Amman on Thursday, February 17, to attend Reem's fashion show, which was held on Friday, February 18, 2000.  More details on Amal's return and on Reem's fashion show next week.

Tarek Organizes Two Activities in Edinburgh's Arab Society

Tarek, who is currently the president of the Arab Society in Edinburgh, had organized a gathering on the occasion of the passing away of HM King Hussein on February 8, 2000.  A speaker delivered a speech on this occasion.  Tarek is now arranging for another lecture to be delivered discussing the continuation of the sanctions against Iraq.

Talal Updates us on his Latest Skiing Activities

"I had a ski competition last Sunday, and there is also another major one... this weekend"

Talal send an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly last week.  In his e-mail, which included his latest news, Talal said, "I had a ski competition last Sunday, 15 km, and there is also another major one, which is this weekend.  It is about 28 km; it is really fun, and we enjoy it a lot.  The last one was really difficult, especially if you forgot your water in the van and had to ski for three hours with nothing to drink!"
