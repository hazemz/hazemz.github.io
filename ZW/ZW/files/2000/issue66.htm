Editor in Chief: The calendar will be launched in September

As you can see, two new features have been added to The Zureikat Weekly in August.  Here is a brief explanation on each one:

Zureikat Chat:

Now, with "Zureikat Chat", you can chat with anyone reading The Zureikat Weekly at the same time you are.  Just click on "Chat" when you enter the site, and continue reading the latest issue as usual.  Anyone else who clicks on "Chat" will see you online, and you can start chatting.  We are also planning to set a certain time every Sunday so Zureikats can all meet in The Zureikat Weekly's chat room and chat.  Please choose the time that best suites you and send us your suggestion.

Pic of the Week:

The new "Pic of the Week" section is found in every issue right under "Quickies".  It will include a picture from The Zureikat Weekly's archive.

The Zureikat Calendar is expected to be launched in September, 2000.  Please send us all important dates in your family (birthdays, anniversaries, etc) as soon as possible.

More services to be launched within the next few months: Zureikat Address Book

Enjoy the new services...and we welcome your suggestions anytime

Ayman Returns Home...Sunday

After spending one month in a summer camp in England, Ayman will be returning home this Sunday, August 6, 2000.

Zureikats Celebrate Two Birthdays

Dana returns August 12...Samer and Amal leave for the US August 19

  

Zureikats celebrated Dana's birthday on August 1.  Dana, who is currently in Egypt, will be returning home on August 12.  Zureikats will also be celebrating Samer's birthday on Sunday, August 6.  Samer, who graduated last May, will be studying in the US.  He, along with Amal, is expected to leave for the US on August 19, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Dana and Samer all the best in the future.
