Three years after her tragic death

Zureikats Remember Umm Harran

Exactly three years have passed since the passing away of Umm Harran on Wednesday, July 30, 1997.  In remembrance of the late Umm Harran, some Zureikats may be visiting the Umm Harran Gazebo, which was built in Ajlun in 1998.  The family had visited the gazebo on July 30, 1998 and 1999.  On this occasion, Mona sent the following e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly:

"To Umm Harran,
It still hurts that you are not there on the other end,
Your memory will last forever and ever,
Because you are the breeze of my summer wind!
Your daughter,
Mona"

Marwan also sent the following message to The Zureikat Weekly:

"Twenty years ago, I was attending a lecture about stress and relaxation.  The lecturer asked each one of the attendants to lie down and try and recall moments in his life where he enjoyed complete peace of mind and relaxation.  The idea was that if one wants to relax, one of the best ways would be to try and carry himself back in time to a place where he generally feels comfortable, relaxed, happy, content, satisfied, and has a peace of mind.  For me, that place was always sitting in the veranda of my mother's house opposite my mother and enjoying the love and affection that she always kept around her wherever she went and whatever she did."

"I never took my mother to be a "great" person.  For me, she was a simple, loving mother whose only mission in life is to take away from herself and give to her children and family.  Only after her death did I realize that my mother was truly a "great" person.  It is now after her death that we all realize how much she meant to each one of us and how much she affected our lives.  The Zureikat Weekly is only but one testimony to the greatness of Umm Harran.  It carries with it the message that Umm Harran lived for all her life.  "Nothing in life equals a happy, loving, and united family"." 

Showtime internship kicks off in Beirut

Dana in Cairo after Visiting Beirut for 2 Days

Dana will return to Amman on August 12 and leave to London on August 14

After visiting Beirut between July 27 and July 29, Dana arrived in Cairo on Saturday, July 29.  Dana is currently an intern with Showtime Radio & Television Network.  She had earlier won the internship award as an outstanding AUC graduate.  The internship, which includes airfare and accommodation, will officially start in London in mid-August.  Dana is expected to return to Amman on August 12 and leave for London on August 14, 2000.  She will probably return home from London in November.

Yet another award for a Zureikat...

Bana Leaves for Denmark...August 13

After the training course in Denmark, Bana will be making a social visit to Sweden

Bana won an award to attend a training course for Arab youth leaders entitled "Youth Empowerment and Democracy" between August 13 and September 4, 2000.  After the course, Bana will leave for Sweden to visit the Swedish Zureikats there.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Bana on this award and wish her all the best in the future.
