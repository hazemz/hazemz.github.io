Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

47th Issue

March 26, 2000

After spending two days in the hospital

Abla Returns Home

After spending two days in the hospital, Abla N. returned home on March 22, 2000.  During her stay there, Abla underwent Gastroscopy and Colonoscopy.  The tests showed excellent results.  Abla, who was admitted to the Amman Surgical Hospital on March 20, returned to her usual work on Saturday, March 25, 2000.

Alia and Bana Return from the UK

Bana left Amman ten days ago to visit Alia, Reem,  and other friends in the UK

  

Alia and Bana returned home on Friday, March 24, 2000.  Family members visited Harran's house on Friday evening to welcome back Alia and Bana.  Bana, who left Amman on March 14 to visit Alia, Reem, and her friends in the UK, said in a telephone call with The Zureikat Weekly, ""I went to different museums, exhibitions, and galleries.  I had a lovely time seeing my friends again.  It was like we were never apart."  In the phone call, Bana also said that she visited Reem for one day and spent two nights with Alia.  On the other hand, Reem is expected to return to Amman in mid April.

Zureikats Celebrate Mother's Day

         

Zureikats around the world celebrated Mother's Day on March 21, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Zureikat mothers all the best on this special occasion.

In the second day of the Papal visit to Jordan

Yacoub Takes his First Communion

Yacoub S. took his first communion from cardinals accompanying Pope John Paul II in his visit to Jordan.  Yacoub took his communion in a large mass held by the Pope.  The mass was attended by tens of thousands of Muslims and Christians, including Abla Z., Basil, and Kareem, who was there with Christian students in his class.

Hassan Visits Canada

Hassan left for Canada on a business trip on Monday, March 20, 2000.  Hassan is expected to return sometime this week.

Mona to Visit Jordan April 11

A source told The Zureikat Weekly that Mona is expected to arrive in Amman on her first visit for this year on April 11, 2000.

Harran Cancels his Trip to Turkey Due to "Poor Service" from RJ

Harran cancelled his trip to Turkey which was due on Friday, March 24, 2000.  In a brief message to The Zureikat Weekly, Harran said, "I cancelled my trip due to poor service from Royal Jordanian!"

Basil Attends IT Forum in the Dead Sea

Basil attended the Jordan IT (Information Technology) Forum in the Movenpick Resort in the Dead Sea.  The forum was attended by King Abdullah II and representatives from 150 local and international companies.  Other Zureikat relatives and friends who attended were Dr. Yousef Nusseir, president of the National Information Center, and Dr. Shabeeb Ammari, Chairman of the Board of Jordan Telecommunications Company.

Amal and Family Return Home

Amal, Ziad, Samer, and Ayman returned home after spending four days in the Sharm Al-Sheikh Resort in Egypt.

Zureikats Celebrate Kareem's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Kareem's birthday on March 25, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Kareem a very happy birthday.
