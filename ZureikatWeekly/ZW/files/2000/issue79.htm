Tarek Organizes Activities in Support of Palestine

"I was at the main street in Edinburgh with several activists"

"I do believe we can make a difference here in Edinburgh"

Tarek is currently heading a society which is organizing several activities in support of the Palestinians in their Intifada.  "I'm heading a society which is organizing several activities in support of Palestine," he said in an e-mail sent to The Zureikat Weekly.  "Yesterday (Oct. 29), I was at the main street in Edinburgh with several activists, not Arabs, and we were stopping random people on the street explaining the situation and then having them sign a petition to the Scottish Parliament to take a stand against Israel.  This wasn't my thing but I was asked to help, and I did.  I have to say we were quite successful; many people signed...I do believe we can make a difference here in Edinburgh."
