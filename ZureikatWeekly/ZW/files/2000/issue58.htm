Zureikats to Visit Cairo this Week

Marwan, Abla, Hazem, Reema, and Alia will attend Dana's graduation

Marwan, Abla N., Dana, and Hazem are expected to leave for Cairo on Sunday, June 11, to attend Dana's awards dinner on June 13 and her graduation from the American University in Cairo after earning a Masters degree on June 14.  Reema and Alia are also expected to attend the graduation; they will be leaving Amman on Tuesday, June 13 and returning on Thursday, June 15.  Marwan, Abla N., Dana, and Hazem will probably return home on Friday, June 16, 2000.  A source had earlier told The Zureikat Weekly that Amal may not be able to attend Dana's graduation.

Omar Starts his Tawjihi Finals...Thursday

Omar's graduation will be held on July 10

Omar, who is currently in his last year in school, will start the government's "Tawjihi" finals on Thursday, June 15.  Omar will finish his exams on June 5 and will graduate from Amman National School on July 10, 2000.

Tarek Leaves for the US on June 15

Tarek may be accompanied by Bashar

  

Accompanied by a number of Jordanian and foreign students which may include Bashar, Tarek is expected to leave for the US on June 15 to start his third year with the Southwestern Company.  Tarek and his team will be working in Virginia this year.  Tarek is expected to make a brief visit to Jordan on September 29, 2000. 

Alia Returns Home...Sunday

Alia is expected to return home for her summer holiday on Sunday, June 11.  During her stay, Alia is expected to visit Egypt between June 13 and June 15, 2000 to attend Dana's graduation there.

Salem Gets a Cup in Little League Closing Ceremony

 

Left: Salem holding his cup \ Right: Salem trying out for the September soccer season

 

Left: Salem with his coaches and team mom \ Right: Salem standing beside his teammates

Salem, who was been playing baseball in the Amman Little League since March, got a cup in the ceremony which was held on Friday, June 9, 2000, to close the baseball season for this year.  The ceremony was attended by Hala, Naief, Dalia, Deena, and the Editor in Chief.  What should be mentioned is that Salem had participated earlier this week in the KG party at Amman National School.  Salem, who studies at ANS, will become in 1st grade this September.

Zureikat Students in Amman Celebrate

the Start of the Summer Holiday 2000

ANS, MS, and UJ students already finished...ABS finishes next week

               

The summer holiday for the year 2000 is now upon us.  Bashar and Bana, who study at the University of Jordan, Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z., who study at the Modern School, and Hazem, Yacoub S., Dalia, Deena, and Salem, who study at the Amman National School, all started their summer holiday last week.  Ayman and Kareem, who study at the Amman Baccalaureate School, will start their holiday next week.
