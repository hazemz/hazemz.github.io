After 20 days of final exams

Tawjihi Fear Ends,

Omar Graduates...Monday

     

Left: Amer's graduation on May 26 \ Middle: Samer's graduation on May 31 \ Right: Dana's graduation on June 14 

Omar has finally completed Tawjihi, the Jordanian General Certificate of Secondary Education.  Twenty days after he started his finals on June 15, Omar finished his exams on July 5.  Now, Zureikats are preparing for Omar's graduation from Amman National School which will be held in the school on Monday, July 10, 2000.  It is not yet clear who exactly will attend Omar's graduation.  Omar's graduation is the fourth and last in a series of Zureikat graduations for the year 2000.  The first was Amer's on May 26; the second was Samer's on May 31, and the third was Dana's on June 14.

Just two days after celebrating Harran's birthday on July 1

Zureikats Celebrate 3 Occasions

  

  

After celebrating Harran's birthday on July 1, Zureikats celebrated three other occasions on July 3 and 4, 2000, Abla Z. and Basil's anniversary and Amal's birthday on July 3 and Marwan and Abla N.'s anniversary on July 4.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish the Ablas, Basil, Marwan, and Amal all the best.

After being delayed at Heathrow and losing his luggage

Ayman Starts his Summer Camp in London

Ayman left for London to start a one-month summer camp there on Sunday, July 2.  After being delayed at Heathrow Airport in London and finding his lost luggage, Ayman finally started his summer camp.  Ayman is expected to return home on August 5 or 6, 2000.

  • With the help of Basil, Talal, Hazem, and Kareem, Omar successfully completed three days of shopping for his graduation and prom night.

  • Zureikat pools are busy these days due to the heat wave affecting Jordan.  Temperatures in Amman have exceeded 40 C in the last two days.
