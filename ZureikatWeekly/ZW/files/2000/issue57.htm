Samer Graduates from the

Amman Baccalaureate School

The May 31 graduation was attended by Amal, Ziad, Reem, Samer, Ayman, Talal, Abu Harran, Abla Z., Hala, and Abla N.

Photographs were taken by Abla N.

 

 

Along with 83 other students, Samer graduated from the Amman Baccalaureate School (ABS) on Wednesday, May 31, 2000.  The graduation was attended by Amal, Ziad, Reem, Samer, Ayman, Talal, Abu Harran, Abla Z., Hala, and Abla N..  Zureikats around the world called Samer to congratulate him on his graduation.  The Zureikat Weekly would also like to take this opportunity to congratulate Samer and wish him all the best in the future.

Extracts from what was Written about Amer in the Yearbook 2000 of the Royal College of Sergeants in Ireland:

A Jordanian pioneer at the RCSI, Amer will leave a lasting legacy for future Jordanians to aspire to.  From day one, Amer engineered his way among the elite in his class.  In his pre-medical year, Amer realized that his mental ability was so superior that one late night, tutorial with a needy student was sufficient to insure a smooth run through that student's years at the RCSI.  His contribution to RCSI was not just purely academic, however, he joined soccer training and took part proving his sportsmanlike nature.  In the fastest win ever at the intervarsity squash championships in Belfast (unfortunately it wasn't his).  Well-known to his friends for his obsession with toes and tattoos, he was frequently seen visiting Dublin's prestigious night clubs.  This gave him a premature knowledge of clinical signs and symptoms and sparked a new born interest in doctor-patient relationships as he was frequently practicing this with total strangers.
In his clinical years, his Jordanian ties were strengthened when he became a true ambassador for his country by frequently entertaining Jordanian visitors for long periods of stay.  With his Toyota Corolla and his good looks and charm in hand, he was often seen cruising around the Irish capital at odd hours of the day and night.  It has to be mentioned that Amer, during these last three years, tried soccer again and thought, but only thought, about regaining his tennis game.
On a final note, we are sure that this protective, proud, and idealistic Jordanian will lead wherever he lands.  He has made his mark par excellence and will surely be remembered for his sparkling personality, nicotine consumption, and his love for Guinness.
In ten years time, we see Amer: as a cardiology consultant back home in Jordan advising his patients to stop smoking with a pack of Marlboro's in his white coat.

Dana's Graduation to be Held in Cairo Next Week

Dana returns home to spend one week before her graduation

Source: Reema and Amal may attend the graduation

Dana will be graduating from the American University in Cairo on Wednesday, June 14, with a Masters degree in Mass Communications and TV Journalism.  Dana is expected to return home late Sunday night (June 4) to spend one week in Amman before her graduation.  Dana will then leave to Cairo with Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem on Sunday, June 11.  Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem will be attending Dana's awards dinner on June 12 and her graduation on June 14.  A source told The Zureikat Weekly that Amal and Reema may also attend the graduation in Cairo.

 

No Changes in The Zureikat Weekly

There won't be any changes regarding The Zureikat Weekly due to the Editor in Chief's trip to Cairo.  The 58th issue will be released before he leaves on June 11, and the 59th issue will be released on June 18 (its normal date).  The Editor in Chief is expected to return home on June 15 or 16, 2000.

Marwan Returns from Denver, Colorado on Monday

Marwan had earlier visited Dublin, Ireland and Hanover, Germany

Marwan, who attended Amer's graduation in Dublin on May 26 then left for two business trips in Germany and Denver, Colorado, is expected to return home early Monday morning (June 5).

Reem Makes a Surprising One-Week Visit

Reem came to Amman on May 28 and will leave on June 4

Reem, who is currently studying in London, made a surprising one-week visit to Amman to attend Samer's May 31 graduation.  Reem arrived in Amman on Sunday, May 28, and is expected to head back to London on Sunday, June 4, 2000.

Harran and Reema Return Home

Family and friends congratulate Harran and Reema on Amer's graduation

  

After attending Amer's graduation in Dublin and visiting Alia in London, Harran and Reema returned home on Wednesday, May 31, 2000.  Family and friends visited Harran and Reema after their return to congratulate them on Amer's graduation.

In the past month, Zureikats celebrated four birthdays.  First, there was Gero's birthday on May 11.  Then, there was Helene's on May 30, then Sabine's on May 31, and finally Sylvia's birthday on June 4, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish them all happy birthdays and all the best.
