Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

45th Issue

March 12, 2000

Hassan Visits Syria...Dian's Sister Arrives in Amman

Hassan, Dian, Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub visited Syria on Friday, March 3, and returned to Amman on Sunday, March 5.  Hassan also visited Syria by himself on Thursday, March 9, 2000, and returned on the same day.  On the other hand, Dian's sister, May, and her children arrived in Amman early last week.  Dian's other sister, Jumana, visited Amman late last year.

Zureikats Spend Friday in Umm Al-Amad

Abla N. announced the "opening" of their summer house in Umm Al-Amad, Jordan for this summer.  Abu Harran, Hala, Dalia, Deena, Salem, Dian, Sabine, Nadine, Yacoub, and Dian's visiting sister and her children spent Friday, March 10, there.

Dana Returns to Cairo

Dana returned to Cairo on Sunday, March 5, 2000, after a four-day visit to Jordan.  Dana is expected to make another similar visit early next month.

Zureikats Celebrate Yacoub's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Yacoub Z.'s birthday on March 8, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Yacoub a very happy birthday.  A source told The Zureikat Weekly that Yacoub is expected to have a party for his friends celebrating his birthday on Monday, March 13, 2000.

End of 45th Issue
