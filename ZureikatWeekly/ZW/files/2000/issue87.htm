Zureikats Celebrate Christmas

Dancing and singing filled Harran's house as Zureikats gathered there for Christmas Eve

Celebrating Christmas 2000, Zureikats gathered at Harran's on Christmas Eve and at Marwan's on Christmas Day.  The celebrations were filled with dancing and singing as Santa Claus visited the family and gave each Zureikat two gifts, one from him and the other from (Jiddo).  Guests in the Christmas Eve and Day celebrations included Umm Phillip, Dian's mother.

Post-Christmas Mansaf at Hala's!

Eight days after a pre-Christmas Mansaf was held at Marwan's for the Zureikat grandchildren, a similar post-Christmas Mansaf was held at Hala's on Saturday, December 30, 2000.  Besides the Zureikat grandchildren, Hassan and Dian and her mother attended.

Tarek Nominated for Student Excellence Award

Tarek was selected from a group of 700 students

Mukherjee: Well done, I am very proud of you

Tarek, a Student Manager and an Organization Leader at the Southwestern Company, was chosen among 700 students to be a nominee for the Student Excellence Award.  The award will be presented to the winner on January 6, 2001.  In an e-mail to Tarek informing him of his nomination, Robin Mukherjee, Tarek's manager, said, "Tarek, congratulations, out of the 700 students who work with DC each year, you have been nominated to be the Causer Group representative to be in the finals of the Student Excellence Award.  Well done, I am very proud of you...(The award) is presented at the Freddies on January 6, 2001...Good luck."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Tarek the best of luck in winning this award.
