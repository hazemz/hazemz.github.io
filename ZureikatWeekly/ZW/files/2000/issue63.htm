Omar Graduates from ANS

Zureikats gathered at Abla's after the graduation to celebrate

In his graduation speech, Omar thanked his parents and the school

Safarini: Omar is the Einstein of the 21st century and an excellent athlete

 

 

 

 

Left: Omar's graduation certificate \ Right: Omar's graduation cake

After completing the Jordanian General Certificate of Secondary Education (Tawjihi), Omar graduated from Amman National School on Monday, July 10, 2000.  Besides Basil, Abla Z., Bana, and Yacoub S., Abu Harran, Reema, Kareem, Marwan, Abla N., Hazem (as the Vice President of the student council), Dian, Amal, Samer, Talal, and Hala attended the graduation which was held in the school Monday afternoon.  Immediately after the graduation, Zureikats gathered at Abla Z.'s to celebrate this special occasion.  During the graduation ceremony, every student was given a certain time to speak.  In his graduation speech, Omar thanked his parents and the school for all that they have done.  (You can read Omar's graduation speech below).  Director of grades 5-9 in ANS, Fayza Safarini, presented each graduate with a few words before he\she gave his\her speech.  She presented Omar as "the Einstein of the 21st century", an excellent athlete, and a student with outstanding academic results.  Later that evening, after the gathering at Abla's, Basil, Abla Z., Bana, and Omar attended the graduation dinner at the Intercontinental Hotel - Amman.  Omar also attended the Prom on Tuesday, July 11.

Hassan in Tunisia...Hala to Visit Cyprus this Week

Hassan: I'll be taking a compass to help me if I get lost in the desert

  

Hassan left on a business trip to Tunisia on Wednesday, July 12, for a few days.  Two days before his departure, Hassan told The Zureikat Weekly that he'll be going to the Tunisian Desert during his visit.  "I'll be taking a compass to help me if I get lost in the desert!"  On the other hand, Hala will also be leaving to Cyprus to attend a conference there between Wednesday, July 19, and Sunday, July 23, 2000.

Tarek and Bashar to Return Home September 28

   

In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Tarek and Bashar, who are currently working for the Southwestern Company in Virginia, USA, said that everything was okay with them, and that they'll be returning home on Thursday, September 28, 2000.

Zureikats Celebrate Nadine's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Nadine's birthday on Tuesday, July 11, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Nadine a very happy birthday and all the best.
