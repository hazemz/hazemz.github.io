54th Issue

May 14, 2000

Dr. Amer Zureikat

Graduates on May 26

Breaking News: Amer to start a 1-year internship program next July

Amer completed his doctorate degree with honors becoming Dr. Amer Zureikat.  Zureikats around the world called Amer to congratulate him on his great achievement.  The Zureikat Weekly would also like to take this opportunity to congratulate Amer and wish him all the best in the future.  On this occasion, Amer called the Editor in Chief a few minutes before this issue was supposed to be released.  In this exclusive phone call, Amer said:

"I just called to congratulate myself and my family on achieving my medical doctorate degree.  I would also, at this time, like to express my gratitude to my parents for the overwhelming support and invaluable advice that they have given me throughout my college years.  They have been a source of inspiration, and words cannot express how much I am proud of them.  My gratitude also goes out to all members of the Zureikat family for their support over the past years, and a special thank you to the Editor in Chief for making my graduation an exception in that this is a breaking issue story.  I would like to invite everyone again to attend the ceremony on the 26th of May, and I hope that everyone will be able to make it."

In his phone call, Amer also said that he got the job that he wanted, a 1-year internship program in cardiology, endocrinology, general surgery, and brain surgery.  On the other hand, Amer's graduation, which will be held on Friday, May 26, 2000, will be attended by Harran, Reema, Alia, Marwan, and Reema's brother, Samir Ayoub.

Describing it as a "huge success"

Tarek Holds his Final Event at the Arabic Society

Tarek to work in Virginia this summer...returns home September 29

As President of the Arabic Society in Edinburgh, Scotland, Tarek held his final event for the society for the academic year 1999-2000.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Tarek said, "It was a huge success...The party was amazing.  At least 80 people came, and everyone had an amazing time.  Everyone danced Arabic!"  "I also stood up and made a small speech about where the money was going to and had a very small quiz with some prizes," he continued.  In his e-mail, Tarek also said, "With all the pressures of this year, this Arabic Society will be something I will always look back at with pride."  On the other hand, in a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Tarek said that he'll be working in Virginia this summer.  Tarek has previously worked for the Southwestern Company in the United States in the summers of 1998 and 1999.

Alia Returns to London

Alia returned to London on Tuesday, May 9, 2000, after spending her Easter holiday in Amman.  Alia is expected to join her parents on Amer's graduation in Dublin and return home in June.

Talal...the First to Return Home on May 22

Talal started his final exams on Wednesday, May 10, and will be finishing on Thursday, May 18, 2000.  In a short message to The Zureikat Weekly, Talal also said that he will be returning home for his summer holiday on Monday, May 22, 2000.
