38th Issue

January 21, 2000

Hassan Visits 3 Countries in One Week

Hassan returned from Syria and is currently in Cairo

Hassan is currently making three business trips to Syria, Egypt, and Germany.  He left for Syria on Monday, January 17, returned to Amman on Wednesday, January 19 at 11 AM, and left for Cairo at 5 PM on the same day.  Hassan is expected to return from Cairo on Friday, January 21, and leave for Hamburg, Germany on Monday, January 24, 2000 to spend 4-5 days there.

Reem Visits Dublin, Returns to London

Amal: Reem went to get some notes and books

Just two weeks after returning to London from Amman, Reem visited Amer in Dublin on Friday, January 14 and returned on Sunday, January 16, 2000.  "Reem went to Dublin this afternoon to visit Amer for a long weekend before the 2nd term starts to get some notes and books," said Amal in a visit to Marwan's house last week, "She will be returning to London on January 16, 2000."

Zureikat ANSers Start Their Mid-Year Holiday

Samer, who studies at ABS, will finish his exams on the 26th

  

Omar, Hazem, and Dalia, who study at Amman National School, started their mid-year holiday on Thursday, January 20, 2000.  Dalia finished school on Wednesday, January 19, while Omar and Hazem finished on January 20.  Yacoub S. and Deena, who don't sit for any final exams finished school on Wednesday, January 19, 2000.  Amman National School will start its second term on January 31, 2000.  Samer, on the other hand, who is studying at the Amman Baccalaureate School, will finish his final exams on Wednesday, January 26, 2000.

Dana Returns to Cairo

Dana returned to Cairo to start her final term in the two-year Masters program she has been taking since 1998.  Dana left Amman on Monday, January 17, 2000.  She was the last one to leave after Amer, Tarek, Reem, Alia, and Talal left during the last two weeks.

  • Hassan Hosts the Zureikat Men on a Friday Lunch

  

Abu Harran, Harran, Reema, Kareem, Marwan, Abla N., Dana, and Hazem had lunch at Hassan's on Friday, January 14, 2000.  The lunch included Makloobeh, Freekeh, Fillet, and other items.

  • Amal: Talal is Taking Skiing Courses

Amal told The Zuriekat Weekly that Talal, who is currently in Trieste, Italy, started taking skiing courses last week.
