In a surprising four-day visit to Jordan

Dana Returns Home

In a surprising four-day visit to Jordan, Dana returned to Amman on Wednesday, March 1, and is expected to return to Cairo on Sunday, March 5, 2000.  Dana is also expected to visit Amman early next month.

Tarek Holds a Talk on Iraqi Sanctions

The talk was attended by 50 people, and it was "successful"

A poster designed by Tarek announcing the talk on the UN sanctions on Iraq

Click on the poster to see a larger version

As the president of the Arab Society in Edinburgh, Scotland, Tarek held a talk on the UN sanctions on Iraq on Wednesday, March 1, 2000.  In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Tarek said that the talk was very successful; it was attended by 50 people.  Tarek, who is currently studying at the University of Edinburgh, is expected to return to Amman on March 29 after making a week-long trip to Morocco later this month.

Reem's Fashion Show was ALSO "Successful"

"We raised over 2500 pounds in one night!!"

In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly almost two weeks after her fashion show, Reem said, "I had a hectic time with the fashion show, and it was a big success; we raised over 2500 pounds in one night!!  At the moment, I just finished choreographing, a musical we are doing called "Little Shop of Horrors", which we will perform this Thursday, Friday, and Saturday.  After that, I probably will just concentrate on my studies and do some medicine for a change!!"  In her e-mail, Reem also congratulated Marwan on the upgrading of JCE.

In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly

Helene: My Mother is Coming on the 28th of March to Visit Me

"This weekend I will be heading  to Verona...the city of Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet"

Helene told The Zureikat Weekly in an e-mail she sent last week that Mona will be visiting her on March 28.  "My mother is coming on the 28th of March to visit me," she said, "I really hope Auntie Amal, who, according to my mother, wants to visit Milan as well, will come too.  The two ladies can do some serious shopping in Milan, the fashion city of Europe."  In her e-mail, Helene also said, "This weekend, I will be heading  to Verona.  Verona is the city of Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet.  The balcony of Juliet is actually being exposed in Verona.  I saw the ballet of Romeo and Juliett at la Scala a couple of weeks ago and have been inspired to visit the city for some time now."  Helene also conveyed her friend, Louise's, regards to the family.  Louise visited Amman with Helene late last year.

Omar and Hazem Receive Certificates for Outstanding Results

Last week, Omar and Hazem, who study at Amman National School, received certificates for outstanding academic results in the first term.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Omar and Hazem on these certificates and wish them all the best.

Talal Visits France

Talal left for France on a project week on Friday, March 3, 2000.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Talal said, "I am so excited.  I will be staying at my friend's house, and I hope I will visit all the wonderful sites there."  More details on Talal's visit to France after his return.
