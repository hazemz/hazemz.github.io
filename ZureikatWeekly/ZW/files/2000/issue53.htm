In his first e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly

Amer Updates the Family on his Latest News

Amer congratulates the Editor in Chief on the 1st Anniversary

Amer's graduation to be held on May 26

"I would like to take the opportunity to extend an invitation to all Zureikats to join me in a Fiesta weekend in Dublin on May the 26th"

In his first e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Amer sent his congratulations to the Editor in Chief on the occasion of the 1st anniversary of The Zureikat Weekly, which was celebrated on April 23, 2000.  "What you have managed to put together in the past year is truly impressive," he said, "Your dedication to The Zureikat Weekly never ceases to amaze me, and I wish you all the success in your future editions."  In his e-mail, Amer, who's graduation will be held in Dublin on May 26, also said, "As you already know, my college years are over, and I would like to take the opportunity to extend an invitation to all Zureikats to join me in a Fiesta weekend in Dublin on May the 26th."  "I will also take this opportunity to remind everyone to take a couple of nights off and think carefully about my graduation gift(s)!!!" he continued, "May I also add that cash is also acceptable, and should anyone fail to come up with a good idea of a gift, my bank account number will be forwarded to The
Zureikat Weekly!!"

Bana...2nd in JU's Economics Honor List

Bana is the second in Jordan University's Economics Department's Honor List.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Bana on this accomplishment and wish her all the best in the years to come.

Mona Sends an E-mail after her Return Home

"Life has been very intensive since I came back"

Mona met with Queen Sylvia in a reception Friday afternoon

A few days after her return home, Mona sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly.  In her e-mail, Mona said, "Life has been very intensive since I came back.  Preparing books for the printers, answering lots of mail and trying to adapt again to the Swedish way of life.  The big news is that the spring is here now.  It goes on very quickly seeing all the green leaves, flowers, and birds.  My garden is just like a paradise."  In her e-mail, Mona also said that Dag made a one-day business trip to Germany last week.  "Today (Friday, May 5),  I am going to meet Queen Sylvia in a reception this afternoon," she continued, "There is a big opening for an international library in central Stockholm.  Very few guest are invited, and Dar Al-Muna is one of them.  It will be interesting to be there.  The whole month of May will be a lot of work.  It is the month of the year where every thing has to be done before midsummer, from social activities to any thing else.  Gero and I are invited tomorrow to the Dutch embassy for dinner, and today in the evening, I have some friends for a drink."
