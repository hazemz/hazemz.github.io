To attend a training course on "Youth Empowerment and Democracy"

Bana Leaves to Denmark...Monday

Bana will also visit Sweden for a few days in September

After winning an award to attend a training course for Arab youth leaders entitled "Youth Empowerment and Democracy" in Denmark, Bana will be leaving for Denmark on Monday, August 14.  After the training course ends on Monday, September 4, 2000, Bana will be visiting Mona and the family in Sweden.

Samer in Tulane Next Week

Amal and Samer leave on Saturday...Amal returns end of September

Amal will also visit Arizona and Hawaii with Reem

  

In a phone call with the Editor in Chief last Friday, Amal updated The Zureikat Weekly on the her latest news and the latest concerning Samer's first year at Tulane University.  Amal and Samer will be leaving Amman on Saturday, August 19.  They will be spending a few days in Chicago and Houston and will then leave for New Orleans on August 25.  Samer, who graduated last May, will be studying in the School of Engineering at Tulane University - New Orleans.  After spending a few days with Samer, Amal will be leaving for Arizona to attend a course there.  She will then make a four-visit to Honolulu to attend a meeting.  Reem will meet with her there.  After Amal's meeting, she and Reem will take a three-day vacation at the Maui Islands in Hawaii, after which Reem will return to London and Amal will return to Amman before the end of September.  Reem, who just finished her third year, will be starting her fourth year in London on October 4, 2000. 

After spending two days in Damascus with Hassan

Dian and the Children in Aleppo on a 1 Month Visit

Hassan thanks companies that produce canned tuna...and chickens 

Hassan returned to Amman on Monday, August 7, after making a two-day visit to Damascus with his family.  On the same day, Dian and the children left Damascus to visit Dian's family in Aleppo.  In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Hassan said that Dian and the children will be returning home in one month.  He also added, "I would like to take this opportunity to thank companies that produce canned tuna fish, and I would also like to thank chickens, because I'm not eating anything these days except tuna fish and eggs."

Dana Returns...Leaves to London Next Thursday

After starting her Showtime internship in Beirut and Cairo, Dana will be returning home on Saturday, August 12.  She will spend a few days at home and then leave for London to start her three-month internship there on Wednesday, August 16, 2000.

Zureikats Celebrate Two Occasions

     

Zureikats celebrated Hassan's birthday on August 9.  The family also celebrated Amal and Ziad's anniversary on August 11, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Hassan, Amal, and Ziad all the best.

  • Reema, Abla N., Hazem, Abla Z., Omar, and Yacoub S. participated in a peaceful rally beside the UN headquarters in Amman to protest over the economic sanctions imposed on Iraq ten years ago.  On the same day, Omar, along with his two friends, was distributing stickers and flyers in the streets of Amman.  They were all taken to the police station for an unknown reason, but then they were "freed" by the Minister of Justice.

  • Ayman returned from a month-long summer camp in England.

  • Abla Z. and Basil hosted the Shomalis on a Thursday dinner.
