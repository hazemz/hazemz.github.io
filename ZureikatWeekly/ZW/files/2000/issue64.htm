Dag sends his first e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly

Swedish Zureikats Finally Respond

Dag returns from Greece...visits Gotland

Mona and Gero visit Sylvia in Germany next week

Dag: Tell everybody I send my regards, and that I love you all

Dag is working at a software company, Sylvia is training at a hospital, Helene is working with IBM

  

Zureikats in Sweden have finally responded to our calls.  After not sending any news updates for a long time, Mona sent the following update to the Editor in Chief: "Sylvia is in Heidelberg, Germany practicing in a university hospital, and Gero and I will be visiting her next week.  Helene is working for IBM in Milan and has an interesting job.  Dag is coming today (July 16) back from Greece.  He has been on a sailing trip with other friends.  We have been living almost every day in rain.  The sun we saw only few times.  Gero and I have done a wonderful trip in Gota Canal, a boat trip in this channel which binds the different lakes in Sweden together all the way from Stockholm to Gothenburg on the west cost.  The trip took us 4 days.  Otherwise, I have been very busy with Dar Al-Mona's new books to be published soon."  Dag also sent his first e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly two days after returning from his sailing trip in Greece mentioned above: "I work since August, 1998 as key account and channel manager at a software company which is specialized in network security solutions.  Please visit our website.  There is a lot of traveling involved around Sweden and in Europe.  I live in a one-room apartment in the Stockholm center and I drive a black, two-seated sports car with red leather seats, 0-100 km/h in just 6 seconds.  I have no girlfriend (still working on it).  I just came home from a sailing trip down in Greece, and on Wednesday (July 19), I will be going to Gotland.  Gotland is the biggest island in the Baltic Sea.  There will be a lot of partying, swimming, and hanging out with some of my best Swedish friends.  Well, I guess that the Baltic Sea will not be as warm as the Red Sea.  I will probably get a cold.  I will try to take a week off this fall to come down to visit you guys.  I know it has been a while, and I feel bad about it.  Tell everybody I send my regards, and that I love you all."

Hassan returns from Tunisia and Lebanon

Hala now in Cyprus to Attend a Conference

Hala is expected to return home on Sunday, July 23

After spending a few days in Tunisia and Lebanon, Hassan returned home on Wednesday, July 19.  On the same day (July 19), Hala left for Limassol, Cyprus to attend a conference there.  Hala is expected to return home on Sunday, July 23, 2000.

More Changes on Dana's Internship at Showtime

Dana to spend 2 days in Beirut and 2 weeks in Cairo

Dana told The Zureikat Weekly that more changes have been made on her internship award at Showtime - London.  Dana will first leave for Beirut for two days on July 27.  She will then immediately go to Cairo on July 29 to spend two weeks there.  After that, Dana is expected to return home to spend a few days with the family on August 12, 2000.  She will be starting her three-month internship in London later in August.
