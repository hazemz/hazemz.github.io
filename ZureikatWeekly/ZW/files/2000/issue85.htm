4 Down...2 More to Go

Talal, Tarek, Alia, and Amer return

Reem returns Sunday...Samer returns Thursday

Almost everyone is back in Amman to celebrate Christmas.  As we mentioned in the last issue, Talal returned home on Saturday, December 9.  The next to return was Tarek on Thursday, December 14.  Then, Alia returned on Friday, December 15, and only shortly before writing this issue, Amer returned to Amman on Saturday, December 16.  Reem is expected to return on Sunday, December 17, and finally Samer will be returning on Thursday, December 21, 2000.

 

Zureikats Gather at Marwan's and Harran's Celebrating the Return of Tarek and Alia

Celebrating the return of Tarek and Alia, the family gathered at Marwan's on Thursday, December 14, and at Harran's on Friday, December 15, 2000.

Dana to Work in JTV Starting January 1

After earning her Masters degree in TV Journalism and completing a three-month internship program with Showtime in London, Dana was officially appointed in Jordan Television last week.  She will start her new job on January 1, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Dana on her new job and wish her all the best in the future.
