Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

37th Issue

January 14, 2000

The Zureikat Weekly Switches to Sunday

Editor in Chief: This decision will take effect on February 1

Editor in Chief of The Zureikat Weekly, Hazem Zureikat, said that starting February 1, 2000, The Zureikat Weekly will be released on Sundays instead of Fridays.  This decision comes after The Zureikat Weekly conducted a vote through e-mail after noticing that most readers enter the site on Mondays (after their weekends).  It also comes after the Jordanian government announced Friday and Saturday as the new weekend in Jordan.

Talal, Tarek, and Alia Leave Amman

Dana returns to Cairo on the 16th

     

After spending their mid-year holiday in Jordan, Talal, Tarek, and Alia left Amman last week.  Talal returned to Italy on Friday, January 7, Tarek returned to Scotland on Sunday, January 9, and Alia returned to London on Wednesday, January 12, 2000.  Dana is expected to return to Cairo on Sunday, January 16, 2000.  Amer and Reem were the first two to leave Amman.  They left to the UK last week.  

Harran, Hazem, Kareem Visit "the Arctic"

 

Harran and Kareem                                                               The gate of Harran's land (from inside)

 

View from Harran's land                                                      A cloud covering a mountain

On Friday, January 7, 2000, just three days after a snow storm hit some high elevations in Jordan, Harran, Hazem, and Kareem visited Harran's land in Anjara.  During their trip to what Harran called, "the Arctic", Harran, Hazen, and Kareem walked through Harran's land, which is located on one of the highest mountains in Jordan.  They also took pictures with Hazem's digital camera.

Dana Returns Home After Minor Operation

Dana removed two of her wisdom teeth

    

Dana returned home after undergoing a minor operation with her teeth.  In a short message to The Zureikat Weekly, Amal said, "Dana came to the clinic to extract her two wisdom teeth (left side) on Sunday.  It was a tough extraction; the lower molar was broken due to enclosed roots to the bone, it is Zureikat teeth.  But Dana did well; she was put on a course of antibiotic.  Marwan and Hazem came to pick her up because she couldn't drive and 3maimeh (her aunt) did a one hell of a job!"

Zureikats Celebrate Amer's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Amer's birthday on January 12, 2000.  Amer, who is 24 now, returned to Dublin earlier this month.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Amer a happy birthday and all the best.

Zureikats in ANS Start Their Finals

Hazem and Dalia started on January 12...Omar started late last month

Zureikats in Amman National School are currently doing their final exams for the 1st semester of the year 1999-2000.  Omar, who started his Tawjihi finals late last month, is still doing his exams.  Hazem and Dalia started their finals on Wednesday, January 12, 2000.  Dalia will sit for her last exam on Wednesday, January 19, and Hazem on January 20. 

  • Marwan and Omar beat Ayman and Talal in a pool match just before Talal's departure.  The match was attended by Hazem and Kareem.
