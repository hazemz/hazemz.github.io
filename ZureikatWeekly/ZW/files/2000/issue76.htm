Zureikats Celebrate Abu Harran's Return with Mashawi and Knafeh

Abu Harran left the hospital on Monday, October 9

After undergoing a successful operation at the Jordan Hospital, Abu Harran returned home on Monday, October 9.  On Monday night, Zureikats gathered at Abu Harran's celebrating his return with Mashawi and Knafeh.  Later that night, Mona returned to Sweden after making a brief visit to Amman.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Abu Harran on his return and wish him a speedy recovery and a long a healthy life.

Marwan and Abla Return from 4-Day Visit to Egypt

  

Marwan and Abla N., who left for Egypt on Wednesday, October 11, are expected to return from Egypt on Saturday (Oct. 14) night.  Marwan and Abla were invited to a two-day celebration on the 21st anniversary of Hamza Associates, a company currently working with Marwan's. 

  • Ayman sat for the SAT exam on Saturday, October 14.
