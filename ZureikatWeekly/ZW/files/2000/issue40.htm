February 6, 2000

Ziad Returns after Winning Elections

Ziad is the president of Zone 4 of the World Congress of Gastrointorology

From Al-Ra'i Newspaper - News about Ziad's winning the elections

Ziad returned home from Algeria after attending the World Congress of Gastrointorology.  During the conference, Ziad was elected as the new president of Zone 4 of the Congress.  This zone includes the Middle East, Africa, and Iran.  The Sharaiha family held a party for Ziad after returning home.  The party was held in the Le Meridian Hotel in Amman on Friday, February 4, 2000.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Ziad on winning the elections and wish him all the best.

Harran and Hassan Return Home

    

Harran returned home from Gaza on Saturday, January 29, 2000.  Harran had left Amman on January 27 on a two day business trip to the Gaza Strip.  Hassan, who visited Syria and Egypt in the last two weeks, returned from Hamburg, Germany last week.

Zureikat Students in ANS Resume School

Friday and Saturday is the new weekend for all Zureikat students

Omar, Hazem, Dalia, Deena, and Salem, who study at Amman National School, started their 2nd term for the year 1999-2000 last week.  Zureikats in ANS and in other schools have a new weekend now, Friday and Saturday.

Nothing to Worry About...It Was Only Sylvia's Boyfriend

Last Week, Editor in Chief of The Zureikat Weekly, Hazem Zureikat, discovered that someone from the University of Waikato in New Zealand had entered The Zureikat Weekly's website three times.  One day later, The Zureikat Weekly received an e-mail from Nils Kaehler, Sylvia's boyfriend, who works in Waikato Hospital in New Zealand.  Kaehler, who was only checking Sylvia's e-mail because she didn't have a computer yet, apologized for The Zureikat Weekly for "any trouble" he may have caused.
