Tarek Moves to his Own Apartment

 

 

Tarek moved to his own apartment on Thursday, October 16, 2003.  The brand-new, three-bedroom apartment is located near the 7th circle.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Tarek on his new apartment, wishing him all the best.

Ziad Leaves to Iran

Heading a delegation from Jordan, Syria, and Lebanon, Ziad left to Iran on Friday, October 17, to attend a meeting and give two lectures there.  He is expected to return to Amman on Monday, October 20, 2003.

Marwan and Abla Spend a Night at the Dead Sea

 

Marwan and Abla left to the Dead Sea on Wednesday, October 15, to attend a wedding.  They returned to Amman on Thursday, October 16, 2003.

Hala Attending Conference in Washington

After attending a one-day conference on women and journalism last Tuesday, Hala is currently attending another conference in Washington, DC.  The conference, which started on Sunday, October 19, 2003, is expected to go on throughout the week.

Zureikats Celebrate Dalia's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Dalia's birthday on Sunday, October 19, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Dalia a very happy birthday and all the best.
