Dalia and Yacoub Perform in Play

Zureikats attended the first performance which was held at Al-Hussein Cultural Center on Sunday

After months of rehearsals, Dalia and Yacoub S.'s play finally made its debut at Al-Hussein Cultural Center on Sunday, May 4, 2003.  The play, which is about the famous poet Bader Shaker Al-Sayyab, was written by Jameel Awwad and directed by Juliette Awwad.  The opening night was attended by Abla Z., Basil, Bana, Hala, Naief, Deena, Salem, Marwan, Abla N., Amal, Reema, Dr. Najla Nusseir, Abla N.'s sister who is currently visiting Amman, and Muntaha and Fayeq Karadsheh.  What should be mentioned is that Sunday's performance is only the first among many performances for this play which will beheld in different places, including a number of schools in Amman.

The Zureikat Weekly's Photographer would like to apologize for the low quality of the pictures above, which was due to poor lighting.

IB Externals Begin

Kareem, this year's Zureikat graduate, started his IB finals (externals) on Friday, May 2.  He is expected to finish his exams on Tuesday, May 13, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Kareem all the best in his exams.

Long 'Labor Day' weekend in Jordan

Bashar Visits Sharm El-Sheikh

Amer and Leanne spent the weekend at the Dead Sea...Bana in Aqaba

      

Just a few days after Easter, Zureikats enjoyed yet another long weekend.  Thursday, May 1, 2003, was a holiday in Jordan celebrating Labor Day.  Bashar spent his long weekend in Sharm El-Sheikh, Egypt, Amer and Leanne spent it at the Dead Sea, while Bana spent it in Aqaba.

  • Abla and Abla attended the 12th Conference for the Private Schools Council (PSC) which was held at the Amman Baccalaureate School on Friday and Saturday, May 2 and 3, 2003.  Dr. Najla Nusseir, Abla N.'s sister from Lebanon, also attended the conference.  Dr. Nusseir, who arrived in Amman last week, was awarded at the opening ceremony for her continuous participation in the conference.  What should be mentioned is that Abla N. and Dr. Nusseir attended a dinner held by the PSC on Friday at the Mövenpick Hotel and Resort in the Dead Sea.

  • Abu Harran, Marwan, Abla, Tarek, Dr. Najla, and several other Nusseirs had lunch in Umm Al-Amad on Friday, May 2, 2003.

  • Yacoub S.'s confirmation ceremony was held on Friday, May 2, 2003.

  • Dag spent the weekend in central Sweden with some of his friends.
