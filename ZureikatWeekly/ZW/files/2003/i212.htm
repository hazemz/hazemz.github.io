Dr. Reem Sharaiha Graduates from St. George's

Amal, Ziad, Samer, Ayman, and Talal attended the June 15 ceremony in London

After completing all the requirements for her MD a week or so ago, Dr. Reem Sharaiha graduated from the St. George's Hospital Medial School University of London with distinction on Sunday, June 15.  Amal, Ziad, Samer, Ayman, and Talal all attended the ceremony.  Amal, Ziad, and Samer left Amman on Friday, June 13; Ayman also left Boston on the same day.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Reem on her graduation and wish her all the best in the future.

Congratulations from Harran and family

In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Alia sent her family's congratulations to Reem on her graduation.  "We would like to congratulate Reem on becoming the third doctor in her family," she said, "and we wish her all the best in the future."

Amer and Leanne Return on Monday, Leave to the US Soon

The honeymoon in Paris began a few days late due to visa procedures

Amer and Leanne on their wedding day

After several delays due to visa procedures, Amer and Leanne left to Paris for their honeymoon on Wednesday, June 11.  They are expected to return home on Monday, June 16, 2003, and leave again to Chicago in the next few days.

Mona and Gero in Paris

After attending Amer and Leanne's wedding on June 6, Mona left Amman on Wednesday, June 11, 2003.  Upon arriving at the airport in Stockholm, Mona met Gero, and they both left to Paris for a few days.  Both are expected to return to Sweden this week.
