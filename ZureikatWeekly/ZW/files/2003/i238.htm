Dr. Sylvia Henning

Sylvia receives degree from the University of Vienna

Sylvia has received her degree as a Doctor in Medicine from the University of Vienna and is now Dr. Sylvia Henning.  "We are so happy that Sylvia has achieved her dream," Mona said in her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief after her visit to Amman.  "Many congratulations arrived from Amman already when I told them the news a few minutes after my arrival from the airport."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Sylvia on her degree, wishing her all the best in the future.

After a weeklong visit to Jordan

Mona Returns to Sweden

"I want...to extend my deepest gratitude to all my sisters and brothers and their families, and above all Abu Harran"

 

Pictures from the discussion about Fatat Al-Burtuqal at Darat Al-Funun.

Mona returned to Sweden on Friday, December 12, 2003, after a weeklong working visit to Jordan in which she participated in two events for the new book 'Fatat Al-Burtuqal', one at Darat Al-Funun (on Tuesday, December 9) and the other at the Jordanian Writers' Union.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief which she sent upon her return to Sweden, Mona said, "I want...through The Zureikat Weekly, to extend my deepest gratitude to all my sisters and brothers and their families, and above all Abu Harran who crowned the nice evening we had there.  As Marwan said, we are a special family, and we really are.  We should all be proud and stand together as we are stronger as a team.  The country needs us...My kindest regards to my sister Hala and her family."

Abla Attends PSC Conference at the Dead Sea

Abla N. attended a conference for administrators held by the Private Schools Council at the Dead Sea.  Abla left to the Dead Sea on Friday, December 12, and returned to Amman on Sunday, December 14, 2003.

Ammar Spends the Weekend in Florida

Ammar left to Florida on Saturday, December 13, to spend the weekend there with members of his MBA class.  He returned to DC on Sunday, December 14, 2003.

Zureikats Celebrate 2 Birthdays

 

Zureikats celebrated Tarek's birthday on Monday, December 8, and Salem's birthday on Thursday, December 11, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Tarek and Salem very happy birthdays and all the best.

  • Tarek attended a seminar on Tuesday, December 9, 2003, discussing issues related to the media and America's image in the Arab World.  The seminar, organized by the Washington Institute for Near East Policy, was attended by a number of selected businessmen from Jordan.  Tarek received an ovation after making his remarks at the conclusion of the seminar.

  • Amer and Leanne sent their birthday wishes to Marwan.  "We would just like to wish Ammo Marwan a very happy birthday," they said in an e-mail.  "We miss him a lot."  Zureikats celebrated Marwan's birthday on Tuesday, December 2.
