195th Issue
February 16, 2003

Dana and Ammar's Jaha to be Held Thursday

The jaha (tulbeh) of Dana Zureikat and Ammar Daoud will be held in Amman on Thursday, February 20, 2003.  Both Dana and Ammar currently live in Washington, DC, where Dana is working with the Voice of America and Ammar is doing his MBA.

Mona Returns to Sweden

After making a two-week trip to Amman and Abu Dhabi, Mona returned to Sweden on Tuesday, February 11, 2003.

Amer Leaves to the US for Final Part of USMLE

Amer left to New York on Sunday, February 16, 2003, to sit for the third and final part of the USMLE (United States Medical Licensing Examination).  During his ten-day stay in the US, Amer will be visiting Dana in Washington, DC.

Marwan and Abla Return from Beirut

 

After spending the Eid Al-Adha holiday in Beirut, Marwan and Abla returned home on Thursday, February 13.  Abla had earlier left Amman on Sunday, February 9, while Marwan followed on Tuesday, February 11, 2003.

Ziad Returns from London after Attending Conference

Ziad had lunch and dinner with Talal during his three-day visit

Ziad returned home on Friday, February 14, after making a three-day visit to London during which he attended a conference on liver diseases.  Ziad had earlier left Amman on Monday, February 10, 2003.  During his stay in London, Ziad had lunch and dinner with Talal, who is currently studying at the St. George's Hospital Medical School.

Zureikats Celebrate 2 Birthdays

 

Zureikats celebrated Basil's birthday on Wednesday, February 12, and Alia's birthday on Friday, February 14, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Basil and Alia very happy birthdays and all the best.

 

Alia Celebrates her Birthday with Family and Friends at Grappa and Houston's

In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Alia said, "My b-day celebrations were really special.  We celebrated on two nights, Thursday and Friday.  On the first night, we all (Tarek, Bana, and a group of my school friends) went to Grappa Restaurant.  It was loads of fun.  I really enjoyed it.  Tarek and Bana, thanks for everything!!  On the second night, after a day filled with mu3ayadat from the Zureikats, (Got lots of flowers and presents from il 3amat and the Marwan household.  The fact that Dana also called me from the US made my big day even more special.  I'd like to thank her and Reem for remembering!!), a close friend of mine, Nada Al Hassan, arranged for a beautiful dinner party at Houston's.  Amer and Leanne also came.  It was beautiful.  (Ammo Marwan, they all loved the food, especially the Champaign pasta!!)."

Abla N., Tarek, Abla Z., Bana, and Omar participated in an anti-war march held in Amman on Saturday, February 15, 2003.  What should be mentioned is that Hazem also participated in an anti-war candlelight vigil in St. Paul, Minnesota on the same day.
