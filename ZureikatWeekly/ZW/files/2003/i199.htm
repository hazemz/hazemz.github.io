Zureikats Gather at the Daouds' Residence

 

 

 

 

 

Zureikats, along with Dr. Yousef and Mrs. Shadia Nusseir, had dinner at Ammar's parents' house in Amman on Friday, March 14, 2003.  Mr. Fahhad and Mrs. Ayda Daoud had earlier invited the family for dinner during the jaha, but the dinner was postponed due to the recent snow storm.

Spring Break Begins in the US

Hazem left to Canada on Saturday; Ayman leaves to Arizona Monday

 

Hazem left to Montreal, Canada on Saturday, March 15, to visit some friends.  Ayman, on the other hand, will be leaving to Arizona on Monday, March 17, also to visit some friends.  Ayman and Hazem's trips come during spring break, which started in many US universities on Saturday, March 15, and will end on Sunday, March 23, 2003.  What should be mentioned is that Samer, who also studies in the US, had his spring break during the 'Mardi Gras' celebrations late last month, when he was visited by Ayman.

Accompanied by Umm Samir

Reema Spends a Day in Syria

Reema and her mother, Umm Samir, spent Thursday, March 13, 2003, in Syria.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Alia said, "She and Taita Umm Samir went to Saidnaya.  Mohammad, our driver, also went with them to do some paper proceedings for Amer at the Irish Consulate / Syrian Foreign Ministry, since we don't have an Irish consulate here."
