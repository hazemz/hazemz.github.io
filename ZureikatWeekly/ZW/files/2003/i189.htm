Welcome to the New ZW

As Editor in Chief of The Zureikat Weekly, I would like to welcome you all to The Zureikat Weekly - 2003.  Our new layout includes, as usual, new logos and new pictures for Zureikat family members.  This year, we have also added a new section called 'Letters to the Editor' (right under 'Quickies').  This section will include letters you send to me regarding certain news items or other issues related to the Weekly.  Note that the 'Letters to the Editor' section will not be included in every issue in 2003, since I won't be expecting to receive such letters every single week.  For now, I hope you enjoy the new Zureikat Weekly.  We always welcome your suggestions and comments to further improve our newsletter...

Thank you.

Editor in Chief,
Hazem Zureikat

 

'Rewind' Section Removed...For Now

Please note that the 'Rewind' section has been removed for now, since it would be too much to have news from 1999, 2000, 2001, and 2002 all at the same time.  'Rewind' will probably be added in April.  It will only include news in 1999 (i.e. four years before).  Next year, it will include news from 2000, and so on.

2003 Correspondents and Photographers to be Appointed Soon

The Editor in Chief will be appointing The Zureikat Weekly's 2003 Correspondents and Photographers before he returns to the US on January 22, 2003.

Zureikats Celebrate Abu Harran's 90th Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Abu Harran's 90th birthday on Wednesday, January 1, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Abu Harran a very happy birthday and all the best.

 

 

 

 

 

Zureikats Gather at Abla and Basil's

Zureikats gathered at Abla and Basil's for lunch on Friday, January 3, 2003.  The lunch, which is the first family gathering in 2003, was attended by all Zureikats in Jordan in addition to Dr. Yousef Nusseir, Abla N.'s brother, and his family.  Lunch included Kharoof Mahshi, Koosa o Waraq, Shushbarak, Kubbeh, and Rice & Chicken.

Amer Leaves to the US

Amer left to the US on Sunday, January 5, 2003, to attend several interviews including one in Duke University (Durham, North Carolina) and another in the Case Western Reserve University (Cleveland, Ohio).  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Amer all the best in his interviews.

Reem in Houston after Visiting France

Samer Returns to New Orleans

Talal leaves Monday, Ayman on the 14th, and Hazem on the 22nd

 

After spending New Year's Eve in France, Reem left to Houston, Texas on Friday, January 3.  Reem had earlier left Amman to Paris on Sunday, December 29, 2002.  Sources told The Zureikat Weekly that Reem will be staying in Houston till the end of this month.  Samer, on the other hand, who is currently studying at Tulane University in New Orleans, Louisiana, returned to the US on Sunday, January 5.  Samer had earlier spent New Year's Eve in Beirut, Lebanon, and returned to Amman on Thursday, January 2.  Other Zureikats studying abroad will be returning to their universities in the next three weeks.  Talal will be returning to the UK on Monday, January 6, Ayman will be returning to the US on Tuesday, January 14, and Hazem will be returning to the US on Wednesday, January 22, 2003.

In his first business trip with JCE

Tarek Visits Yemen...Sunday

In his first business trip with JCE (the Jordanian Consulting Engineer Co.), Tarek will be leaving to Yemen on Sunday, January 5.  He is expected to return home on Thursday, January 9, 2003.

Zureikat Students in Jordan Start their Mid-Year Exams

   

   

Zureikat school students in Jordan have started their mid-year (first semester final) exams.  Dalia, Deena, Yacoub S. (Amman National School), Nadine, and Yacoub Z. (Modern Schools) started their exams on Sunday, January 5.  Sabine (Modern Schools) will be starting her exams on Monday, January 6, 2003.  The mid-year exams, which will end in less than two weeks, will be followed by a two-week, mid-year holiday.

 

Omar Starts his Exams on the 16th...

Kareem Returns to School after Christmas Vacation

 

Omar, who is currently in his third year at the Princess Sumayya University for Technology, will start his mid-year exams on Thursday, January 16, and finish on Tuesday, January 21.  On the other hand, Kareem, who is currently in the 12th grade at the Amman Baccalaureate School, returned to school on Sunday, January 5, 2003, after a two-week Christmas vacation.  Kareem will not be having any mid-year exams this year.

Harran and Reema Host Yet Another Mansaf for the Young Zureikats

 

Harran and Reema hosted the young Zureikats on a Mansaf on Saturday, January 4, 2003.  This comes one week after Marwan and Abla's Mansaf on December 28, 2002.
