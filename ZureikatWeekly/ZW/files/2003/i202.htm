Amer Accepted in Chicago

Amer and Leanne will be moving to the US next June

Amer left to the US last week, returns home soon

Amer has been accepted in the University of Chicago Hospital's residency program.  Amer was called for an interview on Thursday, March 27.  He left to the US on Saturday, March 29, 2003, and will be returning home next week.  Amer and Leanne are expected to relocate to the US in mid-June, just a few weeks after their wedding, which is scheduled to take place in Amman on May 15.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Amer on his acceptance and wish him all the best in the years to come.

Family Celebrates Ziad's 60th Birthday

Amal: We didn't do anything because of the situation

Zureikats celebrated Ziad's 60th birthday on Tuesday, April 1, 2003.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Amal said, "We didn't do anything because of the situation.  I got him a cake, and we celebrated together.  He got phone calls from the children the night before at exactly 12 midnight, which was really touching.  We received nice flowers..., telephone calls, and champagne."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Ziad a very happy birthday and all the best.

Samer Elected as Treasurer for School of Engineering Student Council

Samer has been elected as Treasurer for Tulane University's School of Engineering Student Council.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Samer and wish him all the best.  What should be mentioned is that Samer is currently in his third year at Tulane University in New Orleans, Louisiana.

Hassan Returns from Egypt after "Important Meeting"

Hassan left to Egypt on Sunday, March 30, and returned home on Tuesday, April 1, 2003.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Dalia said, "He went there for an important meeting, and the results were good."

Zureikats Celebrate Harran and Reema's Anniversary

Reema: We celebrated by opening an excellent bottle of wine

 

Zureikats celebrated Harran and Reema's wedding anniversary on Saturday, April 5, 2003.  In an e-mail she sent to the Editor in Chief on Saturday, Reema said, "Amman is gloomy nowadays, and I think depression is prevailing especially with the news on Iraq.  Harran stopped watching news and confines to the Discovery Channel and the National Geographic.  Today, we celebrated our 29th anniversary...Thanks to Alia, Kareem , Cora, and Awatef, who made it cheerful, Harran and I celebrated by opening an excellent bottle of wine, Come De M, especially for the occasion.  Please note that Harran would never open that bottle had it been any other occasion."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Harran and Reema a very happy anniversary and all the best.
