Abu Harran Returns Home after Spending Few Days in ICU

Abu Harran had a stroke that affected his right side

Amal: He can now move his right hand; his leg is also better

As he was having lunch on Thursday, January 16, Abu Harran suffered a stroke that affected the movement of his right-hand side.  Abu Harran was immediately rushed to the Intensive Care Unit at the Jordan Hospital in Amman.  After spending two nights at the ICU, Abu Harran returned home on Saturday, January 18, 2003, and is now recovering well.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Amal said that Abu Harran's leg is getting better; he can now move his right hand, but he still can't feel it.  "He will hopefully recover with physiotherapy and return to his normal routine," she said.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Abu Harran a speedy recovery and a long and healthy life.
