Alia on 2-Week Visit to London

Alia left to London on Saturday, November 1, for two weeks of physiotherapy in Hemel Hempstead.  In an e-mail she sent to the Editor in Chief, Reema said, "Alia left...to London with Sue and Deena Kawar...Deena will join Alia on Sunday evening in Hemel Hempstead where shell be seen by her physiotherapist Leora for two weeks.  During sessions, Deena will video Alia's program so that I'll know what to do in Amman.  Both Alia and Deena were very excited.  Alia will meet with Kareem on the weekend, if possible, and some university friends too."  Alia is expected to return to Amman on Friday, November 14, 2003.

Amal and Ziad in Madrid, Return Wednesday

 

Amal and Ziad left to Madrid, Spain on Friday, October 31, where Ziad will give a lecture at a European gastroenterology meeting and will meet with leaders of the World Congress of Gastroenterology (OMGE).  Ziad is currently the representative for the Middle East and Africa in the OMGE.  Amal and Ziad are expected to return to Amman on Wednesday, November 5, 2003.

Harran Returns to Work after Food Poisoning

After suffering from food poisoning during the weekend, Harran returned to work on Sunday, November 2, 2003.

 

Marwan on Antibiotics after Suffering Tooth Infection

Due to an infection in his tooth, Marwan suffered from a fever for a couple of days last week "but kept going to work", according to Abla N.  He is currently recovered from the fever but is still on antibiotics.
