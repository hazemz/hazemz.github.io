ZUREIKATS FOLLOW THE LATEST US-LED AGGRESSION AGAINST IRAQ

Mona and Hazem Participate in Protests

 

The anti-war march which was held in St. Paul, Minnesota on Saturday, March 22, 2003.

In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief which she sent on Saturday, March 22, Mona said, "I am joining in a demonstration today against the war.  All Sweden authors and intellectuals will join.  It will be a very big one.  Down with America!!"  Hazem also participated in an anti-war march in St. Paul, Minnesota on the same day.

 

Dana Interviews Foreign Minister as Part of War Coverage

Dana started reading the 15-minute news bulletins at Radio Sawa

As part of Radio Sawa's coverage of the war, Dana interviewed the Jordanian Foreign Minister, Marwan Muasher, on Friday, March 21, 2003.  Dana also started reading the 15-minute detailed news bulletins at Radio Sawa after a few weeks of reading the 2-minute news headlines on the air.

Reema Appointed in Higher Council for Welfare of the Disabled

Alia: We are very proud of her and wish her all the best

Reema has been appointed as a member of the National Higher Council for the Welfare of the Disabled.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Alia said, "We are very proud of her and wish her all the best in this endeavor!  We're sure she'll do her best, as always."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Reema on her new post and wish her all the best.

Dag Attends CeBIT Fair in Hannover

Dag at the CeBIT Fair in Hannover, Germany last week.

Dag left to Hannover, Germany on Tuesday, March 11, where he is currently attending the CeBIT Fair 2003.  Dag is expected to return to Sweden on Monday, March 24, 2003.

 

Samer Gets 1st Prize in Tulane Robot Olympiad;

Dalia Gets 1st Prize in Shouman Competition

From Al-Ra'i Daily Newspaper

Samer got the first prize in the Tulane University Robot Battle Olympiad, which was held on Friday, March 21, 2003.  Dalia also got the first prize in the Shouman Competition for her work in caricature drawing.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Samer and Dalia on their prizes and wish them all the best.

After spending the spring break with their friends

Ayman and Hazem Return to their Universities

 

Ayman and Hazem returned to their universities after spending their spring break with some friends.  Ayman left to Arizona on Monday, March 17, and returned to Massachusetts on Saturday, March 22, while Hazem left to Canada on Saturday, March 15, and returned to Minnesota on Wednesday, March 19.  Ayman and Hazem's classes will resume on Monday, March 24, 2003.
