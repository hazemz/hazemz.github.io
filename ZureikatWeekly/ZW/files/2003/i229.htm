Hala to the Editor in Chief: "3amtak badda3at"

"The Star of Jordan Television" Speaks in Chicago

Hala was one of three panelists at an IIE dinner

Hala was one of three panelists at an annual dinner for the Midwest IIE Office in Chicago (IIE is the Institute of International Education).  At the beginning of the dinner, which was held on Wednesday, October 8, Hala was presented by Allan Goodman, President and CEO of the institute and formerly a professor at Georgetown University.  Goodman talked about Hala and where she comes from, then he introduced her as "the star of Jordan Television, Hala Zureikat".  Hala then talked about her family, her brothers, and about Umm Harran and how she fought to educate her children.  Hala, along with the two other panelists, later answered questions about the Arab-Israeli conflict and about the situation in Iraq.  Many of the attendees (who were about 200-250 from "al-tabaqa al-mukhmalyyah" in the US, as Hala put it) complemented Hala at the end of the dinner.  "You presented the human side," they said, according to Hala.  "This is what we want to hear."  Hala had arrived in Chicago on Tuesday, October 7, and returned to Rockville on Thursday, October 9, 2003.  Two days after her return, Hala said, in a phone call with the Editor in Chief, "3amtak badda3at.  You can put that in The Zureikat Weekly."  During her visit to Chicago, Hala also visited Amer and Leanne's apartment and spent some time with Leanne.  "I was happy to be the first Zureikat to visit Amer and Leanne," she said.

Mona Launches Book in Frankfurt

Mona and Gero left to Germany on Thursday, return Sunday

Dar Al-Mona launched a new book entitled 'Fatat Al-Burtuqal' at the Frankfurt Book Fair this weekend.  'Fatat Al-Burtuqal' is the Arabic version of a book written by Jostein Gaarder.(author of Sophie's World).  Mona and Gero left to Frankfurt, Germany for the event on Thursday, October 9, and are expected to return to Sweden on Sunday, October 12, 2003.

Ayman Visits Washington

Ayman had mlookhyyeh at Hala's on Saturday

Ayman arrived in Washington, DC on Friday, October 10, to spend the weekend there with some friends.  On Saturday, Ayman spent the day with Hala and the family and had mlookhyyeh for lunch at their house.  Ayman is expected to return to Boston on Sunday, October 12, 2003.
