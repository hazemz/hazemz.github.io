Zureikats in the US Start their Summer Holiday

Samer returns May 21...Ayman to work in Tufts till July

Hazem accepting his French award from the Chair of the French Department at Macalester last Tuesday.

Samer, who is studying at Tulane University, finished his finals on Tuesday, May 6; Ayman, who is studying at Tufts University, finished his finals on Thursday, May 8, while Hazem, who is studying at Macalester College, will finish his finals on Monday, May 12.  Hazem will be the first to return home this summer.  He will be leaving to Washington DC on Tuesday, May 13, to spend a few days with Dana and Ammar, after which he is expected to arrive in Amman on Saturday, May 17.  (Note: The next issue of The Zureikat Weekly will be released from Amman.)  Samer, who will be helping in this year's commencement at Tulane, will be returning home four days later, on Wednesday, May 21.  Ayman, however, will be working at the Mechanical Engineering Department at Tufts before he returns home in July.  On the other hand, Hazem, who was awarded a prize for excellence in French studies, received his award at the French Department's annual barbecue on Tuesday, May 6, 2003.

Zureikats Celebrate Gero's Birthday

Mona and the family had lunch at 'Ulriksdals Palace' on this occasion

Zureikats celebrated Gero's birthday on Sunday, May 11, 2003.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief which she sent on Sunday morning, Mona said, "Today, the Henning family is celebrating Gero´s birthday.  Red roses and birthday cake and then lunch with the family at Ulriksdals Palace, where my parents were with us in 1977 at a dinner party with many others celebrating Helene´s baptism.  Today, the 11th of May, is a
beautiful day in Stockholm, and we are going to enjoy it fully with Gero."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Gero a very happy birthday and all the best.
