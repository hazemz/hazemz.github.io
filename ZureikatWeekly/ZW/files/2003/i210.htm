Kareem Graduates from the Amman Baccalaureate School

 

 

Right: Kareem playing the drums as the graduating class sang 'Wind of Change'.

 

 

 

Kareem graduated from the Amman Baccalaureate School on Friday, May 30, 2003.  The graduation ceremony, which was held at the School's sports field, was attended by several family members.  After the ceremony, the family gathered at Harran's for cake, Knafeh, and champagne.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Kareem on his graduation and wish him all the best in the years to come.

Final Preparations Underway for Friday's Wedding

Zureikats, Kawars, and Ayoubs host several pre-wedding dinners

Mona to attend wedding, arrives on 5th

******************************

Schedule

4pm: Church service at the Sweifieh church

6:30pm - 8:30pm: Reception at Harran and Reema's

9:30pm: Dinner party at Al-Sawani Restaurant - Grand Hyatt Amman

******************************

All invitations have been sent, and the final preparations are underway for the first wedding among the Zureikat grandchildren.  Amer and Leanne's wedding will take place in Amman next Friday, June 6 (see schedule above).  Celebrating this occasion, family and friends gathered in Umm Al-Amad on Tuesday, May 27.  The family also gathered at Mr. Ghasoub Kawar's house on Saturday, May 31, and at Dr. George Kawar, Leanne's father's, house on Sunday, June 1.  More pre-wedding dinners are expected to take place before Friday.  On the other hand, sources told The Zureikat Weekly that Mona is expected to arrive in Amman on Thursday, June 5, 2003, to attend the wedding.

Abla, Hala, Ammar, and Ammar's sister, Rita, who was also visiting Jordan at the time.

Zureikats in ANS Win Several Awards

      

Zureikats in Amman National School won several awards in the past few weeks.  Following is the list of awards:

Dalia's awards:
- 1st place in a competition held as part of the Bader Shaker Al-Sayyeb Festival.
- 3rd place in the English creative writing competition (short story category).
- Certificate for participating in a play about Bader Shaker Al-Sayyab.

Yacoub S.'s awards:
- Certificate for outstanding academic performance.
- 1st place in a competition held as part of the Bader Shaker Al-Sayyab Festival.
- 2nd place in the Arabic creative writing competition (article category).
- 3rd place in the English creative writing competition (short story category).
- Certificate for participating in a play about Bader Shaker Al-Sayyab.

Deena's awards:
- 1st place in an art competition.
- 1st place in a poetry recital (mentioned in 208th issue) as part of the Bader Shaker Al-Sayyab Festival.
- 2nd place in the English creative writing competition (short story category).
- 3rd place in the Arabic creative writing competition (article category).
- Certificate for her outstanding efforts in completing a research about the planets.
- Certificate for her outstanding behavior.
- 'You are the Star' award in science class.

Salem's awards:
- Special thanks from the PE teacher for participation in class.

The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Dalia, Yacoub, Deena, and Salem on all these awards and wish them all the best.
