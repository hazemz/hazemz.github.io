Reem, Samer, Ayman, Hazem, Kareem Return

Talal returns Tuesday

   

   

Reem, Samer, Ayman, Hazem, and Kareem are now all in Amman to spend their Christmas holidays with the family in Jordan.  Kareem was the first to return home.  He returned on Monday, December 15, followed by Hazem on Thursday, December 18, Samer and Ayman on Saturday, December 20, and Reem on Sunday, December 21.  Talal is expected to arrive in Amman on Tuesday, December 23.  Celebrating Kareem's return, Zureikats gathered at Harran's on Tuesday, December 16.  The family also gathered at Marwan's upon Hazem's return on Thursday, December 18, and at Amal's on Saturday, December 20, 2003, after Samer and Ayman's return.

In Jordan:

Christmas Eve at Harran's; Christmas Day at Marwan's

No changes in this year's Christmas celebrations: Harran and Reema will be hosting the Zureikats on Christmas Eve (Wednesday, December 24), while Marwan and Abla will be hosting the family on Christmas Day (Thursday, December 25).

 Dr. Henning E-mails the ZW

Sylvia: I would like to thank all my wonderful relatives for their nice congratulations...
It reminded me again what a big and lovely family I have in Jordan

Dr. Sylvia Henning sent an e-mail to the Editor in Chief last week.  In her e-mail, Sylvia said, " I would like to take the opportunity to thank all my wonderful relatives and especially my parents for their nice congratulations last Friday.  It was a wonderful surprise which made me very, very happy, and it reminded me again what a big and lovely family I have in Jordan.  I wish you all a Merry Christmas and a happy New Year.  Hope to see all you soon."

 

Talal Congratulates Sylvia on her Degree

"We should all open a Zureikat's hospital...yet sliding the Sharaiha name somehow!"

Talal congratulated Sylvia on her degree in an e-mail he sent to the Editor in Chief.  In his e-mail, Talal said, "I would like to congratulate Sylvia on reaching one of her highest peaks in her life.  Enjoy it, as you earned it!  I reckon we should all open a Zureikat's hospital in Jordan (yet sliding the Sharaiha name somehow, as there will be two of us soon!!)."
