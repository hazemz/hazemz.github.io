Zureikats Continue to Protest Against the War

Family members around the world have been participating in protests, marches, and other events

As the US-led aggression against Iraq continues, Zureikats around the world are participating in various events to protest against this war.  Family members in Jordan attended several protests, marches, and other events.  Such events included an anti-war vigil on Monday, March 24, 2003, which was attended by Reema and Alia.  The vigil was held at the St. Mary of Nazareth Church.

Rare Spring Snow Storm Hits Jordan

This storm was not as strong as the first one

A street in Amman on Tuesday.  (Photo courtesy of the Jordan News Agency (Petra))

A snow storm hit Jordan on Tuesday, March 25, causing schools and businesses to shut down early on that day and some to also shut down on Wednesday.  The latest snow storm comes four weeks after a stronger snow storm which caused many schools and businesses to shut down for three days.

Hassan Returns from Beirut after 3-Day Trip

Hassan left to Beirut on Monday, March 24, on a three-day business trip.  He returned home on Thursday, March 27, 2003.

Zureikats Celebrate 2 Birthdays

 

Zureikats celebrated Kareem's birthday on Tuesday, March 25, and Naief's birthday on Thursday, March 27, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Kareem and Naief very happy birthdays and all the best.

Yacoub: If you have not been confirmed, you are not a Christian

Yacoub started his first out of a series of lessons to be "confirmed"

Yacoub S. took his first out of a series of lessons to be "confirmed" (Christianity-wise).  Yacoub's message to all Zureikats, "If you have not been confirmed, you are not a Christian."

Pictures from Umm Al-Amad

Marwan, Abla, and Tarek spent Friday, March 28, 2003, in Umm Al-Amad.  Here are some pictures from our Photographer:

 

 

 

  • Amal, Amer, and Tarek had lunch at Abu Harran's on Thursday, March 27, 2003.

  • Bana had a small car accident on Friday, March 28, 2003.  (Note the word "small"...nothing serious guys!!).  Another vehicle turned right without noticing Bana's vehicle, and he crashed into her.  Damage report: No injuries for Bana, but the front-left headlight of the (not so new anymore) Nissan broke.

  • A belated birthday celebration for Yacoub Z. was held on Friday, March 28.  Dalia, Deena, and Salem were among the 18 invited to a place called "Access - computer games".  What should be mentioned is that Yacoub celebrated his 11th birthday on Saturday, March 8, 2003.

  • Bashar and Bana spent Friday, March 28, 2003, at the Dead Sea.  Tarek was supposed to join them, but when he got to the Dead Sea military checkpoint, he was stopped and asked to go back to Amman because his friend had forgotten his ID and was not allowed to pass.

   

  • Harran has decided that watching the Discovery Channel and the National Geographic Channel is far better than watching the news these days, particularly as the western media is blatantly biased!

  • A note to Zureikats abroad: Jordan switched to Daylight Saving time (summer time) on Friday, March 28, 2003.  Jordan's time is now GMT+3.
