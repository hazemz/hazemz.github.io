July 6, 2003

Tarek Returns from Baghdad

Tarek returned from Baghdad on Sunday, July 6, after a five-day working visit to Iraq.  Tarek, the first Zureikat to visit Iraq after the war, left Amman on Tuesday, July 1, 2003.

After spending the first part of his holiday working in the US

Ayman Returns Home

After spending the first part of his summer holiday working at the Mechanical Engineering Department in his university (Tufts), Ayman returned home on Thursday, July 3, 2003.

Abla Returns from DC

Abla N. returned home on Monday, June 30, 2003, after a two-week visit to Washington DC in which she oversaw the final preparations for Dana and Ammar's wedding, scheduled to be held in DC on August 2.  Abla, along with Marwan, Tarek, Hazem, and several members of the Zureikat family, are expected to leave to Washington for the wedding before the end of this month.

Dalia, Deena, Hala, and Naief standing outside the Intercontinental Hotel shortly after the ceremony

Dalia Receives Shouman Award

The ceremony was held at the Intercontinental Hotel on Saturday

In a ceremony held at the Amman Intercontinental Hotel on Saturday, July 5, 2003, Dalia officially received the first prize in the Shouman Competition for her work in caricature drawing for the 13-15 age group.  The ceremony was attended by Hala, Naief, Deena, and the Editor in Chief.  Deputizing for HM Queen Noor Al-Hussein, HRH Princess Alia Al-Faisal distributed the awards.  This comes more than two months after the news of Dalia winning the prize was published in local newspapers.

Zureikats Celebrate 4 Occasions

 

       

Zureikats celebrated Harran's birthday on Tuesday, July 1, Amal's birthday on Thursday, July 3, Abla and Basil's wedding anniversary also on Thursday, July 3, and Marwan and Abla's wedding anniversary on Friday, July 4, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Harran and Amal very happy birthdays and Abla, Basil, Marwan, and Abla very happy anniversaries and all the best.
