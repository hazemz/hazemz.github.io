Just weeks after Naief's acceptance in Ohio

Hala Accepted in Maryland

Hala says that the next few weeks will be very decisive for the Karadshehs.

Hala has been awarded the Hubert Humphrey fellowship to attend the class of 2003-2004 at the University of Maryland at College Park, in the US.  This comes a few weeks after Naief was awarded the Distinguished Scholarship Award from the Arab Fund for Social and Economic Development (based in Kuwait) to teach and do research at the Ohio State University in Columbus, Ohio.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Hala ruled out staying alone with the children in Maryland while Naief stayed in Columbus.  She said that Naief has already started searching for another university in Washington DC (near Maryland) where he can teach and do his research.  "If it does not work out for him," she said, "I would reject the scholarship and go with him to Columbus.  I ask all Zureikats to help me find a solution.  We have less than a month to decide."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate both Hala and Naief on their awards and wish them and their family all the best.

A few days after Hassan's return from two trips to Syria and Lebanon

Dian and the Children Leave to Syria

Hassan accompanies his family to Damascus, returns

Dian and the children will meet Hassan in Beirut on their way back home next month

     

Dian, her mother, Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z. are now in Aleppo, Syria.  They, along with Hassan, left Amman on Saturday, June 28, to Damascus by car, after which Dian, her mother, and the children flew to Aleppo.  Hassan is expected to return to Amman on Sunday, June 29.  "I am returning home in a few hours, after making sure that my mother-in-law is far from Amman," he said from Damascus in a phone call with the Editor in Chief on Sunday afternoon.  Later next month, Hassan will be joining Dian and the children in Beirut, before they all head back to Amman.  On the other hand, Hassan returned home on Sunday, June 22, after making a six-day visit to Syria and Lebanon which took him to Beirut, Tartous, Lathiqiya, and Zahleh.  Hassan also made a one-day visit to Damascus on Wednesday, June 25, 2003.

Amer and Leanne Send their First E-mail from Chicago

"We would like to thank every member of the Zureikat family for making our special day so perfect"

"Chicago is great...We are loving every minute of it"

Amer and Leanne congratulate Reem and send their wishes to Dana and Ammar

 

Amer and Leanne sent their first e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly after moving to Chicago.  Following is the full text of their e-mail:

Hi everyone,

This is our first e-mail to the Weekly as Mr. and Mrs. Zureikat of Chicago!!  We would like to thank every member of the Zureikat family for making our special day so perfect!  A special thank you to all the absentees for their phone calls.  We wish you could have been there with us.  Congratulations to Dr. Reem Sharaiha on her remarkable achievement!!

Chicago is great.  We are settling in slowly, although we are living like nomads (no couches, TV, or tables) on the 21st floor.  we are loving every minute of it and can't wait till you all come and visit!  Looking forward to seeing you all in DC.  Dana and Ammar, don't stress!  Have fun and enjoy it to the max.  We cant wait to see you!

All our love,
Leanne and Amer
