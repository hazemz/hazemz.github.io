Marwan, Abla, Tarek, and Hazem Arrive in Washington

7 more Zureikats expected to arrive in DC in the coming days

 

 

Marwan, Abla, Dana, Hazem, Ammar and his family, and Miss Rana Lababidi having dinner at a restaurant in DC on Saturday night.

Abla N. arrived in Washington, DC on Tuesday, July 22, followed by Marwan and Hazem on Friday, July 25, and Tarek on Saturday, July 26.  This comes as the family prepares for Dana and Ammar's wedding, scheduled to take place in Washington on Saturday, August 2, 2003.  Other Zureikats and relatives expected to attend the wedding include Mona, Dag, Amal, Reema, Amer, Leanne, Alia, Dr. Yousef Nusseir, and Dr. Najla Bashour and her daughter Bana.  They are all expected to arrive in Washington within the next few days.

After spending a few days in Lebanon

Hassan and Family Return Home

Hassan, Dian, Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub returned home on Tuesday, July 22, 2003, after spending a few days in Lebanon.  Before going to Lebanon, Dian and the children had spent a few weeks in Aleppo, Syria.

Reem: I'd like to thank all the Zureikats for the gifts they've given me

Reem hosted some family members and her old school friends for dinner on Tuesday

Reem and her brothers at the dinner on Tuesday

Reem sent an e-mail to the Editor in Chief a few weeks after the family celebrated her graduation from St. George's.  In her e-mail, Reem said, "I'd like to take this opportunity and wish Dana and Ammar a wonderful week, and hopefully I'll get to see them soon.  As for other news, well we had a dinner for family and school friends on Tuesday.  The food and the ambiance was amazing.  I'd like to thank Umm Reem for her relentless effort in making that evening special.  I'd also like to thank all the Zureikats for the gifts they've given me and my dad, as well, for all what he has done."
