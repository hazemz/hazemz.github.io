A SPECIAL SECTION OF THE ZUREIKAT WEEKLY

ZUREIKATS FOLLOW THE LATEST US-LED AGGRESSION AGAINST IRAQ

With great sadness, pain, and anger, Zureikats around the world are following the latest US-led aggression against Iraq.  All what we can say at this time is that our hearts and minds are with the Iraqi people, the ones who are suffering the most from this brutal aggression...
