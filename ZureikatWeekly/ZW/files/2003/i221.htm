A Midsummer Friday at Harran's

 

 

 

 

Zureikats, along with Dr. Yousef Nusseir and his family, Mrs. Hiltrud and Miss Carmen Saad, and Dr. Shabib Ammari, gathered at Harran and Reema's for lunch on Friday, August 15, 2003.  Lunch included Mansaf and Koosa o Warag.

Amal and Ziad Return from South Africa

 

Amal and Ziad returned from South Africa on Wednesday, August 13, 2003.  During their nine-day trip, Ziad attended a conference on gastroenterology patronized by former South African President Nelson Mandela.

 

Zureikats Celebrate Amal and Ziad's Anniversary

Zureikats celebrated Amal and Ziad's wedding anniversary on Monday, August 11, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Amal and Ziad a very happy anniversary and all the best.
