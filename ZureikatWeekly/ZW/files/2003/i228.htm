October 5, 2003

Dana and Ammar Back in Washington

Dana postponed her departure by one day because she got sick

 

Dana and Ammar are back in Washington, DC after making their first visit to Jordan together.  Ammar left Amman on Tuesday, September 30, while Dana, who was supposed to leave on Thursday, extended her visit till Friday, October 3, 2003, because she got sick on the last few days of her visit.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Dana a speedy recovery.

Amal and Reema Return Home

 

After accompanying Kareem to Bristol for the start of his first year as a law student there, Reema returned home on Tuesday, September 30, 2003.  On the other hand, Amal, who visited San Francisco and Houston, returned to Amman on Friday, October 3, 2003.

Hassan and Dian Return from 1-Day Visit to Syria

 

Hassan and Dian returned home last week after a one-day visit to Syria in which they attended the funeral of Dian's uncle.

Samer Visits Minnesota, Leaves Monday

Samer, Hazem, and two of Samer's classmates (both students at Macalester College) at a restaurant in uptown Minneapolis, Minnesota

Samer, who is currently studying in New Orleans, Louisiana, left to St. Paul, Minnesota on Friday, October 3, to spend the weekend there with Hazem and some friends.  He is expected to return to New Orleans on Monday, October 6, 2003.

Bana Celebrates her Birthday in Umm Al-Amad

 

A number of family members and friends gathered in Umm Al-Amad on Friday, October 3, 2003, celebrating Bana's birthday.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Bana a very happy birthday and all the best.

Zureikats Celebrate Hala and Naief's Anniversary

 

Zureikats celebrated Hala and Naief's wedding anniversary on Wednesday, October 1, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Hala and Naief a very happy anniversary and all the best.

  • Harran and Reema hosted a dinner party for Nada Talal Al-Hassan (who will be getting married soon) on Sunday, October 5, 2003.
