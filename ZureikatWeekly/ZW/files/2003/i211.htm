Amer and Leanne Tie the Knot

Zureikats celebrate the first wedding of a grandchild...Many called it "The wedding of Cinderella and prince charming"

Friday, June 6, 2003, 2:30pm: Zureikats gathered at Harran's house before heading off to Mr. George Kawar's residence to get the new bride.  The family was preparing for the first wedding among its grandchildren....

Church Service ... 4pm

Marwan, representing the Zureikats, asked the Kawars for their daughter's hand in what he called the 'moment of truth'.  After the Kawars accepted, Leanne and her father came down the stairs, and the wedding procession moved to the Sweifieh Church.  As Amer and Kareem, the best man, waited at the alter, Abu Harran and Mona first walked down the aisle, followed by Umm Samir, Mr. Samir Ayoub, Harran, Reema, Alia, Sue Kawar (Leanne's mother), Leanne's uncle, Salem and the bride's maids, Deena Kawar (Leanne's sister), the maid of honor, and finally Leanne and her father.  After the service, which lasted for about a half an hour, guests conveyed their congratulations to Dr. Amer and Mrs. Leanne Zureikat and to members of both families.

Reception at Harran's ... 6:30pm

At about 6:30pm, Harran, Reema, Mr. George, and Mrs. Sue Kawar started receiving guests at Harran and Reema's as the wedding reception began.  Amer and Leanne made their entrance at about 7:15.  They were followed by a group of singers who sang traditional Arabic wedding songs.  Drinks and snacks were served at the reception which ended at about 8:30pm.

Dinner at the Hyatt ... 9:30pm

Family members and close relatives and friends attended the dinner party which was held at Al-Sawani Restaurant at the Grand Hyatt - Amman.  Harran, Reema, and Mr. and Mrs. Kawar received the guests at the entrance of Al-Sawani.  Amer and Leanne, after having a pre-party at their Royal Suite, entered at about 10:30.  Dancing continued till the early hours of Saturday morning, even after breakfast was served.  One of the highlights of the evening at the Hyatt was a speech given by Kareem, the best man.  Kareem's speech was followed by another one given by Amer's Norwegian friend.

The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Amer and Leanne on their wedding, wishing them a happy, healthy, and prosperous future.

JCE Receives Ministry of Public Works & Housing Award for 2001

 

Left: The guests \ Right: Marwan giving a speech

In a ceremony held at the Le Royal Hotel on Wednesday, June 4, 2003, Jordanian Consulting Engineer Co. officially received the Ministry of Public Works and Housing Award for Distinguished Performance in Consulting Work for 2001.  The ceremony included speeches by several people in the field including Marwan and Mr. Husni Abu Gheida, the Minister of Public Works and Housing.  In his speech, Marwan said that earning this award wasn't only the result of hard work and perseverance that the JCE staff demonstrated, but that it was also the result of continued support from the institutions in the engineering and consulting fields in Jordan.  What should be mentioned is that The Zureikat Weekly announced that JCE had won the award last March.  The ceremony, however, was not held until last Wednesday.  The award was given to JCE for their work on the Lajjoun Water Supply Project, a major water supply project valued at nearly US$40 million.  JCE managed to complete the designs and carry out the supervision of this project in a record 9 months.  Once again, The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Marwan and the JCE staff on winning this prestigious award.

Mona and Salem...Live on JTV

The topic of discussion: Jinan Fi Bait Ya Lait

 

Jordan Television's kids program 'Waqt Al-Farah' has been spending the last few weeks discussing one of Dar Al-Mona's books, 'Jinan Fi Bait Ya Lait', written in Swedish by Estred Lindgren, and translated to Arabic by Dr. Walid Saif.  As part of this discussion, the presenter of the program, Aroob Soboh, made a live telephone call with Mona on Sunday, May 25.  On the other hand, Salem was also interviewed live on the same program on Sunday, June 8, 2003.  In the interview, Salem gave his opinion on the main characters in the book.
