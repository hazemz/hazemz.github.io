209th Issue
May 25, 2003

 

On his first visit to Amman after the engagement

Family and Friends Meet Ammar

 

 

 

 

Ammar arrived in Amman on Thursday, May 22, on his first visit to Amman after his engagement to Dana last February.  On this occasion, family and friends gathered at Marwan's for dinner on Saturday, May 24, 2003.  Ammar's sister, Rita, who also arrived in Amman from Dubai on Thursday, attended the event.

Samer Returns Home

After completing his third year at Tulane University in New Orleans, Louisiana, Samer returned home on Wednesday, May 21, 2003.

Harran and Reema Return from France,

Ziad from the US, Hassan from Lebanon

      

After making a short trip to Beirut and Zahleh, Hassan returned home on Thursday, May 22.  On the other hand, Ziad, who made a weeklong visit to Boston and Orlando, returned home on Friday, May 23.  Harran and Reema, who left to France last week, also returned home on Saturday, May 24, 2003.

Deena and Salem Camp in Ajloun

 

Deena and Salem spent the weekend in Ajloun on a school camp.  They left Amman on Friday, May 16, 2003, and returned the next day.  This comes one week after Dalia and Yacoub S.'s camp, which was also in Ajloun.

  • Amer and Leanne left to Aqaba on Friday, May 23, and returned to Amman on Saturday, May 24.  What should be mentioned is that Jordan will be celebrating its Independence Day on Sunday, May 25, a national holiday in the Kingdom.
