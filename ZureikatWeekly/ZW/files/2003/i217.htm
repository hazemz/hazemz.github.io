Zureikats Gather at Amal's Celebrating Reem's Graduation

 

Zureikats gathered at Amal and Ziad's for dinner on Tuesday, July 15, 2003, to celebrate Dr. Reem's graduation from the St. George's Hospital Medical School.  The dinner came one week after Reem returned home, along with Talal and three guests.

 

 

Zureikats and Daouds Gather at Abla's

 

Zureikats, along with Mr. Fahhad and Mrs. Ayda Daoud, Ammar's parents, gathered at Abla Z. and Basil's for dinner on Saturday, July 19, 2003.

Less than 2 Weeks Left for the Big Day

Zureikats pack their bags as the family prepares for another wedding

Abla leaves Tuesday, Marwan and Hazem on Friday,
Tarek on Saturday; more Zureikats to follow next week

******************************

******************************

As Dana and Ammar's wedding draws nearer, several Zureikats are preparing to travel to Washington, DC for the big day.  Abla, the mother of the bride, will be the first to leave.  She is expected to depart on Tuesday, July 22, followed by Marwan and Hazem on Friday, July 25, then Tarek on Saturday, July 26, 2003.  Mona, Amal, Reema, and Alia have so far confirmed that they will be attending the wedding.  They, along with other guests traveling from abroad, are expected to arrive in DC next week.

 

Ammar Returns from Las Vegas

After spending the weekend in Las Vegas with his brothers and friends in his last two weeks as a bachelor, Ammar returned to Washington, DC on Sunday, July 20, 2003.

Kareem Accepted at the University of Bristol

Kareem has officially been accepted to study law at the University of Bristol in the UK starting next semester.  In an e-mail he sent to the Editor in Chief shortly after receiving the news of his acceptance, Kareem said, "Your cousin is now formally a law student at the University of Bristol, ranked 6 overall for law!...This is the moment I have been dreaming of all my life.  I am going to Aqaba to celebrate, and when I come back...TIHLAYEH for everyone."  Kareem left to Aqaba on Thursday, July 17, and returned to Amman on Saturday, July 19, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Kareem on his acceptance and wish him all the best in the future.

Hassan Meets Dian and the Children in Beirut

Hassan left to Beirut on Saturday, July 12, 2003, to meet Dian and the children, who spent the last few weeks in Aleppo, Syria.  Hassan and the family are expected to return home soon.

Mansaf for the Zureikat Youngsters at Harran's

 

Harran and Reema hosted the Zureikat youngsters on a mansaf on Wednesday, July 16, 2003.

Zureikats Celebrate 2 Birthdays

 

Zureikats celebrated Bashar's birthday on Friday, July 18, and Dian's birthday on Sunday, July 20, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Bashar and Dian very happy birthdays and all the best.
