Amer and Leanne Reschedule to June 6

Church service to be held at 4pm, followed by a reception, then a dinner at the Hyatt

Amer and Leanne have rescheduled their wedding to Friday, June 6, 2003.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Alia revealed the schedule for the June 6 wedding.  "The actual church ceremony is to take place at 4pm," she said.  "There will be a reception/cocktail party from 6-8pm at the Harran Household, followed by an intimate dinner party for the family and very close friends at the 'Sawani', in the Grand Hyatt Hotel."  In her e-mail, Alia also said, "Salem to walk down the aisle, along with Nour Ayyoub (Khalo Ramez's daughter) and a cousin of Leanne.  The kids have already started trying out their outfits, which are being sewed!...The Harran Household would like to officially invite each and every Zureikat to the wedding."

Zureikats in Sweden Celebrate Easter

Mona, Gero, and Dag celebrated Easter with Dag's best friend's family

Sylvia celebrates Easter on the Alps...
Helene in western Sweden

Zureikats in Sweden celebrated Easter on Sunday, April 20, 2003.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, sent on Easter morning, Mona said, "Today is Easter in Sweden, and we are celebrating it with Dag and his best friend family.  We will have lamb steak for lunch and waraq dawali and tabbouleh."  Regarding Sylvia and Helene's plans for Easter, Mona said, "Helene is with friends in western Sweden.  Sylvia just called us...She said she has a day off today, and, since yesterday evening she left with some friends to the Alp Mountains between Austria and Germany, she was about to get up and look for Easter eggs during her mountains walk."  Meanwhile, Zureikats in Jordan celebrated Palm Sunday on April 20.  Regarding this, Mona said, "Today is the Palm Sunday in Amman, and I still remember the Palm kvist (sha´ninah) which my mother used to make for us children in the old days, going to the Orthodox church on the side of Amman river in downtown.  Those were the days."  What should be mentioned is that Zureikats in Jordan will be gathering in Umm Al-Amad to celebrate Easter next Sunday.

Ayman Invited to Dinner with Dean

Hazem Awarded Prize in French Studies

 

Ayman, who is currently in his second year at Tufts University in Boston, Massachusetts, has been invited to dinner with the Dean of Engineering at Tufts for being on the Dean's List for two consecutive years. The dinner will be held on Tuesday, April 29.  On the other hand, Hazem, who is currently in his first year at Macalester College in St. Paul, Minnesota, has been awarded a prize for Excellence in French Studies (Level 1) by the French Department at Macalester.  The prize will be officially awarded to Hazem on Tuesday, May 6, 2003.  Hazem is currently taking the French II course after taking French I last semester.

Tarek Hosts Family and Friends at Barbeque

Tarek hosted about twenty family members and friends at a barbeque in Umm Al-Amad on Thursday (April 17) night.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Alia, who was among those invited, said, "I had the privilege of being invited to Umm Al-Amad for a barbeque hosted by Tarek on Thursday night.  Bashar and Bana also came, along with another 20 or so of Tarek's friends.  It was hilarious.  We all enjoyed ourselves.  Tarek really outdid himself!  Abu Marwan is the host of all hosts!  The atmosphere, .the food, the hospitality, all was truly amazing!  Above all, however, Abu Marwan was the one who really shined in RED, with his "you'll never walk alone" Liverpool t-shirt!!"
