Hassan Returns Home after Undergoing Catheterization

Hassan underwent catheterization on Saturday, March 8, 2003, and returned home on the same day.  According to Harran, the catheterization (qastara) was successful, and "all is well".  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Hassan a speedy recovery and all the best.

Mona Returns to Sweden after Attending Book Fair in Oman

Sylvia spent a weekend with Gero during Mona's trip

Mona sends her regards to Dana and Ammar

Mona returned to Sweden after spending ten days in Oman where she attended the Omar Book Fair.  Mona left Sweden on Monday, February 24, and returned on Thursday, March 6, 2003.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Mona said, "It was very beautiful, and the people were very nice.  I was both on TV and the radio, and a Swedish flag flew outside the book fair high up in the sky to give a mark that Sweden is participating.  It looked very thrilling.  Sylvia came over in my absence to stay over the weekend with Gero, and she had a good time in seeing all her old friends.  Dag picked me up from the airport, and we all gathered on a nice evening dinner prepared by Gero."  In her e-mail, Mona also sent her regards to Dana and Ammar.  "We look forward to seeing them both in Sweden soon," she said.

Tarek Starts Part-Time Job at Houston's

Last week, Tarek helped out by getting things out of the fridge...
Today, Tarek can prepare his very own Nachos!

Besides his job at JCE, Tarek started a part-time, evening job at Houston's Restaurant.  Tarek is currently working in the kitchen.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Tarek said that, last week, he used to help out by getting meat and chicken out of the fridge, but, today, he can prepare his very own Nachos!  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Tarek all the best in his new 'endeavor'.

A few weeks after returning from the US

Reem Sends an E-mail from London

"I'm now working at Maidstone Hospital which is in a tiny town...in Kent"

Reem congratulates Marwan on the 2002 Ministry of Public Works Award

The sunset in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico.  Reem took this picture during her visit to Mexico last month.

Reem sent an e-mail from London a few weeks after returning from the US.  In her e-mail, Reem said, "Well, I'm back in London now, not at all used to the weather.  I had a great time abroad and gained lots of experience at Baylor in Texas.  I would like to congratulate Khalo Marwan on being awarded the 2002 'Ministry of Public Works' Award for a distinguished project.  I'm now working at Maidstone Hospital which is in a tiny town in the middle of nowhere in Kent.  It's really a good experience, but I think I prefer the bustle of the city."

Zureikats Celebrate Yacoub's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Yacoub Z.'s birthday on Saturday, March 8, 2003.  On this occasion, a small celebration, attended by some family members and friends, was held at Hassan's on Saturday afternoon.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Yacoub a very happy birthday and all the best.
