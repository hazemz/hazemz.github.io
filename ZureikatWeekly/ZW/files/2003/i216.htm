Accompanied by three guests

Reem and Talal Return Home

Several members of the family gathered at Houston's celebrating Reem's graduation

 

Reem and Talal returned home on Tuesday, July 8, accompanied by three guests.  A few days later, Marwan and Abla hosted a small gathering celebrating Reem's graduation from St. George's.  The event, which was held at Houston's Restaurant on Saturday, July 12, 2003, was attended by Samer, Ayman, Talal, Alia, Tarek, Bana, Hazem, and the three guests.

Hala Attends Conference in Belgium

Hala left on Tuesday and returned home on Friday

Hala returned home after attending the Euro-Med Audiovisual Conference in Brussels, Belgium.  She left Amman on Tuesday, July 8, and returned on Friday, July 11, 2003.

Zureikats Celebrate Nadine's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Nadine's birthday on Friday, July 11, 2003.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Nadine a very happy birthday and all the best.

  • Abu Harran, Marwan, Abla, and Hazem spent Friday, July 11, 2003, in Umm Al-Amad.
