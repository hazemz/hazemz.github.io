Family and Friends in Jordan Celebrate Dana and Ammar's Wedding

A reception was held at Marwan and Abla's on Friday

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Family and friends in Jordan celebrated Dana and Ammar's wedding at a reception held at Marwan and Abla's on Friday, September 26, 2003.  This comes as Dana and Ammar are currently visiting Jordan for the first time after they got married last month.

 

Wasef Aazar Hosts Dinner in Honor of Dana and Ammar

Several family members and friends attended the dinner on Sunday

 

 

 

Wasef Aazar, Ammar's mother's brother-in-law, hosted a dinner on Sunday, September 28, 2003, in honor of Dana and Ammar who are currently visiting Jordan.  The dinner was attended by a number of family members and friends from the Daoud and Zureikat families and from the Muasher family (Ammar's mother's side).  Zureikat family and friends who attended include Harran, Ziad, Dr. Yousef and Mrs. Shadia Nusseir, and Dr. Shabib Ammari.

 

Ammar Leaves Tuesday...Dana on Friday

Ammar will be returning to Washington, DC on Tuesday, September 30, while Dana will be leaving on Friday, October 3, 2003.

Kareem Leaves to Bristol to Start his First Year

Reema accompanies Kareem, returns Tuesday

Accompanied by Reema, Kareem left to the UK on Thursday, September 25, to start his first year as a law student at the University of Bristol.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Alia said, "Kareem is doing well and really loves the city, says it's even better than London!!"  Reema is expected to return to Amman on Tuesday, September 30, 2003.

Amal in Texas after Visiting California

After spending about one week in San Francisco, California, Amal left to Houston, Texas on Thursday, September 25.  She is expected to return to Amman on Thursday, October 2, 2003.

Hassan and Ziad Return from Lebanon

 

Hassan returned from a business trip to Beirut on Saturday, September 27.  He had earlier left Amman on Friday, September 26.  On the other hand, Ziad, who made a separate trip, also to Beirut, to attend a conference, left Amman on Wednesday, September 24, and returned on Sunday, September 28, 2003.

Sylvia Returns to Vienna after 2-Day Visit to Sweden

Sylvia made a two-day visit to Sweden this weekend.  She arrived on Friday, September 26, and returned to Vienna on Sunday, September 28, 2003.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief (sent on Saturday), Mona said, "On Sunday, the family will be having lunch here...all of the Hennings, and all will help, I hope, to collect the falling autumn leaves of the garden.  There will be some apple pie (homemade) of our own apples.  You are all welcome to have a tea in the afternoon."  In her e-mail, Mona also said, "I talked to Hala yesterday, and it seems she is already in  the academic life of Washington and made fantastic contacts.  Many journeys inside the US are waiting for her as part of her research.  I am really proud of my little sister."
