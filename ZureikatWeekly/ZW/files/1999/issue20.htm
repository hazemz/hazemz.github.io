Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

20th Issue

Friday, September 3, 1999

Ziad's Leg Still Swollen...Doctors' Decision Thursday

Amal: Ziad is still taking a heavy dose of antibiotics

Two weeks after Ziad's accident, Amal told The Zureikat Weekly that Ziad's leg is still swollen and that the doctors will decide Thursday whether to open Ziad's leg to drain the abscess.  Amal also said that Ziad is cutting down on work because of his injury.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Ziad a speedy recovery.

99/2000 Academic Year Starts in Amman

    

After a two and a half month summer vacation, Hazem, Sabine, Nadine, Yacoub Z., Dalia and Deena returned to school to start the 1999/2000 scholastic year.  However, Samer, Ayman, Talal, Kareem and Salem will be going to school on September 4.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish all the Zureikat students the best in this academic year and especially Omar and Samer who are starting their final year in school.

Mona in Greece...Sylvia Returns to Vienna Next Week

Mona, Gero and Sylvia arrived in Greece last Sunday to spend a week there.  In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly before leaving Stockholm, Mona said, "The vacation in Greece will do me good.  I am really looking forward to it."  Mona also said that Sylvia will be going back to Vienna to continue her work in a hospital there on September 7, 1999.

After visiting Australia, Singapore, Thailand and Malaysia

Reem Returns to London

Amal told The Zureikat Weekly that Reem flew from Bangkok to London on September 1 and will arrive in London on September 2 after spending a few hours in Frankfort, Germany.

Hiba Andeel Starts a Visit to Jordan

Hiba Andeel, Dana's friend from Egypt, arrived in Amman on Saturday, August 28.  Hiba will be spending almost ten days in Amman.  Dana and Hiba are currently touring some places in Amman.

Talal Attends Football Final at 9th Arab Games

    

Pictures from the final football match between Jordan and Iraq

Talal attended the final football match in the 9th Pan Arab Games which were held in Jordan this year between Jordan and Iraq.  The game ended with Jordan's dramatic win over Iraq 7/5 after a penalty shootout.
