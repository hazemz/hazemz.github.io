Zureikats Remember

Umm Harran

Mona: I miss her very much.  I miss her voice on the phone and her warm hug when I used to come home.  Amman to me will never be the same!  My tears are still fresh and her loss still hurts as if it is yesterday.  She gave lots of things, one of them is determination.  She was very ambitious and her ambition is in every one of us.  I will always honor you UMM HARRAN!

On July 30, 1997, Umm Harran passed away.  In 1998, one year after her death, the family built a gazebo in a church in Ajloun in memory of Umm Harran.  Now, two years after her sad death, the family will be gathering in this church in Ajloun to remember the late Umm Harran.  The family will then go to Jerash to have lunch there.  More details and pictures in the 16th issue.

Zureikats Pay Condolences to Reema's Uncle

Reema's uncle, Tawfiq Ayoub, died on Monday, July 26, 1999, in a hospital in Amman.  Many Zureikats attended the funeral and paid their condolences to the Ayoub family.

Marwan Now Fine After Fever

Marwan is now fine after having a fever a few days ago.  Marwan didn't go to work for one day.

Reem Visits the "Insightful and Cosmic" Kuala Lumpur

Reem also visited Singapore and is currently in Australia

After starting her far-eastern trip last week, Reem sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly.  In her e-mail, Reem said, "I've been to Kuala Lumpur and Singapore, very beautiful city, insightful and cosmic, and now I'm in Cairns, Australia.  It's very pretty here.  Tomorrow, I'll be going on a jungle tour, where I'll be snorkeling and swimming and getting to know the jungle!"  Reem also sent her  love to the family.

Dana to Return Friday Morning

Dana, who attended a summer course at AUC, will be returning home from Cairo on Friday, July 30, 1999.

Lara Zureikat updates us on the latest in her family

Lara Ghaleb Zureikat updated us with the latest news in her family.  Lara said that she will be celebrating her first anniversary in Amman in September and that she is currently working on a small landscape practice.  Lara also said that Samer, her brother, is working between Algeria, Yemen and Iraq.
