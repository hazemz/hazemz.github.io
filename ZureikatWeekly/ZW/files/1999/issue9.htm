Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

9th Issue

Friday, June 18, 1999

Hala Returns Home

Family members welcome Hala at Abu Harran's

Another family dinner to be held in Umm Al-Amad

Naief: Hala may resume working at JTV next Saturday

Exclusive interview with Hala and family next week

Hala, Naief, Dalia, Deena and Salem returned to Amman, Jordan on Thursday, June10, 1999 after spending almost one year in Birmingham, Alabama in the United States of America.  Family members welcomed Hala at Abu Harran's house in a a big family dinner.  Hala and the kids spent the first night in Amman at Abu Harran's and  returned home the next day.  A source told The Zureikat Weekly that there will be another family dinner in Umm Al-Amad on Thursday, June 17, 1999.   The Zureikat Weekly will be having an exclusive interview with Hala and her family next week.  In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Naief said that Hala may resume working in Jordan Television next Saturday, June 19.

In a phone call with the Editor in Chief

Mona: Gero and Helene are Now in Frankfort

"I will be publishing nine new books"

Editor in Chief talking to Mona on the phone

Mona called the Editor in Chief, Hazem Zureikat, on Tuesday, June 15.  In the phone call, Mona said that Gero and Helene arrived in Frankfort, Germany on Tuesday, June 15, after leaving Stockholm on Sunday, June 13.   Helene will be working in a Swedish bank in Frankfort this summer and will study in Milan, Italy next winter.  Gero accompanied Helene to help her furnish her small room there.  He will be returning to Sweden on Saturday, June 19.  Mona also said that she will be publishing nine new books and gave her regards to the family.

Zureikats Finish Exams...Summer Holiday Starts

Samer and Kareem were the last to finish their exams

Omar prepares for (Tawjeehi) next year

The summer holiday has officially started for all Zureikat students.  Yacoub S., Sabine, Nadine and Yacoub Z. started their holiday last week.  Meanwhile, Omar and Hazem finished their finals on Monday, June 14, Ayman and Talal finished on June 15 and Samer and Kareem finished their exams on Wednesday, June 16, 1999.  Omar and Samer will be starting their final year in school in a few months.

Bana to sing at the S.O.S and the Royal Cultural Center

Abla: A Star is Born

Samia Ghannoum: She has a promising voice

"A star is born."  This is what Abla Z. told The Zureikat Weekly in a phone call.  She said that many people, including the well-known Samia Ghannoum, said that Bana had a "great and promising voice".  Bana will be singing a duet with Ala' Zureikat, the son of Sami Zureikat, in the S.O.S Village in Amman on June 25, 1999.  Later in mid-July, Bana will be singing along with a choir at the Royal Cultural Center in Amman.

Abla Speaks at a Eulogy for Wadad Bolus

A eulogy for Wadad Bolus, the first Arabic principal for the Ahlieh School for Girls, who died six months ago, was held at the Ahlieh School in Amman.  Abla N., on behalf of the alumni club, spoke at the eulogy about all the things Bolus had done for the school.

Hassan and family spend two days at the Dead Sea

Dian: It was a Great Trip

"We still don't know when we'll be going to Syria"

On Saturday, June 12, Hassan and his family went to the Movenpick Hotel & Resort in the Dead Sea.  They all returned to Amman on Sunday, June 13.  In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Dian said, "it was a great trip."  Regarding Hassan's family's trip to Syria, Dian said, "we still don't know when we'll be going to Syria.  We'll inform you as soon as we know."

Dana Returns to Cairo

Dana in the airport before leaving Amman

After spending almost two weeks in Amman, Dana returned to Cairo on Saturday, June 12, 1999.  Dana will be attending a summer course at the AUC and is expected to return to Amman in July or August.

Basil and Bashar Return from Aqaba After Short Trip

Basil, accompanied by Bashar, went to Aqaba on a business trip on Tuesday, June 15.  They both returned to Amman on Wednesday, June 16, 1999.  Basil told The Zureikat Weekly that the weather in Aqaba was very very hot.

Abla to Spend Two Days in Cyprus

Abla N. will be going to Cyprus on a business trip on June 21.  Abla told The Zureikat Weekly that she will be returning to Amman from this brief visit on June 22, 1999.

Amer to Return June 19

Alia told The Zureikat Weekly that Amer will be returning to Amman on Saturday, June 19, 1999.

Abla, Bana and Omar Have Lunch at Marwan's House

Abla, Bana, Omar, Marwan, Abla and Hazem eating lunch at Marwan's

Abla Z., Bana and Omar had lunch at Marwan's house on Tuesday, June 15, 1999.  Later that day, Omar, Ayman, Talal and Hazem went to eat dinner at Houston's Restaurant.

End of 9th Issue
