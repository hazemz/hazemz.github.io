Zureikats Celebrate Hassan's Birthday

The family had dinner at Hassan's house on August 9

  

Hassan and Abu Harran                                               Basil and Marwan

  

The cakes

On August 9, Zureikats celebrated Hassan's birthday.  The family had dinner at Hassan's house on that day.  Abu Harran, Harran, Reema, Kareem, Marwan, Abla N., Dana, Hazem, Basil, Abla Z. and Basil's brother's wife attended the dinner.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to wish Hassan a happy birthday.

Zureikats Wish Abla a Speedy Recovery

Ziad: Abla has a bacterial infection

After making a blood test and taking some injections, Abla N. is currently at home recovering from a bacterial infection.  Ziad told The Zureikat Weekly that Abla will be fine in a few days.  Zureikats wished Abla N. a speedy recovery.

August 11...Amal's Anniversary and Eclipse Day

August 11, 1999 wasn't only the solar eclipse day.  Zureikats also celebrated Amal and Ziad's 23rd wedding anniversary.  The family called Amal and Ziad and wished them a happy anniversary.  Later in the evening, Amal, Ziad, Samer, Ayman and Talal went to have dinner together.

Amal and Samer Return from Beirut

Source: Amal might be going to the States next month

  

After attending an opera in Beirut, Lebanon, Amal and Samer returned home on August 9, 1999.  Amal told The Zureikat Weekly that this was the first time she spent a long time there, so she got the opportunity to tour Lebanon and enjoy it.  A source also told The Zureikat Weekly that Amal might be going to the States in mid-September to attend a conference there.

Shomalies Gather at Basil's

Basil's sister left for Tunisia after spending almost one day in Amman

Shomalies from Beit Sahour in Palestine gathered at Basil's house.  Majdi, Basil's brother, his wife and their children came to Amman last week.  Rose, Basil sister, spent almost one day in Amman then left for Tunisia where she will be attending a conference.

Reem Returns from a Three-Day Cruise

"Give my love to Samer and wish him a happy birthday"

Reem, who is currently touring the Far East, sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly.  In her e-mail, Reem said, "I hope teta's anniversary lunch was ok.  Give my love to the family.  I have just returned from a three-day cruise on the Whitsunday Islands.  The beaches there are beautiful and untouched.  I also went skydiving.  That was an amazing experience!  Give my love to Samer and wish him a happy birthday, and also Dana."
