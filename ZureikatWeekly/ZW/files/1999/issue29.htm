Accompanying ANS sports teams

Editor in Chief Visits Lebanon Next Week

As the Vice President of Amman National School's Student Council, Hazem will be accompanying his school's sports teams in their four-day trip to Beirut, Lebanon next week.  Hazem is expected to leave Amman on Wednesday, November 10, and return on Sunday, November 14.  Details about the trip's schedule in the next issue.

Editor in Chief receives an e-mail from Edinburgh, Scotland

Tarek: Send my Love to Zureikats All Around

"Thanks for doing an amazing job with the magazine"

After his recent objection, the Editor in Chief received an e-mail from Tarek.  In his e-mail, Tarek said, "I apologize for not providing you with any information for the magazine.  You have every right to be angry.  I promise you I'll do my part from now on.  I think what you are doing is great for the family, and I hope that last message you sent will wake everybody up."  Tarek also sent his latest news in his e-mail.  "I'm spending my next weekend in Bristol for an awards banquet," he said.  "It won't be ours as we are having our own awards banquet in Edinburgh the week after.  However, in Bristol, there is a big "Black Tie Ball" (i.e. must wear a tuxedo) after the awards ceremony.  Please send my love to the Zuriekats all around, and thanks for doing an amazing job with the magazine."

Alia Sends a Short Message to The Zureikat Weekly

"I'm the new disabled representative at Royal Holloway"

The Editor in Chief received a brief message from Alia last week.  In her message, Alia said, "I'm the new disabled representative at Royal Holloway.  I'll send you the details in an e-mail later."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Alia on the great news.

Hassan and Dian in London

    

Hassan and Dian leaved for London on Monday, November 1, 1999, on a business trip for Hassan.  They are both expected to return to Amman on Saturday, November 6.

After postponing his trip a few days

Ziad Attends a Conference in Abu Dhabi

After postponing his trip from Sunday to Thursday, Ziad left Amman for Abu Dhabi on November 4 to attend a conference there.  Sources told The Zureikat Weekly that Ziad will be spending "a couple of days" in the UAE.
