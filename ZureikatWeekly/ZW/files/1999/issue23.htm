Mona to Visit Amman Soon

Abla: We're expecting Mona on October 1 or 2

Abla Z. told The Zureikat Weekly that Mona is expected to visit Amman on October 1 or 2, 1999.  Details in the next issue.

Tarek to Return Home October 3

Tarek will be spending almost ten days in Amman

Tarek is expected to arrive in Amman to spend almost ten days on October 3, 1999.  Tarek, who is currently working in the US, is expected to return to Edinburgh after his short vacation in Amman to start his third year there.

Amal Starts a Two Week Trip to the US and London

Amal left Amman on Monday, September 20, to visit the United States and England.  Before leaving, Amal told The Zureikat Weekly, "I'll be leaving for London to spend one night there.  Then, I'll go to Washington to attend a conference."  "Before leaving for London again to spend a few days with Reem," Amal added, "I'll be going to Houston for a few days."  Amal also said that she'll be returning home immediately if anything changes regarding Talal's UWC scholarship.

Hazem Prepares for Campaign...Elections on the 29th

S'heimat, Hijazi, Malhas and Diab will be running against Hazem

Hazem's campaign logo, "Lazem Hazem", which means "you have to vote for Hazem".

The elections for Amman National School's Student Council will be held on Wednesday, September 29, 1999.  Hazem, who is in 10th grade, will be running for Vice President this year against Omar S'heimat (11th grade), Masa Hijazi (11th grade), Jana Malhas (12th grade) and Omar Diab (12th grade).  The election campaigns will start on Saturday, September 25, and all nominees for President and Vice President, including Hazem, will be delivering their speeches on Tuesday, September 28, a day before the elections.
