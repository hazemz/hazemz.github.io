Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

31st Issue

Friday, December 3, 1999

Family prepares for the return of Zureikats studying abroad

Tarek to Visit the Canary Islands

Dana, Amer, Alia, Reem and Talal return in Mid-December

In an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Tarek said that he will be visiting the Canary Islands between December 13 and December 20, 1999 after winning an award.  Tarek also added that he will be returning to Amman on December 21.  On the other hand, The Zureikat Weekly has learned that Dana will be returning home sometime in mid-December, and that Talal will return on December 11, Amer and Reem will return on December 18, and Alia on December 20.

Reem E-mails her Latest News

"I've been working for the past ten weeks in a hospital...I really am enjoying medicine; it's quite interesting meeting new patients with new problems everyday"

In her first e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly in a long time, Reem said, "I know its been a long time since I wrote, but I've been working for the past ten weeks in a hospital in the middle of nowhere!!  I really am enjoying medicine; it's quite interesting meeting new patients with new problems everyday."  In her e-mail, Reem also said, "I've choreographed some dances for a play that was on two weeks ago.  We also held auditions for a fashion show that a friend and I are organizing in February, where all the money we raise goes to charity (hopefully if all goes well we are hoping to raise around £4000).  Yesterday, we also held auditions for a musical, (a friend and I are choreographing that as well).  I've also been elected to represent my year (3rd year rep), which basically means that I am the connection between my year and the rest of the consultants, sorting out their problems, etc."  Regarding her father's latest visit to London on his way back from Italy, Reem said, "my dad came to meet me for the evening on the 18th.  It was a lovely dinner, and it was really nice to catch up on  family news."

Zureikats Celebrate Marwan's Birthday

The family is expected to gather at Marwan's house on Thursday

Zureikats will be celebrating Marwan's 54th birthday on Thursday, December 2, 1999.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Marwan all the best.  A source told The Zureikat Weekly that the family will be gathering at Marwan's house on Thursday night to celebrate his birthday.  Details of this family gathering in the next issue.

Hassan Returns from Egypt Friday

On Wednesday, December 1, Hassan left for Egypt on a business trip.  Dian told The Zureikat Weekly that Hassan will be returning home on Friday, December 3, 1999.

Harran and Reema Return from Lebanon

  

After spending a few days in Amman upon their arrival from Cairo last week, Harran and Reema left for Beirut on a business trip for Harran on Friday, November 26.  They both returned to Amman on Sunday, November 28, 1999.
