Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

8th Issue

Friday, June 11, 1999

Special family dinner to be held at Abu Harran's

Preparations Underway for Hala's Return

Hala will arrive on Thursday at 18:10 Jordan Local Time

"People started to come and get their things from our apartment"

"We sold our van today...everything is just going smooth"

Hala congratulates Amer on earning his diploma

After spending almost one year in Birmingham, Alabama in the United States of America, Hala, Naief, Dalia, Deena and Salem will be returning home to Amman on Thursday, June 10, 1999 at 6:10 PM Jordan Local Time by Air France (flight #682).  A special family dinner will be held at Abu Harran's house celebrating Hala's return.  During the last week, Zureikats prepared for this joyful event.  In her final e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly from Birmingham, Alabama sent on June 5, Hala said, "I think we will finish our packing tonight, then I might have two days to relax.  People started to come and get their things from our apartment."   Hala also said, "We sold our van today and thanks God everything is going just smooth."  In another e-mail, Hala congratulated Amer on earning his diploma.   "Please convey our congratulations to Amer and his parents.  We were very happy to hear that," she said.

Nabil Ammari Dies at 54

Ammari had a close relation with many Zureikats

Nabil Ammari, a very good friend of the Zureikat family, died at 54 in the United States of America after going there to undergo treatment in May.  Ammari served as minister of planning and the king's economic advisor.  Zureikats paid their condolences to the Ammari family and some will be attending the funeral on Thursday, June 10.

Summer Holiday Closer as Zureikats Continue Finals

Bana already finished her exams on June 7

Zureikat students continued their final exams for the 2nd term of the academic year 1998-1999.  Bana, who studies at the Jordan University, finished her exams on Monday, June 7.  Bashar will be finishing on Friday, June 11.   Hazem and Omar, who study at Amman National School, will finish their final exams on Monday, June 14, 1999.  Kareem will start his exams on Saturday, June 12, Ayman and Talal will finish around June 15, and Samer, who started on June 7, will finish around June 16.  Wednesday, June 9, was a national holiday in Jordan celebrating King Abdullah's accession to the throne as well as Thursday, June 10, which was the Arab Revolt and Army Day.

Tarek to Travel to the US on June 16

Tarek finished his exams on Friday, June 4

Tarek will be leaving for the US from Edinburgh to work in San Diego on Wednesday, June 16, 1999.  Tarek had finished his final exams on Friday, June 4.

Dana to Return to Cairo June 12

Dana will be attending a summer course and will return home in July

Dana, who returned to Amman on June 2, 1999, will be going back to Cairo on Saturday, June 12 to attend a summer course at AUC.  Dana is expected to return to Amman for a month in July.

Amal to travel to Morocco on the 24th

Amal: I'll Be Attending a Wedding for the Ex-P.M's Son

Amal told The Zureikat Weekly that she will be traveling to Morocco on June 24 to attend the wedding of the son of Morocco's ex-prime minister.  Amal said that she will be returning on June 30.

In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly

Dian: We Will Be Going to Syria Soon

Nadine and Yacoub finished school...Sabine has one exam left

In a phone call with The Zureikat Weekly, Dian said that she will be going with Hassan and the children to the Dead Sea in a few days.   Dian also said that Nadine and Yacoub finished school and Sabine has her last exam on Saturday, June 12.  Hassan's children study at the Modern School in Amman.   Regarding Hassan's family's expected trip to Syria, Dian said that they will be going soon.

Reem to Return July 1

Amal told The Zureikat Weekly that Reem may return to Jordan on July 1, 1999.  Reem is currently studying in the United Kingdom.

Abla, Omar and Yacoub Watch Coronation at Marwan's

Wednesday, June 9 was a national holiday in Jordan

Wednesday, June 9, 1999 was a national holiday in Jordan celebrating King Abdullah's accession to the throne.  Abla Z., Omar and Yacoub watched the coronation celebration in Marwan's house.  They, along with Marwan, Dana and Hazem, went to watch King Abdullah and Queen Rania on a motorcade in the 4th circle.

Ziad to Attend a Conference in Brussels

A source told The Zureikat Weekly that Ziad will be attending a conference in Brussels, Belgium from June 18 till June 24, 1999.  The source asked not to be identified.

Marwan and Family have Lunch with Friends and Family Last Week

Marwan, Abla, Dana and Hazem had lunch with Abla N.'s family in a restaurant in Amman on Friday, June 4, 1999.  They also had lunch with some friends at Marwan's house on Tuesday, June 8.

Lama Bashour Returns to Lebanon

Lama Bashour, Abla N.'s niece from Lebanon, who came to Amman on June 1, returned to Beirut on Sunday, June 6, after camping in Dana, a place in southern Jordan.  On the other hand, Hazem will be going to Lebanon to attend Lama's brother's graduation in the beginning of July.

End of 8th Issue
