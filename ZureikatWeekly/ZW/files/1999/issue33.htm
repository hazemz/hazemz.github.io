Friday, December 17, 1999

Zureikats gather at Marwan's celebrating their return

Dana, Tarek, Talal Return Home

Amer and Reem will return on Saturday...Alia on Monday

              

Dana, Tarek, and Talal returned to Amman last week.  Dana, who was supposed to return the same day Tarek did, surprised everyone and returned a day before, Monday, December 13, 1999.  Tarek returned on Tuesday, December 14, and Talal returned on Saturday, December 11.  Zureikats gathered at Marwan's on Tuesday, December 14, to celebrate the return of Dana, Tarek, and Talal.  Harran, Reema, Kareem, Hassan, Dian, Sabine, Nadine, Yacoub Z., Amal, Ziad, Ayman, Talal, Abla Z., Basil, Bashar, Bana, Omar, Yacoub S., Hala, Naief, Mrs. Nabila Lababidi, Rana Lababidi, and Rasha Lababidi attended the gathering.  Amer and Reem are also expected to return to Amman on Saturday, December 18.  Another dinner will be held at Amal's on that day.  Alia is expected to return home on Monday, December 20.

One Week Left for Christmas

Christmas Eve at Harran's....Christmas Day at Marwan's

With just one week left for Christmas, Zureikats are now preparing for this occasion with Christmas trees and decorations.  A source told The Zureikat Weekly that there won't be any changes this year; the family will be gathering at Harran's house for Christmas Eve next Friday, December 24, and at Marwan's for Christmas Day on December 25, 1999.

Helene Visits Amman on December 28

Helene's friend will be accompanying her in her one week trip

After booking the flight tickets, Helene sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly late Thursday and confirmed her visit to Jordan this month.  In her e-mail Helene said that she, along with her friend Louise Kristiansson, will be leaving Sweden on December 27 and will arrive in Amman on Tuesday, December 28, 1999, at 2:20 AM.  Helene also said that she and her friend will be returning to Sweden on Tuesday, January 4, 2000.
