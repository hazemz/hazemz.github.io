The Zureikat Weekly...

or Biweekly ??

Editor in Chief receives only one response to his recent objection

Helene: What about printing the newspaper every second week instead?

Hazem to send questionnaire to subscribers soon

Following is the message that the Editor in Chief of The Zureikat Weekly wrote in the 27th issue:

"As you can see, this issue of The Zureikat Weekly is almost blank.  The Editor in Chief wishes to voice an objection against the lack of cooperation from all the Zureikats who failed to provide The Zureikat Weekly with any news.  The Zureikat Weekly is your magazine and can only continue with your support and cooperation."

After his objection, the Editor in Chief received only one response from Helene.  In her e-mail, Helene said, "I always find it very amusing to read your web paper.  I understand that it can be very difficult to get our family to come up with news every week (I myself have not written very often to you.  I am sorry about that).  What about printing the newspaper every second week instead?  During two weeks time, everyone will have more news to tell.  Now and then, you can always print a special edition if there are urgent news."  In her e-mail, Helene also included her latest news.  These news are included in this issue.  After Helene's suggestion, the Editor in Chief is expected to send the readers of The Zureikat Weekly a questionnaire soon.

Tarek Speaks in the First Event for the Arab Society

After winning the elections for president of the Arab Society in Edinburgh, Scotland last May, Tarek spoke in the first dinner for the Arab Society.  In his e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly, Tarek said, "I had a big day yesterday.  It was the Arabic Society's first event.  It was just a dinner, but it took a whole day of running around and advertising it to have enough people to come.  I even went to a few lectures and made announcements."

Ziad to Spend "a Couple of Days" in Abu Dhabi

A source told The Zureikat Weekly that Ziad will be going to Abu Dhabi on Sunday, October 31, 1999, to attend a conference there.  The source added that Ziad will only be spending a couple of days there.

The Zureikat Weekly receives news from Italy

Talal Visits Trieste During his "Long Weekend"

Talal, who left for Italy earlier this month, sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly last week.  In his e-mail, Talal said, "everything here is going great, and my roommate is ok.  Lately, I have become good friends with him.  He teaches me some Italian, and I teach him some Arabic."  In his e-mail, Talal also said that he may be visiting Trieste or another city in Italy this Saturday because he will be having a long, three-day weekend.

Ayman Witnesses Seeds of

Peace Inauguration in Jerusalem

Ayman returned to Amman on Friday, October 29, 1999, after making a brief visit to Jerusalem to witness the inauguration of the new Seeds of Peace building there.
