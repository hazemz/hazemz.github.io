Marwan Undergoes Cataract, Resumes Work

Marwan also underwent a minor tooth operation on Saturday

Marwan resumed work on Thursday, January 8, 2004, after undergoing a successful cataract operation on his right eye on Tuesday, January 6.  Marwan also underwent a minor tooth operation at Amal's clinic on Saturday, June 10.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Marwan a speedy recovery and all the best.  What should be mentioned is that Harran had also undergone a cataract operation on his right eye late last November.

Samer, Talal, Kareem Leave

   

After spending their Christmas breaks in Amman, Samer, Talal, and Kareem returned back to their universities last week.  Talal left to the UK on Monday, January 5, 2004; Kareem left to the UK on Wednesday, January 7; and Samer left to the US on Saturday, January 10.

Mona Returns from Qatar

Mona returned to Sweden on Wednesday, January 7, 2004, after participating in a book exhibition in Qatar.  Mona had left to Qatar on Saturday, December 27, 2003.

On her first visit to Jordan in many years

Sylvia Arrives in Amman on Wednesday

Sources told The Zureikat Weekly that Sylvia will be arriving in Amman on Wednesday, January 14, 2004, on her first visit to Jordan in many years.

Zureikats Celebrate Ammar's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Ammar's birthday on Monday, January 5, 2004.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Ammar a very happy birthday and all the best.

  • Zureikat school students in Jordan are currently working "diligently" for their final exams as the rest of the family rests after "the December festivities", as Abla Z. put it.

     

  • Sharaihas held a reception for Ziad on the occasion of his appointment as a member of the Jordanian Senate.  The reception was held at the Orthodox Club on Sunday, January 4, 2004.
