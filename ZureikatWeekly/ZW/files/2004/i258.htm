Dana Promoted to Senior Producer, Investigative Reporter

Dana has been promoted to Senior Producer at Al-Hurra.  She is now responsible for supervising regional reporters for the TV station around the world.  Dana was also promoted last week to an investigative reporter.  As part of her new job, Dana will be traveling to a number of US cities in the next few weeks.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Dana on her promotions, wishing her all the best.

After spending a few days in Amman and attending Mona's 60th birthday in Beirut

Talal Returns to the UK

After spending a few days in Amman and attending Mona's 60th birthday party in Beirut, Talal returned to the UK on Friday, April 30, 2004.  Talal, who is currently studying at the St. George's Hospital Medical School, had arrived in Amman on Wednesday, April 21.

Hala Speaks at UMD Business School

Hala also attended part of a session for the US Senate

Hala gave a presentation at the University of Maryland's Business School on Tuesday, April 27, 2004.  The presentation discussed issues of media and the freedom of speech in Jordan.  Hala's director called the presentation, which was part of the monthly Hubert Humphrey Forum at UMD, "the best among all" presentations given by the Humphrey fellows.  On the other hand, Hala and other Humphrey fellows made a field trip to the Court of Justice and the Congress on Thursday, April 29, during which they attended part of the US Senate's session on internet taxes.

Engicon Starts Moving to New Headquarters

First group to move Monday, others to follow in the coming weeks

The new Engicon building as it was being constructed.

A few months after the company changed its name from Jordanian Consulting Engineer Co. (JCE) to Engicon, the company's staff will start moving from the current office in Shmeisani to the new headquarters in Jabal Al-Lweibdeh on Monday, May 3, 2004.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Marwan, chairman of the company's board, said that the Water and Wastewater Department will move on Monday, while other departments will follow in the next three weeks.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Engicon on moving to its new headquarters, wishing the company all the best.

Abla N. at the inauguration ceremony.

Hill House Kindergarten Officially Inaugurated

The ceremony was patronized by Princess Alia Al-Faisal

Some of the attendants.

Hill House Kindergarten, whose co-owners are Abla N., Mr. Ayman Saket, and Mrs. Donna Naber, was officially inaugurated on Monday, April 26, 2004.  The inauguration ceremony was patronized by HRH Princess Alia Al-Faisal.  Zureikats who attended include Marwan, Tarek, Reema, and Dian, as well as Abla N.  What should be mentioned is that the kindergarten had opened its doors to the public at the start of the current academic year last September.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Abla N. and the Hill House Kindergarten on the inauguration, wishing them all the best.
