Sylvia Sends her Congratulations from Cameroon

Sylvia, who is currently volunteering in Cameroon, sent an e-mail to the Editor in Chief last week.  In her e-mail, Sylvia said, "I heard drums coming from Amman to Sweden and finally to Cameroon telling me that my beloved cousin Bashar is getting engaged, so I wanted to send him my best wishes and a lot of hugs."  Sylvia also updated us on the latest regarding her work in Cameroon.  "I have been here now for six weeks," she said.  "Time passed really quick.  I am working in a small hospital in the jungle, 1,500m up the hills [in the] northwestern side of Cameroon.  It is beautiful here and I am living with a lot of African catholic nuns who like to take care of me a lot."  At the end of her e-mail, Sylvia sent her wishes to the family.  "Big kiss from me to you all.  I miss you, and I can't wait to see you all very soon."

Reem Returns to the UK

After spending a few days in Amman and Aqaba, Reem returned to the UK on Friday, April 16, 2004.  As mentioned in the previous issue, Amal, Ziad, and Reem left Amman to Aqaba on Sunday, April 11, and returned on Tuesday, April 13.  "Had a really good time in Aqaba with my parents," said Reem in an e-mail to the Editor in Chief.  "It was very relaxing.  We spent most of the time by the beach, and we all paid the price because we're all peeling now."

Samer to Double-Major in Economics and Electrical Engineering

Samer will still be graduating next month but will spend one more semester at Tulane

Samer, who has been studying Electrical Engineering at Tulane University in New Orleans for the past four days, recently decided that he will also be majoring in Economics.  Samer will still graduate on May 22 but will spend next semester in Tulane completing the requirements for his Economics major.
