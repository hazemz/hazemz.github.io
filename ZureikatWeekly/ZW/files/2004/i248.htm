Omar Okay after Car Accident

Omar drove into an electric pole in Amman on Tuesday, February 17, 2004.  He was not hurt.  Omar was alone in the car at the time of the accident.

 

Omar Starts his Second Semester

Two weeks after Zureikat school students in Jordan started the second semester of this academic year, Omar, who is currently studying at the Jordan University, started his second semester on Sunday, February 22, 2004.

Mona Leaves to Oman for Book Fair

Mona will be returning to Sweden on March 6

Mona left to Muscat, Oman on Sunday, February 22, 2004, to participate in the Muscat International Book Fair.  She is expected to return to Sweden on Saturday, March 6.

Tarek Returns from Baghdad

Tarek returned from a successful, eight-day business trip to Iraq on Wednesday, February 18, 2004.  This was Tarek's second trip to Iraq in less than eight months.

Dalia Uses the ZW in One of her School Projects...

Deena Gets 99% on a Project on 'Songhai'

 

Dalia used one of The Zureikat Weekly's issues and several pictures from the newsletter in a school project on family ties.  Dalia will be presenting her project in class next week.  On the other hand, Deena got 99% on a project she did on an African empire in the region of Songhai.  The project included information about social, cultural, and political aspects of life in this ancient empire.
