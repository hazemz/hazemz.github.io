Sylvia Returns to Vienna

After a historic eight-day visit to Jordan -- her first in many years -- Sylvia returned to Vienna on Friday, January 23, 2004.  Following is a list of Sylvia's activities in Jordan in the last few days of her visit:

 

Lunch at Amal and Ziad's
Monday, January 19, 2004

Sylvia and a number of Zureikats had lunch at Amal and Ziad's on Monday, January 19, (one day before Ayman's departure to the US).

 

Dinner at Hassan and Dian's
Tuesday, January 20, 2004

Sylvia had fajitas for dinner at Hassan and Dian's the next day -- Tuesday, January 20.

 

Kan Zaman and Umm Al-Amad
Wednesday, January 21, 2004

Sylvia, Abla, Abla, and Omar spent Wednesday, January 21, at Kan Zaman Restaurant (near the airport) and in Umm Al-Amad.

 

Lunch at Marwan and Abla's
Thursday, January 22, 2004
