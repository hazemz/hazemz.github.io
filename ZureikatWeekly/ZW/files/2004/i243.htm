Sylvia in Amman

Zureikats in Amman host lunches and dinners celebrating the visit

Sylvia visits Anjara, the Jordan Valley, and the Dead Sea

On her first visit to Jordan in many years, Sylvia arrived in Amman on Wednesday, January 14, 2004.  Amer, Leanne, Tarek, and Bana received Sylvia at the airport on Wednesday evening before the family all gathered for dinner at Abu Harran's.  Following is a list of Sylvia's activities in Jordan so far:

 

Dinner at Abu Harran's upon arrival
Wednesday, January 14, 2004

 

 

 

 

 

 

Upon Sylvia's arrival on Wednesday, January 14, the family gathered at Abu Harran's for a dinner prepared by Reema.

 

Lunch at Harran and Reema's
Thursday, January 15, 2004

On the first full day of her visit, Thursday, January 15, Sylvia had lunch at Harran and Reema's along with a few Zureikats.

 

Dinner with Hassan and Dian at Peking Restaurant
Thursday, January 15, 2004

 

 

 

 

Above 4 pictures: Hassan paying the bill

On Thursday night, Hassan and Dian invited Sylvia, Amer, Leanne, Bashar, Tarek, and Bana to Peking Restaurant in Amman for dinner.

 

Trip to Anjara and the Jordan Valley with Harran
Friday, January 16, 2004

On Friday, January 16, Sylvia and Abla Z. accompanied Harran throughout the day as he visited Anjara and the Jordan Valley.

 

Dinner at Tarek's
Friday, January 16, 2004

 

Friday night, Tarek hosted the Zureikat cousins at his apartment for dinner.  The menu included Tarek's infamous Champagne Pasta.

 

Lunch at Marwan and Abla's
Saturday, January 17, 2004

 

'Magloobet Beitenjan' was the main dish at Marwan and Abla's where Zureikats gathered for lunch on Saturday, January 17.

 

Trip to the Dead Sea
Sunday, January 18, 2004

 

 

 

 

Left: Sylvia 'opening with the cup' for Abla.

Sylvia, Abla, Abla, and Hazem spent Sunday, January 18, -- the first day in Abla and Abla's mid-year break -- at the Dead Sea.  The warm weather allowed Sylvia to have her first ever swim in January.

 

Dinner at Abla and Basil's
Sunday, January 18, 2004

After a long day at the Dead Sea, Sylvia and a number of Zureikat cousins had an early dinner at Abla and Basil.  The main dish there was 'Koosa and Warag Dawali'.

Sylvia is expected to leave Amman on Friday, January 23.

Special thanks to Leanne for taking pictures at the Peking Restaurant dinner and at Tarek's dinner

Mona Undergoes Cataract

Mona is the third Zureikat to undergo the operation in less than two months

Mona underwent a cataract operation on her right eye on Tuesday, January 13, 2004, exactly one week after Marwan had undergone the same operation on his right eye and less than two months after Harran had also undergone the same operation on his right eye.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Mona a speedy recovery and all the best.

Amal, Ziad, Ayman Visit Dubai

   

Amal, Ziad, and Ayman returned from Dubai (in the United Arab Emirates) on Saturday, January 17, 2004.  They left to the UAE on Monday, January 12, on a four-day business trip for Ziad.

Hassan and Dian Return from Syria

 

Hassan and Dian returned from Syria on Sunday, January 18, 2004.  They left Amman on Saturday, January 17.

Zureikats Celebrate Amer's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Amer's birthday on Monday, January 12, 2004.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Amer a very happy birthday and all the best.

  • Harran, Reema, Marwan, Abla, Abla, Sylvia, Tarek, and Hazem attended a choir in Amman on Thursday, January 15, 2004.

  • Leanne extracted a wisdom tooth at her father's clinic on Tuesday, January 13, 2004.

  • Representing Engicon, Tarek attended a three-day conference in Amman between Sunday and Tuesday, January 11 and 13, 2004.  The conference, entitled 'Outreach 2004', discussed issues related to rebuilding Iraq.
