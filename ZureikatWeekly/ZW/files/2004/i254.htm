Karadshehs in New York for 3 Days

       

Hala, Naief, Dalia, Deena, and Salem who are living in the US this year will be leaving to New York City on Sunday, April 4, 2004, to spend three days there.  They are expected to return to Maryland on Wednesday, April 7.

Zureikats Celebrate Ziad's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Ziad's birthday on Thursday, April 1, 2004.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Ziad a very happy birthday and all the best.

  • Abu Harran, Marwan, Abla, Dana, and Tarek spend Friday, April 2, 2004, in Umm Al-Amad.

 

  • Since a significant percentage of the family is currently in the US, it is probably worth mentioning that the US and Canada switched to daylight saving time on Sunday, April 5, 2004, nine days after Jordan switched to its daylight saving time.  Amman is now 7 hours ahead of Washington, DC and Boston and 8 hours ahead of Chicago, New Orleans, Louisiana and St. Paul, Minnesota.
