Harran and Reema Return Home

Abla Arrives in the US

Hazem joins Abla in DC for weeklong vacation

Abla attended a conference in Williamsburg, Virginia last week

Top: Harran, Reema, and Samer in front of Broussard's Restaurant in the French Quarter in New Orleans, Louisiana on Sunday, March 7.
Bottom: Abla, Dana, Ammar, and Hazem have dinner at the Karadshehs' (in Rockville, Maryland, a suburb of Washington, DC) on Sunday, March 14.

 

 

 

After visiting Chicago and attending a conference in New Orleans, Harran and Reema left the US on Wednesday, March 10, 2004, and arrived in Amman on Thursday, March 11.  On the other hand, Abla N. arrived in Washington, DC on Tuesday, March 9, on the start of an eleven-day visit to the US.  Abla left Washington to Williamsburg, Virginia on Wednesday, March 10, to attend a conference on gifted education.  She returned to Washington on Saturday, March 13, and is expected to stay there till Saturday, March 20.  Hazem, who is currently studying in St. Paul, Minnesota, also arrived in Washington on Saturday to spend his nine-day spring break there.  Celebrating this family reunion, Abla, Dana, Ammar, and Hazem had dinner at the Karadshehs' on Sunday, March 14.

Sylvia Sends her Regards from Cameroon

Mona, in her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief: "In her first telephone call from Cameroon today [March 13], Sylvia said that she is already established there.  She had to take the bus for ten hours away from the capital and two hours more with a taxi.  The clinic and the hospital where she works are on a mountaintop with very beautiful nature.  Lots of patients with Malaria and Aids diseases.  She is fine and gives her regards to all the Zureikats."

Zureikats Celebrate Yacoub's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Yacoub Z.'s birthday on Monday, March 8, 2004.  On this occasion, Yacoub cut one of his birthday cakes at Abu Harran's on Monday evening.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Yacoub a very happy birthday and all the best.

  • The Karadshehs spent Saturday, March 13, 2004, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, where they attended a flower show.

  • Hala attended a talk by Jordanian Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher, who is currently visiting Washington, DC.
