Second Semester Begins

   

   

After a three-week mid-year break, Zureikat school students, along with Abla and Abla, returned to school on Saturday, February 7, 2004, for the start of the second semester of the 2003-2004 academic year.  What should be mentioned is that Dalia, Deena, and Salem (who are spending this academic year in the US) started their second semester on Wednesday, January 28, as we mentioned in our previous issue.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Zureikat students, teachers, and administrators around the world all the best in this semester.

Sylvia Graduates Wednesday;

Hennings to Host Reception in Vienna

Mona and the family will be leaving to Austria on Tuesday

After completing the requirements for her degree in December, Dr. Sylvia Henning will be graduating from the University of Vienna on Wednesday, February 11, 2004.  The graduation ceremony will be followed by a reception hosted by the Hennings at the Palais Pallavicini in downtown Vienna.  Mona and the family are expected to leave to Vienna on Tuesday, February 10, and return to Sweden on Saturday, February 14.

Marwan and Abla Return from Egypt

 

 

After spending the Eid Al-Adha holiday on a Nile cruise between Luxor and Aswan (in Egypt), Marwan and Abla returned home on Wednesday, February 4, 2004.

Zureikats Celebrate Omar's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Omar's 21st birthday on Saturday, February 7, 2004.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Omar a very happy birthday and all the best.
