Sharaihas in New Orleans for Samer's Graduation

Hazem will also attend the graduation, arrives Thursday

Amal and Ziad arrived in New Orleans, Louisiana on Friday, May 14, 2004, where Ziad will be attending a conference and where the family will be attending Samer's graduation from Tulane University on Saturday, May 22.  Reem left London to New York on Saturday, May 15, and arrived in New Orleans on Sunday, May 16.  Ayman also arrived in New Orleans on Sunday, May 16, while Talal is expected to arrive on Wednesday, May 19.  Hazem will also be attending Samer's graduation.  He will be arriving in New Orleans on Thursday, May 20.  What should be mentioned is that both Samer and Ayman finished their final exams for this semester earlier this week.

Hazem Leaves St. Paul

Hazem will be traveling to New York, Washington, and New Orleans before leaving to Amman next Sunday

Hazem left St. Paul, Minnesota to New York City on Sunday, May 16, 2004.  He will be spending a night in New York and two nights in Washington, DC before leaving to New Orleans on Thursday, May 20, to attend Samer's graduation.  Hazem will be leaving to Amman on Sunday, May 23, and arriving the next day.  What should be mentioned is that Hazem finished his final exams on Friday, May 7.

Harran and Reema Return from Beirut

 

Harran and Reema left to Beirut on Wednesday, May 12, 2004, and returned to Amman on Saturday, May 15.  During the visit, Harran attended the Middle East Medical Assembly Conference (MEMAC), which included a session on cardiology.

  • Amal and Ziad spend 7 hours in Chicago and a night in Boston before arriving in New Orleans

    Waitress at Chicago airport to Amal after she ordered a beer: "Can I see your ID?"

    Although they left Amman on Thursday, May 13, and were expected to arrive in New Orleans the same day, Amal and Ziad had to spend seven hours at the airport in Chicago waiting for their flight, which was delayed because of a tornado watch.  When they learned that the flight was canceled, Amal and Ziad were flown around midnight to Boston, where they spent the remainder of the night.  They left to New Orleans the next morning.

    As Amal and Ziad were waiting at a restaurant in Chicago's O'Hare airport, Amal ordered a beer from one of the waitresses..  The waitress then asked her: "Can I see your ID?...You look like you're younger than twenty-one."  The waitress actually thought that Amal was Ziad's daughter!
