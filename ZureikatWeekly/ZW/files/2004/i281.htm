281st Issue - October 10, 2004

With great sadness and deep sorrow, The Zureikat Weekly and the Zureikat family in Jordan and abroad mourn the passing away of

Yacoub Soboh Zureikat
Abu Harran
(January 1, 1913 - October 9, 2004)

The Editor in Chief would also like to extend his personal condolences to all members of the Zureikat family at home and abroad.

May God rest his soul

Abu Harran Laid to Rest

One day after his death at his home in Amman, Abu Harran was laid to rest in a funeral service held on Sunday, October 10, 2004.

Funeral Houses at Harran's and Marwan's

Two funeral houses were opened for the family to receive condolences.  For three days (Sunday, Monday, and Tuesday), men will pay their condolences at Harran's house and women at Marwan's house.

Church Service on Tuesday

A church service will be held on Tuesday to mark three days since the passing away of Abu Harran.
