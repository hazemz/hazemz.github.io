Kareem Returns Home

Kareem, who is currently studying at Bristol University in the UK, arrived in Amman on Saturday, March 20, 2004, to spend his Easter vacation with the family.  Kareem is expected to return to the UK mid-April.

The Zureikat travel season to the US continues...

Abla Returns Home, Hazem to Minnesota

 

After attending a conference in Williamsburg, Virginia and spending a week in Washington, DC, Abla N. left to Amman on Saturday, March 20, 2004.  She arrived the next day.  On the other hand, Hazem, who spent his spring break in Washington, returned to St. Paul, Minnesota on Sunday, March 21.

 

Tarek Arrives in the US

Tarek left Amman to Denver on Tuesday for business meetings. He arrived in Washington on Saturday.

Tarek left to Denver, Colorado on Tuesday, March 16, 2004, after an unexpected decision by Marwan to send him to the US for some business meetings. After spending three days in Denver, Tarek arrived in Washington, DC on Saturday, March 20, a few hours after Abla's departure and the night before Hazem's departure.  Tarek is expected to leave Washington to New York on Monday, March 22, and head back to Amman on Tuesday.

 

Reema E-mails the ZW after Returning from the US

A few days after she and Harran returned from Chicago and New Orleans, Reema sent an e-mail to the Editor in Chief.  In her e-mail, Reema said, "It seems the Zureikats love traveling to the States; maybe it's the big steaks there!...It was great seeing Amer and Leanne, and I must say Chicago is a beautiful city to visit.  Having Amer and Leanne there makes it even the most beautiful...ha ha ha.  We had a lovely time with Samer in New Orleans, drinking fabulous wine chosen by Harran.  I think we interrupted his exams, but he managed so well the next day."  In her e-mail, Reema also congratulated Ammar on being appointed in the Beta Gamma Sigma honor society.  "We all extend our heartiest congratulations to Ammar," she said.  "We're happy for you and proud and wish you and Dana all the success."

Ayman in Florida for Spring Break

Ayman left to Florida on Saturday, March 20, 2004, to spend his spring break there.  He is expected to return to Boston on Saturday, March 27.

Yacoub Sits for Jubilee School Entrance Exam

Yacoub S. sat for the entrance examination for the Jubilee School (whose director is Abla N.) on Saturday, March 20, 2004.  The exam covered three subject areas: Math, Logic, and Arabic.  If Yacoub passes the test and an interview that follows, he will enroll in the school next academic year (2004-2005).  What should be mentioned is that Yacoub is currently in the 8th grade at Amman National School.  The Jubilee School hosts gifted students from around Jordan from the 9th up to the 12th grade.
