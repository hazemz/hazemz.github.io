Engagement Party!

Zureikats, Shomalis, Dababnehs, and friends celebrated Bashar and Rana's engagement in a party held at the Amman International Hotel on Monday, July 12, 2004.  The party, which came almost three months after Bashar and Rana's tulbeh, coincided with the unprecedented Shomali reunion in Amman.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Bashar and Rana on their engagement, wishing them all the best.

Ayman Returns Home

After completing his third year at Tufts University in Massachusetts and working and doing summer courses for the first part of the summer, Ayman returned to Amman on Saturday, July 17, 2004.

Harran and Reema Host the Shomalis for Dinner

Harran and Reema hosted the visiting Shomalis as well as the Zureikats for dinner on Friday, July 16, 2004.

 

Shomalis Start Leaving Amman after Historic Reunion

Basil's visiting brothers and sisters and their families have started leaving Amman after a historic family reunion.  More are expected to leave this week.  Umm Basil, however, will be remaining in Amman for some time.

Hazem Visits Beirut; Kareem Returns from Sharm

 

Hazem left to Beirut, Lebanon on Thursday, July 15, 2004.  He is expected to return home on Monday, July 19.  Meanwhile, Kareem left to Sharm El-Sheikh, Egypt on Thursday, July 15, and returned to Amman on Sunday, July 18.

Zureikats Celebrate Bashar's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Bashar's birthday on Sunday, July 18, 2004.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Bashar a very happy birthday and all the best.

  • Farid Al-Hourani, husband of Muntaha Zureikat (Abu Harran's niece), passed away on Wednesday, July 14, 2004.  Several Zureikats attended Al-Hourani's funeral service in Rabbah on Thursday, July 15.  Others paid condolences on Friday and Saturday.

  • Alia's friend from England, Dalia, arrived in Amman on Saturday, July 10, 2004.  She will be returning to the UK on Tuesday, July 20.
