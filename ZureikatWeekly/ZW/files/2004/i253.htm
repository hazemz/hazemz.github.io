Dana in Amman for 2 Weeks

Ammar leaves to Mexico for MBA project

 

 

Dana arrived in Amman on Saturday, March 27, 2004, on her second visit to Jordan after moving to Washington, DC in October 2002.  Celebrating Dana's visit, a number of family members and friends gathered at Marwan and Abla's for dinner Saturday night.  On the other hand, Ammar, who will be graduating from Georgetown University with an MBA next month, left to Mexico on Friday, March 26, to work on a final project with his classmates.  Both Dana and Ammar are expected to return to Washington in two weeks.

 

Tarek Returns from the US

Tarek returned home on Wednesday, March 24, 2004, after a weeklong trip to the US which took him to Denver, Colorado, Washington, DC, and New York City.

Dag Attends CeBIT Fair in Hanover

Dag left to Hanover, Germany on Monday, March 22, 2004, to attend the CeBIT international trade fair.  According to last week's e-mail from Mona, Dag is supposed to have returned to Sweden "by the end of the week".

Yacoub Grabs 2nd Place in ANS Talent Show

Yacoub played a Bach piece on the flute in the school's annual competition

Yacoub S., who is currently in the 8th grade at Amman National School, won second place in the school's annual talent show for his performance of a classical Bach piece on the flute.  What should be mentioned is that Yacoub has been taking flute lessons for a year now.  The second prize was a 10JD cash reward.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Yacoub on his award, wishing him all the best.

After spending his spring break in Florida

Ayman returned to Boston on Saturday, March 27, 2004, after spending his weeklong spring break in Florida.
