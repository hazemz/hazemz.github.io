Brunch at Engicon's cafeteria

Alia Lane, an accessible entrance to the recently-opened headquarters of Engicon, was inaugurated by Alia on Friday, June 11, 2004.  On this occasion, family and friends had brunch at the new headquarters on Friday.  During the event, both Marwan and Alia gave speeches.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Alia said, "I would just like to take this opportunity to thank Ammo Marwan for the lovely opening of 'Alia Lane'.  It was truly beautiful!!  He never ceases to amaze me.  Ammo, everything you do is special just like you.  The fact that you related Engicon to my perseverance is a great honor to me.  I, myself, believe that such perseverance would not have been possible had it not been for the continuous love, support and acceptance of my family.  Walking down Alia Lane, I could not help but remember all the times that you were there for me, sharing with me lovely moments of laughter, singing and dancing, let alone carrying me on your shoulders and being my one and only valentine (so far!!!!!!)"  Guests who attended the event include Mr. Ghaleb and Mrs. Laila Zureikat and Mr. George and Mrs. Sue Kawar.

Dana's First Investigative Report Airs on Al-Hurra

Dana's first lengthy investigative report aired on Al-Hurra TV on Wednesday, June 9, 2004.  The report discussed issues related to reconstruction in Iraq with a special focus on preserving Iraq's museums and artifacts.  The airing of the report comes a few months after Dana was appointed as a senior producer and an investigative reporter at Al-Hurra.  During these months, Dana conducted interviews for her report in New York, Boston, and Washington, DC.

Reem to Work at St. George's Next Year

"i'm also moving houses,  I'm going to be living...in Wimbledon"

After a job interview on Monday, June 7, 2004, Reem was accepted to work at St. George's next year.  In an e-mail she sent to the Editor in Chief, Reem said, "I [will be] working at St George's doing Accident and Emergency (ER) for the first six months and then internal medicine for the following six months.  I'm also moving houses.  I'm going to be living with two other girls from my med school in Wimbledon."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to congratulate Reem on being accepted at St. George's, wishing her all the best.

Samer Starts Summer Job with NASA

A few weeks after graduating from Tulane University, Samer began his summer job with the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) at one of their offices near New Orleans, Louisiana.  What should be mentioned is that Samer will be studying at Tulane University for one more semester to complete a second major in economics.
