Amal and Ziad Return from Short Trip to Beirut

 

Amal and Ziad left to Beirut on Friday, July 2, 2004, where Ziad attended a conference.  They returned to Amman on Sunday, July 4.

Tarek Returns from London

Tarek returned home on Thursday, July 1, 2004, after spending one week in London.  Tarek resumed work on Saturday, July 3.

Abla: The new terrace has been raided

Shomali Reunion at Abla and Basil's

 

"The new terrace has been raided," was how Abla Z. described the situation at her house as she spoke to the Editor in Chief on the phone trying to get her voice through the noisy background.  Abla and Basil are currently hosting what seems to be one of the largest Shomali reunions.  Basil's brothers Khaled and Majdi and their sister Wijdan have already arrived with their spouses and children: Khaled from Germany, and Majdi and Wijdan from Palestine.  Tarek, Basil's brother who lives in Flynt, Michigan, is expected to arrive with his daughter Rachel on Tuesday, July 6, while Basil's other sister, Rose, will be the last to join the reunion on Thursday, July 8.  All the guests will be staying at Abla and Basil's throughout their visit.

Zureikats Celebrate 4 Occasions

Harran's birthday on July 1; Amal's birthday on July 3; Abla and Basil's anniversary on July 3; and Marwan and Abla's anniversary on July 4

Zureikats celebrated Harran's birthday on Thursday, July 1, 2004, Amal's birthday on Saturday, July 3, Abla Z. and Basil's wedding anniversary also on Saturday, July 3, and Marwan and Abla N.'s wedding anniversary on Sunday, July 4.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Harran and Amal a very happy birthday and Abla and Basil and Marwan and Abla very happy anniversaries and all the best.
