Samer Graduates from Tulane University

The ceremony was held in New Orleans on Saturday, May 22

Samer graduated from Tulane University with a BSc in Electrical Engineering.  The graduation ceremony was held at the Louisiana Superdome in New Orleans on Saturday, May 22, 2004, and was attended by Amal, Ziad, Reem, Ayman, Talal, and Hazem, as well as two other guests.  What should be mentioned is that Samer will be studying at Tulane for one more semester to obtain another major in Economics.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Samer on his graduation, wishing him all the best.

Amal, Ziad, Hazem Leave to Amman

Reem and Talal return to London, Ayman to Boston

Amal, Ziad, and Hazem left the US back to Amman on Sunday, May 23, 2004.  On the same day, Reem and Talal returned to London, and Ayman returned to Boston.  What should be mentioned is that Samer will probably be working in the US during the summer, while Ayman will be working and taking summer courses at Tufts until mid July.

Dana, Ammar, and Hazem had dinner at the Karadshehs on Tuesday, May 18.

Before leaving to New Orleans, Hazem visited his cousin Bana Bashour in New York and later visited Dana and Ammar and the Karadshehs in Washington, DC between May 17 and 19, 2004.

Hala Graduates from the Hubert Humphrey Program

 

Hala officially graduated from the Hubert H. Humphrey program last week.  A special ceremony was held in West Virginia on Sunday, May 16, 2004, for all of this year's 113 Humphrey fellows.  During the ceremony, the fellows received certificates from the US State Department.  Hala returned to Maryland from West Virginia on Monday, May 17.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Hala on her graduation, wishing her all the best.  What should be mentioned is that the Karadshehs will be returning to Amman on August 20.

Alia and Reema in New York

Alia is part of a five-member Jordanian delegation participating in a UN convention

 

Alia and Reema left to New York on Sunday, May 23, 2004, on a two-week trip, where Alia will be part of the five-member Jordanian delegation participating in the United Nations International Convention on the Protection and Promotion of the Rights and Dignity of Persons With Disabilities.  Reema is accompanying Alia as a carer.

Mona Elected on Consul Club Board

Mona has been elected on the board of the Consul Club in Sweden.  In a presentation she delivered in front of the assembly last week, Mona talked about Jordan and all the challenges that the country is facing.  The presentation was very much appreciated among the audience.

Sylvia to Work in Hospital in Western Sweden

Sylvia left to Vienna on Thursday for 10 days

A party will be held for Sylvia on June 4

Sylvia will be working at a hospital in western Sweden in a town called Lidkoping starting next August.  On the other hand, Sylvia left to Vienna on Thursday, May 20, 2004, and will return to Sweden in ten days.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Mona said that a "big party" will be held for Sylvia in Djursholm on June 4.  "Family and friends are invited," Mona said.

Helene Returns Home after Vacation in Scotland

Helene left to Scotland on Thursday, May 20, 2004, for a three-day vacation.  She returned to Sweden on Sunday, May 23.

Dana and Ammar Visit Chicago

 

Dana and Ammar left to Chicago on Friday, May 21, 2004.  Dana returned to DC on Sunday, May 23, while Ammar is expected to return on Monday, May 24.  Dana and Ammar visited Amer and Leanne at their apartment during the trip.
