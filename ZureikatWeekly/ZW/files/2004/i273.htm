Family Mourns Basil's Nephew

Hani Majdi Shomali died in a bus accident in Syria

Basil's nephew, Hani Majdi Shomali, died on Thursday, August 12, 2004, in a bus accident in Syria.  The 20-year-old was on his way to visit relatives in the city of Hims when the bus he was on crashed killing more than twenty people.  Mr. Majdi Shomali, his wife, and his sister Rose, as well as other Shomalis, arrived in Amman on Friday and Saturday, August 13 and 14.  Meanwhile, Basil and Omar left to Hims early Friday morning to arrange transporting Hani's body.  The body arrived in Amman on Saturday and was transported to Palestine early Sunday.  What should be mentioned is that Hani was in Amman last month, where he attended Bashar and Rana's engagement party.  The Zureikat Weekly and the Zureikat family would like to extend their deepest condolences to Basil, Mr. Majdi, and the Shomali family on the passing away of Hani.  May God rest his soul.
