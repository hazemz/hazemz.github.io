After spending two weeks in New York

Reema and Alia Return Home

 

After spending two weeks in New York City where they attended the United Nations International Convention on the Protection and Promotion of the Rights and Dignity of Persons With Disabilities, Reema and Alia returned to Amman on Saturday, June 5, 2004.

Celebrating her graduation and her 30th birthday

Hennings Host Party for Sylvia

A few months after Sylvia's graduation from Vienna and a few weeks after her return from Cameroon, the Hennings hosted a party for Sylvia at their home in Djursholm, Sweden on Friday, June 4, 2004, which also coincided with Sylvia's 30th birthday.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Sylvia on her graduation and wish her a very happy birthday and all the best.

Zureikats Sit for their Finals

     

   

Zureikat school students are currently sitting for the second semester final exams.  Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z., who study at the Modern Schools, and Yacoub S., who studies at the Amman National School, are all expected to finish their finals by the end of the week.  Dalia, Deena, and Salem, who spent this academic year in the US, are also sitting for their final exams.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief, Dalia said, "This week, my finals begin at school, and I'm going to finish school by the end of this week, on Friday June 11, while Deena and Salem are going to finish school on June 15."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Zureikat school students all around the world the best of luck in their finals.

Amer and Leanne Move to New Apartment 

Amer and Leanne moved to a new, two-bedroom apartment this weekend.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Amer and Leanne on their new apartment.

 

Zureikats Celebrate Amer and Leanne's First Wedding Anniversary

A picture from the wedding reception at Harran and Reema's

Zureikats celebrated Amer and Leanne's first wedding anniversary on Sunday, June 6, 2004.  Both currently living in Chicago, Amer and Leanne got married on Friday, June 6, 2003, at a church in Sweifieh, in Amman.  The afternoon church service was followed by a reception at Harran and Reema's and a dinner party at the Grand Hyatt in Amman.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Amer and Leanne a very happy anniversary and all the best.

Engicon Inaugurates New Headquarters

Family celebration to be held at 11:30 Friday

Engicon's staff standing in front of the new headquarters on Saturday

Engicon celebrated the inauguration of its new headquarters in Jabal Al-Lweibdeh in a luncheon on Saturday, June 5, 2004.  Marwan, Chairman of the company's board, began the luncheon with some opening remarks.  During the event, Engicon employees who served at the company for more than ten years, as well as those who helped establish the new headquarters, were distinguished.

Marwan would like to take this opportunity to invite members of The Zureikat Weekly to a family brunch celebrating the inauguration of Engicon's headquarters.  The event will be held at the new headquarters on Friday, June 11, at 11:30am.
