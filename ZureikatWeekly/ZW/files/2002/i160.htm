160th Issue - June 9, 2002

Tarek Leaves Edinburgh to Start Summer Work

After spending five years in Edinburgh, Tarek left his apartment in the Scottish capital to start his last summer at the Southwestern Company before he returns home next October.  Tarek arrived in Bristol on Saturday, June 8, after driving there all the way from Edinburgh.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief last week, Tarek said that there will be a weeklong "sales school" in Bristol starting Monday, June 10, 2002.  He said that after this sales school, he will be going to Coventry to start his summer work there.

The 2001-2002 school year draws to a close

Hazem Prepares for Tawjihi Exams

Zureikats in school doing their finals, start their holiday next week

       

     

The 2001-2002 academic year, which began in Jordan on Sunday, September 2, 2001, is drawing to a close.  Zureikats in different schools in Jordan are currently doing their final exams.  They will be starting their summer holiday next week.  On the other hand, Hazem is still preparing for the official Tawjihi exam (the Jordanian General Certificate of Secondary Education), which will start on Sunday, June 16, and continue until Saturday, July 6.  Hazem has been on a holiday since Tuesday, May 14, 2002.

 

Omar Starts his Summer Holiday

Omar, who is currently studying at the Princess Sumayya University for Technology, finished his final exams on Thursday, June 6, 2002 and is now on a three-month summer holiday.

Dana Returns from Cairo after Short Visit

Dana attended this year's Awards Dinner at the Adham Center

Dana will be leaving to Cairo on another visit next week

Dana returned from Cairo on Thursday, June 6, after attending this year's Awards Dinner at the Adham Center for Television Journalism - the American University in Cairo (AUC).  Dana had left Amman on Tuesday, June 4, 2002.  She will be leaving to Cairo on another visit next week.

Editor in Chief Undergoes "Surgical Extraction of Impacted Wisdom Tooth"

The Editor in Chief underwent a "surgical extraction of an impacted wisdom tooth" by "Professor Amal" on Saturday, June 8, 2002.  He is almost fully recovered now.

Dana's 37th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 37th story aired on JTV on Sunday, June 9, 2002.  This week's story was about King Abdullah II's achievements throughout the past three years.  June 9, 2002 marked the third anniversary of King Abdullah II's accession to the throne.

Zureikats Celebrate Sylvia's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Sylvia's birthday on Tuesday, June 4, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Sylvia a very happy birthday and all the best.
