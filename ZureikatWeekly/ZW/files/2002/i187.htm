187th Issue - December 22, 2002

Zureikats Abroad Return Home

No changes for this year...Christmas Eve at Harran's; Christmas Day at Marwan's

   

   

Besides Dana who will be spending Christmas in the US due to work reasons, all Zureikats studying and working abroad have returned home.  The first to return was Reem on December 15, then Talal on December 17, then Hazem on December 20, and finally Amer, Samer, and Ayman on Saturday, December 21, 2002.  What should be mentioned is that there will be no changes in the Christmas celebrations for this year.  Zureikats will be gathering at Harran's on Tuesday night (Christmas Eve) and at Marwan's on Wednesday (Christmas Day).

 

Family Gathers at Marwan's after Hazem's Return

 

 

Celebrating Hazem's return, the family gathered at Marwan's house for lunch on Friday, December 20, 2002.

Dana Promoted at VOA

Dana, who is currently working at the Voice of America in Washington DC, has been promoted.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief last week, Dana said, "I applied for a high position (promotion) at VOA.  I had to submit 15 pages.  I got the highest scores amongst the candidates who applied.  Soon, I will be taking that post, which
will not be announced to the ZW until it actually goes through."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Dana on her promotion and wish her all the best.

Ziad Returns from Cairo after Attending Conference

Ziad returned from Cairo on Monday, December 16.  He had earlier left to Cairo on Friday, December 13, 2002, to attend a conference there.

Kareem Leaves to Beirut...Returns before Christmas Eve

Kareem, along with seven of his classmates, left to Beirut on Friday, December 20.  He is expected to return home on Tuesday, December 24, 2002.

Ziad Responds to Samer's Latest E-mail

Ziad sent the following e-mail to the Editor in Chief in response to Samer's e-mail regarding appointing Abla N. as a ZW Photographer:

Dear Editor in Chief of The Zureikat Weekly,

It is almost Christmas and the New Year is on the horizon, so, in this holy month, one can only say truthful things which are relevant to the mind and heart.  If the pictures did not show any difference between Harran and Hassan, it is because they are brothers and they look like each other, and they have the same genetic make up as Umm Harran wa Abu Harran, and the same goes for Abla and Hala.  So, long live our photographer and we wish her a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year, with more similar pictures of the family.

Ziad Sharaiha
