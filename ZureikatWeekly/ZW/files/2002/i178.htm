178th Issue - October 20, 2002

Bashar Promoted to Assistant Manager

Bashar, who is currently working at Citibank, has been promoted from Grade 13 to Assistant Manager.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Bashar on his promotion and wish him all the best.  What should be mentioned is that Bashar is the only one in the history of the bank who got promoted three times.  Bashar was last promoted in April from Grade 11 to Grade 13.

Dana Leaves to Washington Soon

The date of Dana's departure is still unknown

Dana, who received a job offer from the Voice of America (VOA) in Washington DC, will be leaving to Washington soon.  The date of Dana's departure is still unknown.

Reema and Alia Return Home

 

After extending their stay in London for one more night, Reema and Alia returned home on Saturday, October 19, 2002.  Alia has been undergoing a week of intensive physiotherapy in Hemel Hempstead in the north of London.

Amal and Ziad Leave to Geneva and London

 

Amal and Ziad left to Geneva, Switzerland on Friday, October 18, 2002.  After their visit to Geneva, Amal and Ziad are expected to visit London.

Mona Visits France with Some Friends, Leaves to Vienna with Gero Next Week

In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief which she sent before releasing the 177th issue, Mona said, "Next week, I will be in France (the Riviera) with some friends, and, the week after, Gero and I will be in Vienna to see Sylvia and visit Grinzing, the wine area of Vienna."

Hala in Cairo...Returns Monday

Hala left to Cairo on Thursday, October 17, to attend a conference there.  She is expected to return home on Monday, October 21, 2002.

As a new employee at JCE,

Tarek Makes a Two-Day Visit to Aqaba

Tarek sends us details of his first day at the company

"I feel I made the right decision leaving the UK"

Tarek congratulates Bashar on his promotion to Assistant Manager

Tarek, who joined the Jordanian Consulting Engineer Company on Saturday, October 12, made a two-day business trip to Aqaba on Monday and Tuesday, October 14 and 15, 2002.  On the other hand, Tarek, in a message he sent to the Editor in Chief, gave us some details of his first day at the company.  In hi s message, Tarek said, "Saturday was my first day on the job, and my father, who I now call (Abu Tarek), even at home, invited all the employees for lunch to welcome me.  I even had to say a few words, and I talked about my first day in Dara Building, when Majdi was my boss, and how he made me clean about 4 dunums of empty just to make sure that I know I won't be getting any special treatment; they laughed.  Anyway, I spend all my time attending meetings where people use vocabulary that I don't understand, and my challenge is to try and combine these words into a sentence that will hopefully make sense.  Anyways, I am thoroughly enjoying it here; I feel I made the right decision leaving the UK.  I also want to take this opportunity to congratulate Bashar on his promotion at Citibank, to Assistant Manager.  He has really made us proud because he is the only one in the history of the bank to get promoted three times."

Najla, Ahmad, and Khaled Bashour Visit Amman

Marwan and Abla hosted a Mansaf in Umm Al-Amad on Friday

Dr. Najla Nusseir Bashour, Abla N.'s sister from Lebanon, along with her two sons, Ahmad and Khaled, visited Amman on Thursday, October 17, and returned to Beirut on Sunday, October 20.  During their four-day visit, Dr. Najla, Ahmad, and Khaled, along with Dr. Yousef Nusseir, Abla N.'s brother, and his family, had Mansaf for lunch in Umm Al-Amad on Friday, October 18.  They also had lunch at Marwan's on Sunday, October 20, 2002.  What should be mentioned is that Bashar was also at the lunch in Umm Al-Amad on Friday.

Zureikats Celebrate Dalia's Birthday

Dalia will be having a birthday party after Hala's return from Cairo

Zureikats celebrated Dalia's birthday on Saturday, October 19, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Dalia a very happy birthday and all the best.  What should be mentioned is that Dalia will be having her birthday party after her mother's return from Cairo.  In her weekly e-mail to the Editor in Chief which she sent before releasing the 177th issue, Hala said, " Since I will not be here on the 19th for Dalia's birthday, I am intending to do her birthday on the 26th."
