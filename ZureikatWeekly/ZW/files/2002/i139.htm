Amer, Alia, Samer, Talal Leave Town

Samer returned to the US and Talal returned to England on Monday, January 7.  Amer and Alia also left to London on Friday, January 11, 2002.  From there, Amer will be returning to Dublin, and Alia will be staying in London for two weeks to attend a reunion for the Royal Holloway graduates of the last four years.  She will also be undergoing some medical checkups there.

Jordan Celebrates Snow

Harran visits Anjara Sunday to take pictures

A low depression affected Jordan last week causing snowfall in many areas.  This long-awaited snowfall started on Monday (January 7) afternoon and continued till Tuesday (January 8) morning.  Harran is expected to visit Anjara on Sunday, January 13, 2002, to take pictures of the snow.  These pictures will be available in the next issue of The Zureikat Weekly.

Dana Returns to Reporting on JTV

Dana's 14th story aired last Friday...more stories to come every Friday

*** Watch Dana's stories LIVE from The Zureikat Weekly.  Just go to our (JordanLIVE) section and watch JTV every Friday at 10 pm Jordan Local Time - 8 pm GMT.  ***

Dana is now back to reporting on JTV's News at Ten.  You can see her every Friday in the (Editor's Choice) section of the news.  Dana's 14th story aired on Friday, January 11, 2002.  This story was about Hashem Restaurant, a 46 year-old restaurant in downtown Amman, which has become part of the Jordanian culture.

Tarek Returns to Edinburgh from the US

Tarek arrived in Edinburgh, Scotland last week after spending a few days in Nashville, Tennessee.  He will be staying in Edinburgh for the next two months and returning to Amman in March.
