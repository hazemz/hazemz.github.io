July 30, 2002: The 5th anniversary of the passing away of Umm Harran

Hazem's Tawjihi Result: 96.0%

The Jordanian General Certificate for Secondary Education (Tawjihi) results in Jordan were announced on Wednesday, July 31, 2002.  Hazem's result was 96.0%, the highest Zureikat Tawjihi score ever!  Celebrating Hazem's result, the family gathered at Marwan's house on Wednesday evening.  Hazem is the third among Zureikat grandchildren to follow the Tawjihi system after Bashar, Tarek, and Omar.

Dana's 46th Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 46th story aired on JTV on Friday, August 2, 2002.  This week's story was about the Ministry of Water and Irrigation's current and future plans to solve the water shortage problem in Jordan.

Zureikats Celebrate Dana's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Dana's birthday on Thursday, August 1, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Dana a very happy birthday and all the best.
