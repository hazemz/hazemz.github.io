155th Issue - May 5, 2002

 

 

Zureikats in Jordan Celebrate Easter

The family gathered in Umm Al-Amad for Easter lunch

 

 

 

Left: Kareem sending the Zureikat children off to egg hunt \ Right: The Zureikat children egg hunting

Zureikats in Jordan celebrated Easter on Sunday, May 5, 2002.  On this occasion, the family gathered at Marwan's farm in Umm Al-Amad for the annual Zureikat Easter lunch.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish you all a very happy Easter and all the best.

Mona Returns to Sweden after Extending Stay in Jordan

Mona was interviewed on Yis'ed Sabahak last Friday

After extending her stay in Jordan for two more days, Mona returned to Sweden on Saturday, May 4.  On Friday, May 3, 2002, Mona was interviewed by JTV's Friday morning show, Yis'ed Sabahak.  Mona, Jordan's honorary consul in Sweden and owner of Dar Al-Mona, spoke about the Swedish Children's Literature Exhibition in which she participated during her visit to Jordan.  She also spoke about her overall experience in Sweden and in Dar Al-Mona.

Amal Leaves to the US

Reem joins her mother in Houston for a few days

 

Amal left to Houston, Texas on Tuesday, April 30, to attend an advanced course on implants and a wedding there.  Amal was joined by Reem, who arrived to Houston from London also on Tuesday.  Reem is expected to return to London on Monday, May 6, while Amal is expected to return to Amman on Wednesday, May 8.  What should be mentioned is that Reem finished her final exams on Friday, April 26.  She will be starting her final year in medicine on May 7, 2002.

Tarek in Hamburg to Represent JCE in Conference

Tarek arrived in Hamburg, Germany on Sunday, May 5, to represent the Jordanian Consulting Engineer Company (JCE) in a conference there.  He is expected to return to Edinburgh on Tuesday, May 7, 2002.

Bana's Choir Sings at the Orthodox Club

The "Voices Sing for Palestine" choir, with Bana as one of its members, participated in a solidarity week held at the Orthodox Club in Amman last week.  A number of Zureikats attended the event.  What should be mentioned is that the choir's first performance was held at the St. John Baptist Church - De La Salle College in Amman more than two weeks ago.

Sabine, Nadine, Yacoub Participate in Fashion Show

   

Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z. participated in a fashion show on Saturday, May 4, 2002.  All proceeds from the fashion show, which was held at the Sheraton Hotel in Amman, have gone to the Palestinian Intifada.

Dana's 33rd Story Airs on JTV

Dana's 33rd story aired on JTV on Friday, May 3, 2002.  This week's story was about the Annual "Education for All" Week which was held in Amman this year.
