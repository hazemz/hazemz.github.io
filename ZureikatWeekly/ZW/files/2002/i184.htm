184th Issue - December 1, 2002

Amal Leaves Hospital

Amal spent one week at the hospital after fracturing three transverse processes

After spending one week at the hospital, Amal returned home on Friday, November 29.  Amal had earlier fractured three transverse processes on Friday, November 22, 2002, and was hospitalized on the same day.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Amal a very speedy recovery and all the best.

Samer, Ayman, and Hazem Travel on Thanksgiving

Samer visited Atlanta; Ayman visited Philadelphia; Hazem visited NYC

   

Samer, Ayman, and Hazem, who are currently studying in the US, traveled on Thanksgiving break between Wednesday, November 27, and Sunday, December 1, 2002.  Samer visited his friend in Atlanta, Georgia, Ayman visited his friend in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, and Hazem visited his cousin, Bana Maan Bashour, in New York, New York.  They all returned to their universities on Sunday, December 1, 2002.
