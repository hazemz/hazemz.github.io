163rd Issue - June 30, 2002

Hala Hosts the "Younger Generation" for Lunch

Hala and Naief hosted the Zureikat "younger generation" for lunch on Friday, June 28, 2002.  The lunch was attended by Abu Harran, Amer, Leanne, Alia, Kareem, Samer, Ayman, Hazem, Bana, Omar, and Yacoub S., along with Abla Z. and Basil.

The journey continues...

7 Down...3 More to Go

Hazem, who is currently sitting for his Tawjihi exams, has already completed seven exams, which include Arabic, Social Studies, English, Computer, Biology, and Math (two papers).  Hazem has three exams left, Physics, on July 1, Geology, on July 3, and Chemistry, on Saturday, July 6, 2002.

"The Most Beautiful European City"

Harran and Reema return from Prague

 

Harran and Reema returned from Prague, the Czech Republic on Friday, June 28.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief a day after her return Reema said, "Prague is the most beautiful European city.  It's an open museum under the sky.  We had a wonderful time.  In fact, Harran didn't want to come back!"  In a the phone call, Reema also said, "We visited Carlo Vivari, which is a health farm, and we recommend to all members of the Zureikat family to visit that spot."  Harran and Reema had left to Prague on Friday, June 21, 2002.

Ziad Returns from Beirut

Ziad returned from Beirut on Saturday, June 29, after a three-day business trip to the Lebanese capital.  Ziad had left to Beirut on Thursday, June 27, 2002.

2 More Stories for Dana Air on JTV

Dana's 38th story aired on JTV on Wednesday, June 26.  Her 39th story also aired on Friday, June 28, 2002.  This week's stories concerned the latest developments in the Palestinian territories.
