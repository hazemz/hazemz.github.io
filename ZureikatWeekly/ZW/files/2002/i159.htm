159th Issue - June 2, 2002

Reem Returns Home for Brief Visit

Reem, who is currently studying at the St. George's Hospital Medical School in London, returned home on Saturday, June 1, for a brief visit.  She is expected to fly back to London on Wednesday, June 5, 2002.

Tarek Traveling throughout the UK to Prepare for "Selling Season"

Tarek, who is currently working in England for the Nashville-based Southwestern Company, sent an e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly last week.  In his e-mail, Tarek said, "These next weeks before the selling season starts are going to be hectic.  I have to meet everyone who is going to be selling in my group, which means, in the next 5 days, I will be in Bradford, Leeds, Liverpool, London, maybe Southampton, and Belfast in Northern Ireland.  I have someone working with me from Dublin, but I think I need a visa to go there... I'm looking forward to have a good selling season and leave my company on a good note so that I can go back to Jordan and start a business with my dad."  In his e-mail, Tarek also congratulated Amer and Bana on their new jobs.  "I would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Amer and Bana for their new jobs and wish them the best of luck," he said.  In reference to what we mentioned in last week's issue about Tarek calling himself "the busiest Zureikat", Tarek said, "I would also like to deny reports in your paper that I refer to myself as the busiest Zuriekat.  I might be the most good looking, but I think it's unfair to call myself the busiest as I don't know how everyone else works."

Harran and Reema Return from Beirut

 

Harran and Reema returned home on Saturday, June 1, after making a two-day visit to Beirut.  During their visit, Harran attended a conference, and both Harran and Reema visited the fields on Kifrayya in the mountains of Lebanon.  Harran and Reema had left to Beirut early Friday, May 31, 2002.

Abla and Basil Get a New (Nissan Sunny)

 

Abla Z. and Basil got a new car, a Nissan Sunny.  The new car arrived at the Shomalis' residence on Saturday, June 1, 2002.  In a phone call with the Editor in Chief, Abla Z. said, "I drove the car yesterday (Saturday, June 1), and Bana was sitting beside me.  Each time we passed beside a Nissan Sunny taxi, Bana said, "Garaba!"."  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate the Shomalis on their new car...mabrouk!

Mona and Amer Congratulate Hazem on Latest Decision to Keep The Zureikat Weekly Running

 

In two separate e-mails to the Editor in Chief, Mona and Amer congratulated Hazem on is latest decision to keep The Zureikat Weekly running during his college years in Minnesota.  In her e-mail, Mona said, "Thank you very much for the latest Zureikat Weekly which carried with it the GREAT NEWS that the newsletter will continue under your command.  This shows how fantastic you are, and all the Zureikats must feel very proud of you."  In his e-mail, Amer said, "I was delighted to hear about the unanimous decision to keep the 'Zureikat Weekly' running.  Special thank you to our 'Editor in Chief'.  I would however like to draw your attention to the issue of selecting the Weekly's proposed 'Correspondents'.  I believe that we are at an important juncture in the Weekly's existence and that careful deliberation and wisdom are needed to secure that the weekly continues to function in a CONSISTENT manner."

Dana Interviews Executive Director of UNICEF

Dana interviewed the Executive Director of UNICEF last week.  The interview was aired on Jordan Television on Friday, May 31, 2002.

Zureikats Celebrate 3 Occasions

     

Zureikats celebrated three occasions last week, Helene's birthday on Thursday, May 30, Hassan and Dian's wedding anniversary also on Thursday, May 30, and Sabine's birthday on Friday, May 31, 2002.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Helene and Sabine very happy birthdays and Hassan and Dian a very happy anniversary and all the best.
