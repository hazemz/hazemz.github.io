A New Generation

Jad Ammar Daoud is born

 

 

 

Jad Ammar Daoud, Dana and Ammar's first child, Marwan and Abla's first grandchild, and Abu Harran and Umm Harran's first great grandchild, was born at the George Washington University Hospital in Washington, DC on Friday, January 14, 2005.  His weight at birth was 7.6 pounds.  Dana and Jad are both expected to return home from the hospital on Monday, January 17.

 

A Letter from Marwan to Jad

Dear Jiddo Jad,

On the plane, on my way to see you after you were born, I had this awkward feeling.  I was happy for your arrival, but I also felt older.  The image on the plane lavatory mirror showed much to reinforce this feeling.

On this day, Sunday, the second day after you were born and after I had the chance to carry you and hug you, things looked remarkably different.  Abla, your Teta, looked 20 years younger and as charming as ever.  I was later out with Hazem to buy his new laptop and witnessed the same looks on his face that he used to have 15 years ago when we bought his first computer.

Thank you Jad for the life you brought to our living.

Your Grandfather

 

Family in Amman Celebrates Birth of Jad

Marwan and Hazem leave to DC

On Friday evening, January 14, the family in Amman gathered at Marwan and Abla's awaiting the birth of Jad.  As the news came in from the US, the family cheered and celebrated with champagne.  Later that night (early Saturday), Marwan and Hazem left to Washington, DC to see the new baby.

 

Jad, Dana, Abla in Amman Next Month

Jad, Dana, and Abla are expected to arrive in Amman next month, where Dana and Jad will be spending a month or two before moving to Dubai, where Ammar will be starting his new job.
