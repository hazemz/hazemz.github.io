127th Issue

Sunday, October 21, 2001

Amer Returns Home

Zureikats gather at Harran's

 

Amer returned home on Thursday, October 18.  Celebrating his return, the family gathered at Harran's for lunch on Friday, October 19, 2001.  Dr. Amer is expected to stay in Amman till next February.

After spending 12 days in Amman

Tarek Leaves to Edinburgh

This time, Tarek is on a business trip!

After spending twelve days in Amman, Tarek, who is currently working with the Southwestern Company, left to Edinburgh on a three-week business trip on Saturday, October 20.  He is expected to return home on Saturday, November 10, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Dalia's Birthday

The family choir, led by Marwan, celebrating Dalia's birthday at Harran's

Zureikats celebrated Dalia's birthday on Friday, October 19, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Dalia a very happy birthday and all the best.

  • Yacoub S. got a certificate from the British Council after completing the "Flyers" English course.  Congratulations Yacoub!
