JCE Classified as Class 1A

Marwan: This, in effect, makes JCE one of the largest consulting firms in Jordan

Marwan sent the following e-mail to the Editor in Chief:

To the Editor in Chief
The Zureikat Weekly
Amman-Jordan

Dear Sir,

On Tuesday, November12, the Central Tenders Board of Jordan committee responsible for the classification of local consulting engineering offices visited the offices of JCE.  This committee comprise members from the Ministry of Public Works, the Central Tenders Board, the Jordan Association of Engineers, the Contractors Association, and several others, including representatives of the private sector.  The committee toured the offices and attended a presentation made by JCE engineers.

We were informed today (November 19) that the committee approved to classify JCE as a CLASS 1A Consultants  This, in effect, makes JCE one of the largest consulting firms in Jordan.  (Only two other firms enjoy the same ranking.)  I will appreciate the inclusion of this info on the next issue of The Zureikat Weekly.

May I take this opportunity to extend my regards.

Regards,
Marwan

The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Marwan on this accomplishment and wish him, and the Jordanian Consulting Engineer Company (JCE), all the best in the future.
