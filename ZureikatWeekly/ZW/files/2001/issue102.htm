Zureikats Celebrate Easter

Zureikats around the world celebrated Easter on Sunday, April 15, 2001.  Celebrating this occasion, the family gathered at Marwan's farm in Umm Al-Amad in a lunch hosted by Hassan on Easter Sunday.

 

Happy Easter from The Zureikat Weekly

The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish all Zureikats around the world a very happy Easter and the very best in the future.

Reem Makes a Surprising Return Home

Reem made a surprising return home on Friday, April 13, 2001, to spend Easter with her family.  The date of Reem's return to London is still unknown.

Tarek Returns to Edinburgh...Tuesday

After spending almost one month in Amman, Tarek is expected to return to Edinburgh, Scotland on Tuesday, April 17, 2001.
