Talal Graduates from the United World Colleges of the Adriatic

The ceremony was attended by Amal, Ziad, Samer, and Ayman

Talal graduated from the United World Colleges (UWC) of the Adriatic in Italy on Saturday, May 26.  The graduation ceremony was attended by Amal, Ziad, Samer, and Ayman.  The Sharaihas are expected to return home on Sunday, May 27, 2001.  More details about the graduation in the next issue.

Amer Returns Home in 4 Weeks

Some of you might say, "Why is Hazem mentioning that someone is returning home after FOUR weeks?   He still has a lot of time."  Well, here's the reason: an e-mail from Amer to the Editor in Chief:

Hazem, please e-mail me the questionnaire again.  I am writing you as part of my
"once per year in-touch with The Zureikat-Weekly campaign" and also because
I have failed to make any of your headlines in the past 12 months!!  (This is
justifiably so as so many of the cousins have now grabbed center stage and I
have been ridiculed to the level of a poor old doctor with nothing
interesting to add!!)  Fear not, my dear cousin, I have some interesting
news for you: I shall be on my way back home in 4 weeks!!  (Please try to add
your own 'editor-in-chief' touches to it to make it more interesting!). Give
my regards to all!!!

Editor's Note: Sorry Amer...but graduations always "grab center stage".
