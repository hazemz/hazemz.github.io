Sunday, June 24, 2001

Amer and Talal Return Home...Monday

 

Amer and Talal will be returning home on Monday, June 25.  In an e-mail sent to The Zureikat Weekly on Tuesday, June 19, 2001, Talal said, "Well, I am in Sardegna, one of the best beach resorts in the world, just having fun and relaxing.  After graduation, I traveled with my roommate through Bolonia, Florence, Napoli, Piza, Rome, Sienna, and then to Sardegna.  It was really enjoying and informing, since Italy has one of the most amazing histories."  In his e-mail, Talal also congratulated Alia and Tarek on completing the requirements for their BA degrees.

Abla, Dana, and Hazem Attend Bashour's Wedding

Lama Bashour and her husband Ameen Hannoun

Abla N., Dana, and Hazem returned to Amman on Saturday, June 23, 2001, after a three-day visit to Lebanon in which they attended the wedding party of Lama Bashour, Abla N.'s niece, and Ameen Hannoun.  Dr. Yousef Nusseir, Abla N.'s brother, and his family, who also attended the wedding, returned home on the same day.

Dana's Plane Receives False Bomb Threat Before Arriving in Beirut

From Al-Ra'i Daily Newspaper - Saturday, June 23, 2001

The plane Dana was on while traveling from Amman to Beirut on Thursday, June 21, 2001, a Royal Jordanian plane, received a false bomb threat 20 minutes before arriving in Beirut.  The plane made an emergency landing and was evacuated, and passengers had to go to the airport by foot.

Zureikats Celebrate Editor in Chief's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Hazem's birthday on Saturday, June 23, 2001.  Hazem's birthday was celebrated after 12 midnight during Lama Bashour's wedding party in Lebanon.

Marwan Returns Tuesday

Sources told The Zureikat Weekly that Marwan, who left to Canada last week, will be returning home on Tuesday, June 26, 2001.
