125th Issue

Sunday, October 7, 2001

Mona Returns to Sweden after a Weeklong Visit to Jordan

The family gathered at Abla Z.'s for dinner before Mona's departure

Zureikats having dinner at Abla Z.'s on the night of Mona's departure

Mona returned to Sweden on Thursday, October 4, after attending a conference in Amman.  She had arrived in Jordan on Thursday, September 27, 2001.  On the night of Mona's departure, the family gathered at Abla Z.'s for dinner.

Tarek Returns Home...Monday

Sources confirmed to The Zureikat Weekly that Tarek will be arriving in Amman on Monday, October 8, 2001.  Tarek, who is returning from his fourth summer in the US, got his BA degree last summer from the University of Edinburgh and is currently working with the US-based Southwestern Company.

Amal and Ziad in Amsterdam

Ziad to visit London...Amal to visit Istanbul to attend a conference

 

Amal and Ziad left to Amsterdam on Friday, October 5.  According to an e-mail Amal sent to The Zureikat Weekly, she and Ziad will be staying in Amsterdam till October 10.  After that, Ziad will make a one-day visit to London and return home, while she, Amal, will fly to Istanbul, Turkey on October 11 to attend a meeting on implants and return home on Monday, October 15, 2001.

Zureikats celebrate Bana's birthday

Bana Starts Last Semester at UJ

Zureikats celebrated Bana's birthday on Wednesday, October 3.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Bana a very happy birthday and all the best.  Bana, who is majoring in Economics, will be starting her last semester at the University of Jordan on Sunday, October 7, 2001.  She will complete all the requirements for her BA degree by the end of next January.

Zureikats Celebrate Hala and Naief's Anniversary

 

Zureikats celebrated Hala and Naief's wedding anniversary on Monday, October 1, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Hala and Naief a very happy anniversary and all the best.

  • Hala and Naief's house is undergoing renovation.

 

  • Rabi' Bashour, Abla N.'s nephew from Lebanon, arrived in Jordan on Friday, October 5.  His sister and brother, Lama and Ahmad Bashour, will also be arriving in Jordan on Thursday, October 11, 2001.
