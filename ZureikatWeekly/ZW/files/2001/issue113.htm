Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

113th Issue

Sunday, July 8, 2001

Abla and Yacoub Leave to Sweden

  

Abla Z. and Yacoub S. left to Sweden on Saturday, July 7, 2001, to visit Mona and her family.  Sources told The Zureikat Weekly that Abla and Yacoub will be staying in Sweden for about two weeks.

Reema and Kareem Leave to London...Harran Leaves July 11

Alia will graduate with honors on Thursday, July 12

  

Reema and Kareem left to London on Saturday, July 7, to attend Alia's graduation.  Harran will also be attending Alia's graduation.  He is expected to leave Amman on Wednesday, July 11.  Alia will be graduating with honors from the Royal Holloway with a BA degree in European Studies on Thursday, July 12.  After the graduation, Harran is expected to return home on Sunday, July 15, Reema and Kareem will stay in London until July 30 to attend Tawfiq Jack Ayoub, Reema's nephew's, wedding on July 28 in Cardiff, while Alia will be starting two weeks of exercises on July 16, 2001.  Amer, who is currently in Amman, will be going straight to Dublin where he will be working and preparing for the USMLE.

Hala, Dalia, Deena, and Salem will accompany Naief for one month

Naief Receives Dad Scholarship from German Government

Naief will be performing researches in Gottingen University for three months

Naief, along with two other professors in Jordan, received a Dad Scholarship from the German Government.  Naief will be performing researches at Gottingen University for three months.  He, along with Hala, Dalia, Deena, and Salem, will be leaving to Germany on Saturday, July 21, 2001.  Hala and the children will spend one month with Naief and return back home by the end of August.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Naief on his scholarship and wish him all the best.  What should be mentioned is that this is the third time Naief receives this scholarship.  He had previously received it in 1984 and 1994.

Hala Attends Conference in Seville...Returns Sunday

Hala left to Seville, Spain, on Thursday, July 5, to attend a conference related to archives.  She is expected to return home on Sunday, July 8, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Harran and Amal's Birthdays and Two Wedding Anniversaries

The family gathered at Harran's on July 1 and at Amal's on July 3

 

   

Zureikats celebrated Harran's birthday on Sunday, July 1 (as we mentioned in the 112th issue), and Amal's birthday on Tuesday, July 3.  Celebrating these occasions, the family gathered for dinner at Harran's on Sunday and at Amal's on Tuesday.  This week, Zureikats also celebrated two more occasions, Abla Z. and Basil's wedding anniversary on Tuesday, July 3, and Marwan and Abla N.'s wedding anniversary on Wednesday, July 4, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Harran and Amal happy birthdays and Abla Z., Basil, Marwan, and Abla N. happy anniversaries.

Abla Recovers after Undergoing Root Canal

Abla Z. underwent root canal at Amal's clinic on Monday, July 2, 2001.  She has now fully recovered.

Dana Returns after 2-Day Working Visit to Egypt

Dana returned home on Tuesday, July 3, 2001, after a two-day working visit in Cairo, Egypt.

The IB results are in:

Ayman: 40...Talal: 42

After getting their IB results, Ayman and Talal defeat Marwan and Omar 4-3 in a pool game making the weekend just perfect

 

Ayman and Talal got their IB results this weekend.  Ayman got 40/42, and Talal got 42/42.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate both Ayman and Talal for their outstanding results and wish them all the best.  Shortly after getting their results, Ayman and Talal defeated Marwan and Omar 4-3 in a pool game attended by the Editor in Chief.
