Sunday, August 12, 2001

Zureikats Celebrate

Hassan's 50th Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Hassan's 50th birthday on Thursday, August 9, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Hassan a very happy birthday and all the best.

Zureikats Celebrate Amal and Ziad's 25th Wedding Anniversary

 

Zureikats celebrated Amal and Ziad's 25th wedding anniversary on Saturday, August 11, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Amal and Ziad a happy silver jubilee and all the best.

Talal in Japan...Returns Friday

Samer returns from Cyprus...Ayman returns from Aqaba

Talal left to Japan on Sunday, August 5, on a fully paid trip entitled "What will I do for spaceship earth?".  He is expected to return home on Friday, August 17, 2001.  Samer returned from a vacation in Cyprus last week.  Ayman also returned from a vacation in Aqaba, Jordan with his friends from Amman National School.

Marwan, Abla, and Hazem Return from Lebanon

Lunch in Brummana

Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem returned home on Friday, August 10, 2001, after a four-day vacation in Lebanon.

Zureikats Celebrate Samer's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Samer's birthday on Monday, August 6, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Samer a very happy birthday and all the best.
