Tarek Graduates from the University of Edinburgh

 

 

Tarek graduated from the University of Edinburgh in Scotland on Wednesday, December 5, 2001.  Tarek now holds a BComm Degree with Honors in Business Studies and Accounting.  The graduation ceremony, which was held at the McEwan Hall in the university, was attended by Marwan, Abla, Dana, and Hazem.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Tarek on his graduation and wish him all the best in the future.
