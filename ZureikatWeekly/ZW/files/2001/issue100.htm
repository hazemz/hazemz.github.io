Zureikats Celebrate Bashar's BA

The family gathered at Abla Z.'s on Thursday

 

 

Abla Z. and Basil hosted the Zureikats and Shomalis on a dinner on Thursday, March 29, 2001, celebrating Bashar's BA degree, which he completed all the requirements for early this year.

Amer, Alia, and Tarek have lunch at Amal's

Amer Returns to Ireland...Sunday

 

After spending 10 days in Amman, Amer will be returning to Ireland on Sunday, April 1.  Two days before his planned departure, Amer, along with Alia and Tarek who also returned home more than a week ago, had lunch at Amal's on Friday, March 30, 2001. 

Amal and Ziad in Egypt...Monday

 

Amal and Ziad will be leaving to Egypt on Monday, April 2.  Amal will return on Thursday, April 5, and Ziad will return on Friday, April 6, 2001.

A few weeks after Talal was accepted at St. George's

Ayman Receives Acceptance from Tufts University

A few weeks after Talal was accepted at St. George's Medical School in London, Ayman, who will be graduating next summer, received an acceptance last week from Tufts University in Boston, Massachusetts.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Ayman and wish him and Talal all the best in the future.

 Zureikats Celebrate Two Birthdays

 

Zureikats celebrated Naief's birthday on March 27 and Ziad's birthday on April 1, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish both Naief and Ziad happy birthdays and all the best.
