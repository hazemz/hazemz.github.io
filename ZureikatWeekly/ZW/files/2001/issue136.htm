In a dinner party held at Dr. Yousef Nusseir's house a few days after Tarek's return

Zureikats Celebrate Tarek's Graduation

Tarek returned home on Sunday, December 16, after graduating from the University of Edinburgh early this month.  Celebrating his graduation, Zureikats gathered at Dr. Yousef Nusseir, Abla N.'s brother's, house for dinner on Friday, December 21, 2001.

Sharaihas Return...Family Gather at Amal's

Reem, Samer, Ayman, and Talal returned home from the UK and the US last week.  Celebrating their return, Zureikats gathered at Amal's for dinner on Thursday, December 20, 2001.

A few days after Hassan's return from a brief visit to the Lebanese capital

Hassan and Family Leave to Beirut on Christmas Day

Sources: They will not be attending the Christmas lunch at Marwan's

Hassan, Dian, Sabine, Nadine, and Yacoub Z. will be leaving to Beirut on Christmas morning to attend a wedding there.  The wedding will also be attended by "people from Aleppo", as Hassan put it.  Hassan had returned a few days ago from a brief visit to Beirut.  What should be mentioned is that Hassan and his family will not be attending the Christmas lunch at Marwan's next Tuesday.
