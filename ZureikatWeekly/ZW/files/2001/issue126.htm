126th Issue

Sunday, October 14, 2001

Tarek Returns Home

Zureikats gather at Marwan's

 

Tarek, who completed all the requirements for his BA degree last June, returned from the US on Monday, October 8, 2001.  Celebrating his return, the family gathered at Marwan's for dinner on the same day.

Amer Returns Home...Thursday

Amer will be returning home on Thursday, October 18, 2001.  Sources told The Zureikat Weekly that he will be staying in Amman till after January.

Istanbul Trip Canceled...Amal Returns Home

Ziad visits Reem and Talal in London, returns

 

After a five-day visit to Amsterdam with Ziad, Amal returned home on Wednesday, October 10, after the trip she was supposed to make to Istanbul, Turkey was canceled.  Ziad, on the other hand, visited Reem and Talal in London and returned home on Friday, October 12, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Dag's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Dag's birthday on Monday, October 8, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Dag a very happy birthday and all the best.

  • Marwan and the family spent two days in Umm Al-Amad.

  • Rabi' Bashour, Abla N.'s nephew from Lebanon, returned to Beirut.  His sister, Lama, arrived in Amman the next day.
