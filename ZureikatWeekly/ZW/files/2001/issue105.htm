Samer...The First to Return Home for the Summer

Samer, the first to return home for the summer holiday, returned to Amman on Saturday, May 12, 2001.  Sources said that Samer will be staying in Amman till August.  On the other hand, a week before his return, Samer sent the following e-mail to The Zureikat Weekly:

Dear Zureikats, and especially Abu Harran (hafi thaho allah wa ra3ah),

I would like to take this opportunity and wish Abu Harran a healthy recovery and wish him all the best
for the next many years.  I would appreciate it if this message was relayed verbally, and I will call Abu Harran (allah edeemo we khalleeh) as soon as possible.

Akhookom, wa ibnakom al-moghtareb,
Samer Z. Sharaiha

Marwan Makes a Short Visit to Denver, Colorado

Marwan returned to Amman on Saturday, May 5, after making a short business trip to Denver, Colorado.  Marwan had left for the US on Wednesday, May 2, 2001.

Alia Leaves...Reema Leaves...Reema Returns

  

Alia returned to London to sit for her finals on Sunday, April 29.  A week later, Reema followed Alia to London and returned back to Amman.

Zureikats Celebrate Gero's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Gero's 65th birthday on Friday, May 11, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Gero a very happy birthday and all the best.

Hassan Returns from a Brief Visit to Syria
