118th Issue

Sunday, August 19, 2001

Tawjihi Begins Sunday

Hazem will be starting his last school year (Tawjihi) on Sunday, August 19, 2001.  He is expected to graduate from Amman National School in July 2002.

Sharaiha news:

Amal, Reem, Samer, and Ayman Prepare to Leave to the US

Talal returns from Japan, leaves to the UK end of next month

Amal, Reem, Samer, and Ayman will be leaving to the US on Thursday, August 23.  Samer will be starting his second year at Tulane, and Ayman will be starting his first year at Tufts.  On the other hand, Talal returned from Japan on Thursday, August 16, 2001, after a ten-day trip entitled "What Will I Do for Spaceship Earth?".  He is expected to leave to the UK to start his first year at St. George's at the end of September.
