116th Issue

Sunday, August 5, 2001

Hassan Returns Home After Undergoing Operation

Hassan was hospitalized on Wednesday, August 1, after suffering from chest pains.  On the same day, he underwent stint placement.  The operation, which was performed at Jordan Hospital, was successful.  Hassan returned back home on Thursday, August 2, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Hassan a speedy recovery and all the best.

Alia, Reema, Kareem Return Home

Harran opening a bottle of champagne celebrating Alia's graduation

Alia, Reema, and Kareem returned home on Wednesday, August 1.  Celebrating their return and Alia's graduation, the family gathered at Harran's on the same day.  During their stay in London, Reema and Kareem attended Alia's graduation, which Harran also attended, on July 12.  Reema, Alia, and Kareem also attended the wedding of Tawfiq Jack Ayoub, Reema's nephew, in Cardiff on Saturday, July 28, 2001.

Marwan, Abla, and Hazem Visit Lebanon...Monday

     

Marwan, Abla N., and Hazem will be leaving to Lebanon on Monday, August 6.  They are expected to return back home on Friday, August 10, 2001.

Zureikats Celebrate Dana's Birthday

Dana cutting her cake

Zureikats celebrated Dana's birthday on Wednesday, August 1, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Dana a very happy birthday and all the best.
