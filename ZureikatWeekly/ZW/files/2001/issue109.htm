Reema in New York to Attend Preparatory Meeting

Reema is representing the NGO's in the Jordanian delegation

Reema left to New York on Sunday, June 10, to attend the Third Substantive Session for the Preparatory Committee for the Special Session of the General Assembly on Children.  Reema is representing the NGO's in the Jordanian delegation.  She is expected to return home on Saturday, June 16, 2001.

 Alia Moves to Flat on Friday...Graduates on July 12

After completing all her requirements at Royal Holloway, Alia will be moving to a flat in London on Friday, June 15.  She will be graduating on Thursday, July 12, 2001.  The graduation will be attended by Harran, Reema, Amer, Kareem, and other family members.

Zureikats Celebrate Sylvia's Birthday

Zureikats celebrated Sylvia's birthday on Monday, June 4, 2001.  The Zureikat Weekly would like to take this opportunity to wish Sylvia a very happy birthday and all the best.
