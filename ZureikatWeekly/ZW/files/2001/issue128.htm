Ayman and Talal Settle Down in University

Ayman: Ishtagna lal manasef

 

Ayman and Talal sent their first e-mails to The Zureikat Weekly from university.  In his e-mail, Ayman, who is now studying at Tufts University in Massachusetts, said, "Well I think I settled in the States.  It's cool around here, although it's gonna get freezing around here soon.  Everything is fine, in terms of school, studying, and all other stuff...ishtagna lal manasef min idain Anti Abla N.  Food is not that good here."  Talal, who is now studying at St. George's Medical School in London, said in his e-mail, "I just settled down in the university.  Well I have actually settled in quite quickly and commenced my university life as a medic.  The first two weeks were sort of introductory, just to get to know each other and all that, and then the real hard work starts.  Yet I am enjoying myself as much as possible and balancing it with my studies.  The weather is as shitty as usual, always raining and all that.  My sister is doing fine, she actually helped me a lot at the beginning."  In his e-mail, Talal also sent a message to Marwan, "I joined the snooker club here, so when I come back, we will beat him again in billiardo."

Reema Holds First Event for FFSPD

The Family and Friends Society of Persons with Disabilities, with Reema as its President, held its first event on Saturday, October 27, 2001.  The breakfast, which was held at the Blue Fig Restaurant in Amman, was attended by a number of Zureikats.  During the event, Reema spoke briefly about the society and its goals and thanked all those who supported it throughout the years.
