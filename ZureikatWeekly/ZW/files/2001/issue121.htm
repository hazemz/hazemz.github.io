Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

121st Issue

Sunday, September 9, 2001

Harran and Reema Attend a Party in Sweden Celebrating Gero's 65th Birthday

 

Harran and Reema returned home after a visit to Sweden in which Harran attended a conference held by the European Association for Cardiology.  Harran and Reema also attended a party celebrating Gero's 65th birthday (which was on May 11, 2001).  During the party, Harran, the guest of honor, gave a speech.  Helene, Dag, and some of Gero's friends also gave speeches.  Reema's "speech", on the other hand, was a song in Arabic!  After the party, which Reema described as "superbly and accurately organized", Harran and Reema joined Dag, Sylvia, Helene, and their friends for a drink at a night club.

Reem, Samer, and Ayman are Back in their Dorms, Amal is Back in Amman

Talal leaves to London by the end of this month

Amal returned home after a two week trip to the US in which she accompanied Ayman to Boston on his first year at Tufts University.  After her return, Amal told The Zureikat Weekly that Samer and Ayman are now settled and back in their dorms.  She also said that Reem left to London (from the US) on Thursday, August 30, 2001.  Talal, who will be studying at St. George's in London, is expected to leave Amman by the end of this month.

Ziad in Cairo...Returns Monday or Tuesday

Ziad left to Cairo on Friday, September 7, to attend a conference there.  Sources told The Zureikat Weekly that Ziad will be returning home on Monday, September 10, or Tuesday, September 11, 2001.

Alia, Bana, Omar, and Talal in Aqaba

The summer holiday is over for some Zureikats, but it's not for others! Alia, Bana, and Bana's Norwegian friend left to Aqaba on Friday, September 7.  They will be returning to Amman on Sunday, September 9.  Omar, Talal, and their friend also left to Aqaba on Saturday, September 8.  They will be returning to Amman on Monday, September 10, 2001.
