123rd Issue

Sunday, September 23, 2001

Mona to Visit Amman on Wednesday

Mona will be arriving in Amman on Wednesday, September 26.  This is Mona's second visit this year.  Her first was on April 23, 2001.  During her visit, Mona will be attending a conference for Arab women who live abroad.

Reema and Alia Attend Conference in Beirut

 

Reema and Alia returned home on Friday, September 21, after attending the First International Conference on Disability Rehabilitation and Inclusion in Beirut, Lebanon.  Reema and Alia had left Amman on Tuesday, September 18, 2001.

Hassan Returns Home...Also from Beirut

Hassan returned home on Saturday, September 22, 2001, after also visiting Beirut, Lebanon.

  • Amal was interviewed live on ART (Arab Radio & Television) on Saturday, September 15, 2001.

  • Please note that Jordan will be switching to its winter time (GMT+2) on Friday, September 28, 2001.
