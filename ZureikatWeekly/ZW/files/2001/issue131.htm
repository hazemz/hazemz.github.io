Editor in Chief: Hazem Zureikat

131st Issue

Sunday, November 18, 2001

Announcements from

the Editor in Chief

First of all, the Editor in Chief wishes to voice an objection against the lack of cooperation from all Zureikats who failed to provide The Zureikat Weekly with any news.  The Zureikat Weekly is your magazine and can only continue with your support and cooperation.  The Editor in Chief kindly asks all Zureikats, even those who are in Jordan, to send in their latest news.

The Editor in Chief of The Zureikat Weekly would also like to make the following announcements:

  • On Saturday, November 17, 2001, all the 2001 files of The Zureikat Weekly were moved from the servers of Angelfire - Lycos to Fleebo.com.

  • On Saturday, November 17, 2001, the new address (www.zw.777i.net) was registered at 777i.net.

  • The 132nd issue, to be released on Sunday, November 25, 2001, will be the last with the 2001 layout.  The 133rd issue, to be released on Sunday, December 2, 2001, will contain The Zureikat Weekly's Christmas layout.

  • The 138th issue, to be released on Sunday, January 6, 2002, will contain an all-new layout for The Zureikat Weekly, just like every new year.  The 2002 layout will be designed to allow minimum loading time.

Abla Returns from US after Successful Visit

Abla N. returned home on Wednesday, November 14, 2001, after a successful working visit to the United States.  During her visit, Abla attended a convention in Cincinnati Ohio and visited schools and institutions in Chicago, Illinois and Washington D.C.

Tarek Returns...Sunday

Tarek, who is currently in Edinburgh, is expected to return home on Sunday, November 18, 2001.  During his two-week stay in Amman, Tarek will be holding the second Awards Banquet for the Jordanian students who participated in his organization last summer.

  • Naief: New kitchen to be completed by Monday.  We'll be moving back on Thursday or Friday.

Dalia at school.

Date: November 13, 2001
